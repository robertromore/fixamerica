# Congressional Reform Implementation

## Overview

This document outlines implementation considerations for congressional reform constitutional provisions.

---

## Enabling Legislation

### Fair Representation Act

- Multi-member districts for House
- Ranked-choice or proportional voting
- Independent redistricting commissions

### Congressional Ethics Reform Act

- Independent ethics office
- Mandatory investigation timelines
- Public reporting requirements

### Congressional Transparency Act

- Real-time financial disclosure
- Lobbyist meeting disclosure
- Campaign finance transparency

---

## Procedural Implementation

### House Rules Changes

- Adopt proportional voting for committee assignments
- Establish discharge petition reforms
- Limit committee chair power

### Senate Rules Changes

- Reform or eliminate filibuster
- Establish majority cloture for nominations
- Require floor votes on House-passed legislation

---

## Metrics for Success

- Proportionality index (votes to seats)
- Time to floor vote for legislation
- Ethics investigation completion rate
- Disclosure compliance rate
