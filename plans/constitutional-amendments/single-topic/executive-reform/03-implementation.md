# Executive Reform Implementation

## Overview

This document outlines implementation considerations for executive reform constitutional provisions.

---

## Enabling Legislation

### Executive Order Reform Act

- Define scope of executive orders
- Establish one-year sunset for policy orders
- Create congressional disapproval procedures

### Independent Agency Protection Act

- Define "cause" for removal
- Establish judicial review of removals
- Protect inspector general independence

### Presidential Accountability Act

- Subpoena enforcement procedures
- Executive privilege limits
- Records preservation requirements

---

## Pardon Reform Implementation

### Self-Executing Provisions

- Pardon limits take effect upon ratification
- DOJ shall not recognize void pardons
- Courts shall not give effect to void pardons

### Reporting Requirements

- Late-term pardons reported to Congress
- Public justification required
- GAO review of pardon patterns

---

## Judicial Enforcement

### Standing

- Removed officials may challenge in court
- Congress has standing to enforce subpoenas
- Citizens have standing to challenge void pardons

### Review Standards

- De novo review of removal decisions
- Expedited review of subpoena disputes
- Declaration of pardon validity

---

## Metrics for Success

- Executive order volume (reduction expected)
- Congressional ratification rate
- Successful removal challenges
- Pardon compliance rate
