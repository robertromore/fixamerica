# Tax Reform Implementation

## Overview

This document outlines implementation considerations for tax reform constitutional principles.

---

## Enabling Legislation

### Tax Simplification Act

- Eliminate or consolidate tax expenditures
- Broaden tax base
- Reduce rates proportionally
- Implement transparency requirements

### Tax Enforcement Enhancement Act

- Increase IRS funding
- Strengthen audit capabilities
- Enhance criminal penalties
- Close enforcement loopholes

---

## Metrics for Success

- Tax code length (reduction target)
- Effective rate by income level (progressivity measure)
- Tax gap (difference between owed and collected)
- Compliance costs (reduction target)
