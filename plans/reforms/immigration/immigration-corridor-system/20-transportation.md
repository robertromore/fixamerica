# Immigration Corridor System: Transportation

---

## Overview

Transportation infrastructure connects ICS participants to employment, education, healthcare, and community resources. This document details inter-city transit, travel documentation, mobility restrictions by tier, and transportation investments in corridor cities.

---

## Mobility by Tier

### Geographic Restrictions

| Tier | Mobility | Documentation |
|------|----------|---------------|
| **Phase 0** | Intake facility only | Provisional ID |
| **Tier A** | Hub city metro area | CLP card (Tier A) |
| **Tier B** | Corridor region (multi-city) | CLP card (Tier B) |
| **Tier C** | Full US mobility | CLP card (Tier C) |
| **Permanent Resident** | Full US + international | Green card |

### Travel Documentation

| Document | Tier | Use |
|----------|------|-----|
| **Provisional ICS ID** | Phase 0 | Facility identification |
| **CLP Card** | A/B/C | Primary identification; travel within allowed area |
| **State ID/Driver's License** | A+ (state option) | General identification; driving |
| **Advance Parole** | C only | International travel authorization |

## Inter-City Transportation

### Hub-to-Corridor Transit

| Mode | Use Case | Funding |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Bus (intercity)** | Primary hub-to-corridor transport | ICS contracted routes |
| **Rail (Amtrak)** | Where available | Subsidized tickets |
| **Air** | Long-distance placement | ICS-funded for initial placement |
| **Private vehicle** | If participant owns | No ICS subsidy |

### Initial Placement Transport

| From | To | Mode | Cost |
|------|-----|------|------|
| **Border processing** | Hub city | Bus/air | Fully covered |
| **Hub city** | Corridor city | Bus/rail | Fully covered |
| **Corridor city** | Final housing | Local transit | Covered for 90 days |

### Relocation Within System

| Situation | Transport Provided |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Tier advancement** | One-way transport to new location |
| **Approved transfer** | Transport to new corridor city |
| **Employment-based move** | Employer or self-funded |
| **Emergency relocation** | Covered by ICS |

### Domestic Air Travel

| Tier | Air Travel |
|------|------------|
| **Tier A** | Not permitted outside hub metro |
| **Tier B** | Within corridor region only |

| Requirement | CLP Compliance |
|-------------|----------------|

| Tier | International Travel |
|------|---------------------|

| **Tier C** | Advance parole available |

## Safety and Compliance

### Transportation Checkpoints

| Policy | ICS Position |
|--------|--------------|
| **Interior checkpoints** | CLP holders have lawful presence documentation |
| **Traffic stops** | CLP card + license (if held) |
| **Bus/train checks** | CLP card is valid travel document |

### Compliance Verification

| Situation | Protocol |
|-----------|----------|
| **Tier A outside hub** | Violation; warning then consequences |
| **Tier B outside corridor** | Violation; tier freeze possible |
| **Verification method** | CLP card has tier/location encoded |

### Mobility Enforcement Guardrails (Summary)

- Verification limited to program administration contexts; no random street checks.
- No continuous location tracking; verification logs are time-limited and audited.
- Adverse actions require notice, hearing, and appeal; no automated-only decisions.

### Due Process

| Violation | Process |
|-----------|---------|
| **First violation** | Warning; documented |
| **Repeat violations** | Tier freeze; hearing available |
| **Appeal** | Oversight Office review |
