# Immigration Corridor System: Employer Compliance

---

## Overview

Employers are essential partners in ICS success. This document details employer registration requirements, sector-specific rules, worker protection mechanisms, wage enforcement, and anti-exploitation measures. The system balances employer flexibility with robust worker safeguards.

---

## Employer Registration

### Registration Requirement

| Employer Type | Registration Required | Timeline |
|---------------|----------------------|----------|
| **Hiring 1+ CLP workers** | Yes | Before first hire |
| **Staffing agencies** | Yes (enhanced) | Before placing CLP workers |
| **Agricultural employers** | Yes (sector-specific) | Before season |
| **Household employers** | Simplified registration | Within 30 days of hire |

### Registration Process

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| **1. Application** | Submit online via employer portal | Day 0 |
| **2. Business verification** | EIN, state registration, OSHA history | Days 1-5 |
| **3. Labor law compliance check** | WHD violations, NLRB orders, discrimination findings | Days 1-5 |
| **4. Wage attestation** | Certify prevailing wage compliance | Day 5 |
| **5. Approval/denial** | Registration decision issued | Days 5-10 |
| **6. Annual renewal** | Update attestations; reverification | Annually |

### Registration Denial

| Basis | Standard |
|-------|----------|
| **Wage violations** | Pattern of WHD findings in past 3 years |
| **Safety violations** | Willful OSHA citations in past 3 years |
| **Discrimination** | EEOC findings or consent decrees |
| **Immigration violations** | Prior I-9 penalties; knowingly hired unauthorized |
| **Labor violations** | NLRA violations; anti-union activity findings |

### Registration Tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Standard** | Basic compliance | Can hire CLP workers |
| **Preferred** | 3+ years clean record; training programs | Expedited hiring; tax credit eligible |
| **Probationary** | Recent minor violations | Enhanced monitoring; limited hires |

## Wage Requirements

### Prevailing Wage Standard

| Requirement | Application |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Minimum** | Greater of: federal minimum, state minimum, local minimum |
| **Prevailing wage** | DOL occupational wage for geographic area |
| **CLP floor** | CLP workers paid no less than similarly situated US workers |
| **No wage depression** | Hiring CLP workers cannot reduce existing worker wages |

### Wage Verification

| Mechanism | Frequency |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Payroll reporting** | Quarterly to DOL |
| **Wage attestation** | At registration; annually |
| **Spot audits** | Random selection (5% of employers annually) |
| **Complaint-triggered** | Any credible wage complaint |

### Wage Violation Consequences

| Violation | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **Underpayment (<10%)** | Back wages + 25% penalty | Back wages + 50% penalty; probation |
| **Underpayment (10-25%)** | Back wages + 50% penalty | Suspension 6 months |
| **Underpayment (>25%)** | Back wages + 100% penalty; probation | Debarment 2 years |
| **Wage theft** | Criminal referral; debarment | Criminal referral; permanent debarment |

## Sector-Specific Requirements

### Construction

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Prevailing wage** | Davis-Bacon rates for federal projects |
| **Safety training** | OSHA 10 required within 30 days |
| **Apprenticeship** | Registered apprenticeship access encouraged |
| **Union neutrality** | No anti-union requirements |

### Agriculture

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Housing** | If provided, meets OSHA field sanitation + DOL housing standards |
| **H-2A parity** | Terms no less favorable than H-2A workers |
| **Transportation** | If provided, meets DOT safety standards |
| **Heat illness prevention** | Required program in applicable states |
| **Child labor** | Full FLSA child labor protections apply |

### Healthcare

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Credential verification** | Employer verifies credential evaluation |
| **Supervision** | Appropriate supervision during bridge programs |
| **Malpractice** | Standard coverage required |

### Hospitality

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Tip credit** | State law applies; full minimum for non-tipped |
| **Service charges** | Disclosure requirements |
| **Scheduling** | Predictive scheduling where state requires |

### Domestic/Household

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Minimum wage** | Full FLSA minimum wage |
| **Overtime** | Applicable after live-in exclusion eliminated |
| **Written agreement** | Required employment contract |
| **Workers' comp** | Required coverage |

### Staffing Agencies

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Joint liability** | Agency and client jointly liable |
| **Disclosure** | Must disclose host employer identity |
| **Placement fees** | Prohibited from charging workers |
| **Duration limits** | Cannot keep worker in temp status indefinitely |

## Worker Protections

### Anti-Retaliation

| Protection | Standard |
|------------|----------|
| **Complaint protection** | No adverse action for filing complaints |
| **Status-based retaliation** | Cannot threaten immigration consequences |
| **Whistleblower** | Full whistleblower protections |
| **Remedies** | Reinstatement, back pay, compensatory damages |

### Anti-Trafficking

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Document retention** | Prohibited (employer cannot hold documents) |
| **Debt bondage** | Prohibited (no fees that create debt) |
| **Movement restriction** | Cannot restrict worker movement |
| **Recruitment fees** | Employer-paid; worker fees prohibited |
| **Reporting** | Mandatory reporting of trafficking indicators |

### Workplace Safety

| Standard | Application |
|----------|-------------|
| **OSHA coverage** | Full OSHA protections |
| **Language access** | Safety training in worker's language |
| **PPE** | Employer-provided at no cost |
| **Hazard communication** | Multilingual required |
| **Injury reporting** | No retaliation for reporting |

### Anti-Discrimination

| Protection | Standard |
|------------|----------|
| **Title VII** | Full coverage (race, color, religion, sex, national origin) |
| **INA anti-discrimination** | Citizenship/national origin in hiring |
| **CLP-based discrimination** | Prohibited; treat as similarly situated to work-authorized |
| **Language discrimination** | English-only rules must be justified |

## Compliance Monitoring

### Self-Reporting Requirements

| Report | Frequency | Content |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **Payroll report** | Quarterly | Wages, hours, job classifications |
| **Workforce report** | Annually | CLP worker count by classification |
| **Incident report** | Within 72 hours | Injuries, complaints, terminations |
| **Separation report** | Within 7 days | Reason for any CLP worker separation |

### Government Audits

| Audit Type | Trigger | Scope |
|------------|---------|-------|
| **Random audit** | 5% annual selection | Payroll, I-9, safety |
| **Complaint audit** | Credible complaint | Full investigation |
| **Sector audit** | High-risk sector selection | Industry-wide |
| **Follow-up audit** | Prior violation | Verify remediation |

### Third-Party Monitoring

| Mechanism | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Union access** | Right to organize; union representatives can access |
| **Worker center access** | Community organizations can conduct outreach |
| **Legal services** | Workers have right to legal representation |
| **Hotline** | DOL/DHS joint hotline for complaints |

## Enforcement

### Investigation Process

| Stage | Timeline | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Complaint receipt** | Day 0 | Intake; initial assessment |
| **Investigation opening** | Days 1-14 | Notice to employer; document request |
| **On-site investigation** | Days 15-45 | Interviews; payroll review |
| **Preliminary findings** | Days 45-60 | Employer response opportunity |
| **Final determination** | Days 60-90 | Findings; remedies ordered |
| **Appeal** | Days 90-120 | Administrative appeal available |

### Remedies

| Remedy | Application |
|--------|-------------|
| **Back wages** | Full amount owed plus interest |
| **Liquidated damages** | Equal to back wages for willful violations |
| **Civil penalties** | Per-violation fines |
| **Injunctive relief** | Cease and desist; compliance orders |
| **Debarment** | Temporary or permanent bar from hiring CLP workers |

### Criminal Referrals

| Conduct | Referral Basis |
|---------|----------------|
| **Trafficking** | 18 U.S.C. ยง 1589 (forced labor) |
| **Document servitude** | 18 U.S.C. ยง 1592 |
| **Wage theft (severe)** | State criminal statutes |
| **Fraud** | False statements to federal agency |

## Staffing and Subcontracting

### Joint Employment

| Situation | Liability |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Staffing agency placement** | Joint and several liability |
| **Subcontractor workers** | General contractor has oversight duty |
| **Franchise** | Franchisee primarily liable; franchisor if control |

### Subcontractor Requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Verification** | Prime must verify sub registration |
| **Flow-down** | Wage/safety requirements flow to subs |
| **Monitoring** | Prime must monitor sub compliance |
| **Termination** | Must terminate non-compliant subs |

## Portability and Worker Rights

### Job Mobility

| Right | Protection |
|-------|------------|
| **Quit freely** | CLP status continues; no employer tie |
| **Change employers** | No approval needed; notify within 14 days |
| **Multiple jobs** | Permitted; all employers must register |
| **Self-employment** | Permitted with business registration |

### Notice Requirements

| Action | Notice |
|--------|--------|
| **Voluntary separation** | 2 weeks (encouraged, not required) |
| **Involuntary termination** | Employer reports within 7 days |
| **Layoff** | WARN Act applies (60-day notice for mass layoffs) |

### Unemployment Insurance

| Coverage | Standard |
|----------|----------|
| **CLP workers** | Covered under state UI |
| **Employer contribution** | Standard FUTA/SUTA rates |
| **Eligibility** | Same as US workers |
