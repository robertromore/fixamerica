# Appendix B: Regulatory Authorities

---

## Overview

This appendix details the regulatory authorities required to implement ICS, organized by agency. Each agency must promulgate rules within its statutory mandate to operationalize the system.

---

## Department of Homeland Security (DHS)

### USCIS Regulations

| Topic | Authority | Key Provisions |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| **CLP issuance** | INA + ICS statute | Application procedures, documentation requirements, processing timelines |
| **Biometrics standards** | 8 CFR | Collection, storage, and sharing protocols |
| **Status verification** | E-Verify authority | Employer verification procedures for CLP holders |
| **Tier advancement** | ICS statute | Criteria, documentation, appeal rights |
| **Revocation triggers** | ICS statute | Grounds for revocation, due process requirements |

### CBP Regulations

| Topic | Authority | Key Provisions |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| **Border processing** | INA Section 235 | Intake procedures, initial screening, transfer protocols |
| **Surge operations** | ICS statute | Trigger conditions, staffing mobilization, facility standards |
| **Data collection** | Privacy Act | Minimum data requirements, retention limits |

### ICE Regulations

| Topic | Authority | Key Provisions |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| **Enforcement priorities** | Prosecutorial discretion | CLP compliance enforcement, violation response |
| **Removal procedures** | INA Section 240 | Due process for CLP revocation and removal |
| **Detention standards** | ICS statute | Alternatives to detention, facility conditions |

## Required Rulemaking Elements

### Tier Advancement Standards

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Tier C benchmarks** | Objective criteria for advancement |
| **Denial standards** | Grounds for denial with appeal rights |
| **Appeal timelines** | Maximum processing times |

### Algorithmic Fairness

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Fairness thresholds** | Disparate impact limits |
| **Audit cadence** | Quarterly or annual review |
| **Public disclosure** | Methodology publication requirements |

### RID Administration

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Eligibility caps** | Maximum payments per household |
| **Verification procedures** | Documentation requirements |
| **Fraud penalties** | Civil and criminal consequences |

### Data Governance

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Minimization** | Collection limited to necessary fields |
| **Retention limits** | Default 5-year retention |
| **Privacy-safe sharing** | Inter-agency sharing protocols |

## Rulemaking Timeline

| Phase | Timeline | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Advance notice** | Months 1-3 | Agency consultation, stakeholder input |
| **Proposed rules** | Months 4-8 | Publication in Federal Register |
| **Comment period** | Months 8-11 | 60-90 day public comment |
| **Final rules** | Months 12-18 | Response to comments, final publication |
| **Effective date** | Month 18+ | Phased implementation |
