# Appendix Q: Training Curriculum

---

## Overview

This appendix outlines the training curriculum for ICS staff, partners, and stakeholders. Effective training ensures consistent, high-quality implementation across all hub and corridor cities.

---

## Training Framework

### Training Levels

| Level | Audience | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Level 1: Awareness** | All staff; community partners | 4-8 hours |
| **Level 2: Foundational** | Direct service staff | 40 hours |
| **Level 3: Specialized** | Role-specific training | 24-80 hours |
| **Level 4: Leadership** | Supervisors and managers | 40 hours |
| **Level 5: Expert** | Trainers; policy specialists | 80+ hours |

### Delivery Methods

| Method | Application |
|--------|-------------|
| **In-person classroom** | Core training; specialized skills |
| **Virtual instructor-led** | Remote staff; refreshers |
| **Self-paced online** | Foundational knowledge; compliance |
| **Simulation/role-play** | Interview skills; crisis response |
| **Field practicum** | On-the-job training |
| **Peer learning** | Cross-hub exchanges |
