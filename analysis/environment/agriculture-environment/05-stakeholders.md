# Agriculture and Environment: Stakeholders

## Overview

Agricultural environmental policy involves diverse stakeholders with varying interests. Farmers range from industrial operations to sustainable practitioners, agribusiness promotes input-intensive systems, environmental groups advocate for reform, and rural communities face both economic and environmental concerns. Understanding stakeholder dynamics is essential for building coalitions and advancing sustainable agriculture.
