# Agriculture and Environment: Solutions

## Overview

Transitioning to sustainable agriculture requires comprehensive reform of farm programs, conservation approaches, market incentives, and regulatory frameworks. Solutions must support farmers through the transition while achieving measurable environmental outcomes. This document outlines evidence-based solutions for agricultural environmental challenges.
