# Agriculture and Environment: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can advance sustainable agriculture through consumer choices, policy advocacy, community engagement, and direct action. Individual purchasing decisions send market signals, while collective advocacy drives policy change. Both are necessary for transforming the food and agriculture system.

## Consumer Actions

### Food Purchasing

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Buy organic** | Support chemical-free farming |
| **Buy local** | Support regional agriculture |
| **Farmers markets** | Direct farmer support |
| **CSA membership** | Seasonal, local food |
| **Regenerative products** | Emerging market |
