# Agriculture and Environment: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transitioning to sustainable agriculture requires sustained action across farm programs, conservation, markets, and research. This roadmap outlines a phased approach leveraging Farm Bill cycles, administrative action, market development, and state/local initiatives to achieve measurable environmental outcomes.
