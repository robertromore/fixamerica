# Environmental Justice: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Achieving environmental justice requires sustained, phased action across federal, state, and local levels. This roadmap outlines a realistic implementation timeline balancing immediate actions with long-term systemic change. Success requires building political power, reforming institutions, and securing durable legal protections.
