# Environmental Justice: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can advance environmental justice through community organizing, policy advocacy, monitoring, and coalition building. Whether living in an affected community or acting as an ally, individual and collective action can shift power dynamics and achieve environmental equity.
