# Clean Energy: Current State

## Overview

U.S. clean energy deployment is accelerating rapidly following passage of the Inflation Reduction Act, with record investment, manufacturing announcements, and project pipelines. However, significant barriers remain in permitting, transmission, supply chains, and workforce, threatening the pace needed to meet climate goals.
