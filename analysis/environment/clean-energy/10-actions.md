# Clean Energy: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can accelerate the clean energy transition through personal choices, consumer power, advocacy, and political engagement. Individual actions contribute to both direct impact and building momentum for systemic change.

## Personal Energy Choices

### Home Electricity

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Green power** | Purchase renewable electricity |
| **Community solar** | Subscribe if available |
| **Rooftop solar** | Install panels |
| **Battery storage** | Add home battery |
| **Green tariffs** | Utility programs |

### Energy Efficiency

| Action | Savings |
|--------|---------|
| LED lighting | 75% electricity |
| Smart thermostat | 10-15% heating/cooling |
| Insulation | 20%+ heating/cooling |

| Appliance upgrades | Efficient models |

### Electrification
