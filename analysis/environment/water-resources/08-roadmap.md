# Water Resources: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming America's water systems requires sustained, phased action on infrastructure, allocation, quality, and governance. This roadmap outlines a realistic implementation timeline balancing immediate needs with long-term systemic change. Success requires political commitment, adequate funding, and adaptive management.
