# Water Resources: Opposition

## Overview

Water resource reforms face opposition from entrenched interests benefiting from existing arrangements. Agricultural water users resist reallocation, ratepayers resist rate increases, states resist federal authority, and property rights advocates resist regulation. Understanding opposition dynamics is essential for developing effective strategies.
