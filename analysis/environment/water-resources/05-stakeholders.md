# Water Resources: Stakeholders

## Overview

Water resources involve diverse stakeholders with competing interests over a shared resource. Municipal utilities, agricultural users, industry, environmental advocates, tribal nations, and the public all have legitimate but often conflicting claims. Understanding stakeholder dynamics is essential for developing sustainable water solutions.

## Water Users

### Municipal Utilities

| Type | Characteristics |
|------|-----------------|

| **Medium cities** | Variable capacity |
