# Water Resources: Solutions

## Overview

Solving America's water challenges requires comprehensive action on infrastructure, allocation, conservation, and governance. Solutions must balance immediate needs with long-term sustainability, address equity concerns, adapt to climate change, and reform outdated frameworks. This document outlines evidence-based solutions across key water issues.
