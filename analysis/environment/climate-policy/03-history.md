# Climate Policy: History

## Overview

U.S. climate policy history reflects cycles of scientific advancement, political engagement, and policy reversals. From early environmental awareness through partisan polarization, the trajectory has been marked by insufficient action relative to the growing scientific consensus on urgency.

## Timeline of Key Developments

### Scientific Foundation (1950s-1980s)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 1958 | Keeling begins CO2 measurements |
| 1965 | President's Science Advisory Committee warns of warming |
| 1970 | Earth Day, EPA created |
| 1979 | First World Climate Conference |
| 1988 | NASA's Hansen testifies to Congress |
| 1988 | IPCC established |

### Early Policy Era (1989-2000)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 1989 | First climate bills introduced |
| 1992 | UNFCCC signed at Rio Earth Summit |
| 1993 | Clinton proposes BTU tax (fails) |
| 1997 | Kyoto Protocol adopted |
| 1997 | Byrd-Hagel Resolution (95-0 against Kyoto) |
| 1998 | Hottest year on record (at time) |

### Bush Era Retreat (2001-2008)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2001 | US withdraws from Kyoto Protocol |
| 2003 | McCain-Lieberman climate bill fails |
| 2005 | Katrina highlights climate risks |
| 2006 | *An Inconvenient Truth* released |
| 2007 | Supreme Court: EPA can regulate GHGs |
| 2007 | IPCC Fourth Assessment, Nobel Prize |
| 2008 | Lieberman-Warner bill fails |

### Obama Era Advances (2009-2016)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2009 | Waxman-Markey passes House |
| 2009 | EPA endangerment finding |
| 2009 | Copenhagen Accord |
| 2010 | Cap-and-trade dies in Senate |
| 2013 | Climate Action Plan announced |
| 2014 | US-China climate deal |
| 2015 | Clean Power Plan finalized |
| 2015 | Paris Agreement adopted |
| 2016 | Paris Agreement enters force |

### Trump Era Reversal (2017-2020)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2017 | Executive order rolling back climate policies |
| 2017 | Paris withdrawal announced |
| 2019 | EPA finalizes CPP repeal |
| 2019 | ACE rule replaces CPP |
| 2020 | Formal Paris withdrawal |

### Biden Era Action (2021-Present)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2021 | Rejoins Paris Agreement |
| 2021 | Enhanced NDC (50-52% by 2030) |

## Key Legislative History

### Failed Cap-and-Trade

| Bill | Year | Outcome |
|------|------|---------|
| **McCain-Lieberman** | 2003, 2005 | Failed |
| **Lieberman-Warner** | 2008 | Filibustered |
| **Waxman-Markey** | 2009 | Passed House, died in Senate |
| **Kerry-Boxer** | 2009 | Never voted |

### Successful Legislation

| Law | Year | Key Provisions |
|-----|------|----------------|
| **Clean Air Act Amendments** | 1990 | Framework for GHG regulation |
| **ARRA** | 2009 | Clean energy investments |
| **IIJA** | 2021 | Infrastructure, grid, EVs |
| **IRA** | 2022 | $370B climate investment |
