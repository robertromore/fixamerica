# Climate Policy: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can take meaningful action on climate through personal choices, civic engagement, community organizing, and political participation. Individual actions matter both for direct impact and for building the social and political momentum for systemic change.

## Personal Actions

### Reduce Your Footprint

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Home energy** | Efficiency, renewable power |
| **Transportation** | EV, transit, biking, less driving |
| **Diet** | Less meat, local food |
| **Flying** | Reduce air travel |
| **Consumption** | Buy less, buy better |

### Home Energy

| Action | Savings |
|--------|---------|
| LED lighting | 75% electricity |
| Smart thermostat | 10-15% heating/cooling |
| Insulation | 20%+ heating/cooling |
| Heat pump | 50%+ heating |
| Solar panels | Significant, varies |
| Green electricity | 100% if available |

### Transportation
