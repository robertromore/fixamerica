# Conservation: Current State

## Overview

U.S. conservation efforts are at an inflection point, with the America the Beautiful initiative establishing ambitious goals while facing continued development pressure, climate impacts, and chronic underfunding. Public lands face record visitation, wildlife populations show mixed trends, and the biodiversity crisis deepens despite decades of endangered species protections.
