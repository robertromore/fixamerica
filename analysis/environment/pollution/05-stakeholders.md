# Pollution: Stakeholders

## Overview

Pollution control involves diverse stakeholders with different interests, resources, and influence. Understanding these dynamics is essential for building coalitions and advancing reform.

## Government Stakeholders

### Federal Agencies

| Agency | Role | Position |
|--------|------|----------|
| **EPA** | Primary regulator | Stronger standards |
| **OSHA** | Worker safety | Workplace exposure |
| **FDA** | Food safety | Contamination |
| **USDA** | Agriculture | Agricultural practices |
| **DOD** | Federal sites | Major polluter/cleaner |
| **DOE** | Energy facilities | Cleanup responsibility |

### Congress

| Group | Position |
|-------|----------|
| **Environmental Democrats** | Strong regulation |
| **Moderate Democrats** | Balance approach |
| **Some Republicans** | Local pollution concerns |
| **Most Republicans** | Reduce regulation |

### State and Local
