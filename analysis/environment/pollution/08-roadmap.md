# Pollution: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Reducing pollution requires sustained action across regulatory, enforcement, and investment dimensions. This roadmap outlines a phased approach to achieving cleaner air, water, and communities.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)

### Standards

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Review air quality standards | Year 1 | EPA |
| Finalize PFAS drinking water | Year 1 | EPA |
| Update industrial water limits | Year 1-2 | EPA |
| Strengthen mobile source rules | Year 1-2 | EPA |
| Toxic chemical prioritization | Year 1 | EPA |

### Enforcement

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Increase inspections | Year 1 | EPA/States |
| Enhanced EJ enforcement | Year 1 | EPA |
| Criminal prosecution | Year 1 | DOJ |
| State capacity grants | Year 1-2 | EPA |

### Infrastructure

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Lead service line inventory | Year 1 | Utilities |
| PFAS treatment planning | Year 1 | Utilities |
| Monitoring expansion | Year 1-2 | EPA/States |
| Community monitoring grants | Year 1 | EPA |

### Environmental Justice

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Cumulative impact methodology | Year 1 | EPA |
| EJ in permitting | Year 1 | EPA |
| Community grants | Year 1 | EPA |
| Title VI enforcement | Year 1 | EPA |
