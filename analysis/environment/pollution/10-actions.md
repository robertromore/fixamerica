# Pollution: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can take meaningful action on pollution through personal choices, community engagement, advocacy, and political participation. Individual actions contribute to both direct impact and building pressure for systemic change.

## Personal Actions

### Reduce Your Contribution

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Transportation** | Drive less, EVs, transit |
| **Home energy** | Efficiency, clean power |
| **Products** | Non-toxic alternatives |
| **Water use** | Conservation |
| **Waste** | Reduce, reuse, recycle |

### Protect Yourself

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Check air quality** | AirNow.gov daily |

| **Test home** | Lead, radon |
| **Avoid exposure** | Bad air days |
| **Know sources** | Local polluters |

### Consumer Choices
