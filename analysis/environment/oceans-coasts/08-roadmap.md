# Oceans and Coasts: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Protecting oceans and coasts requires sustained action across conservation, fisheries, pollution, and resilience. This roadmap outlines a phased approach balancing immediate needs with long-term transformation, recognizing that ocean challenges require both domestic and international action.
