# Oceans and Coasts: Solutions

## Overview

Protecting oceans and coasts requires comprehensive action on climate change, pollution, fisheries, conservation, and coastal resilience. Solutions must operate at local to global scales, integrating marine protection with coastal adaptation. This document outlines evidence-based solutions for ocean and coastal challenges.
