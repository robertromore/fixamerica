# Oceans and Coasts: Stakeholders

## Overview

Ocean and coastal policy involves diverse stakeholders with varying interests in marine resources, coastal land, and ocean health. Fishing communities, coastal residents, conservation groups, industries, and government agencies all have stakes in how oceans and coasts are managed. Understanding stakeholder dynamics is essential for developing effective policies.
