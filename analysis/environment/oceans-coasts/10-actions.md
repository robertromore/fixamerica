# Oceans and Coasts: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can advance ocean and coastal protection through personal choices, community engagement, and policy advocacy. Individual actions reduce pollution and support sustainable practices, while collective action drives systemic change in ocean policy and coastal management.
