# Transportation Emissions: Root Causes

## Overview

High transportation emissions result from systemic factors including market failures that don't price pollution, infrastructure investments that favor driving, land use patterns that require car travel, and policy choices that subsidize fossil fuels. Addressing emissions requires understanding these interconnected causes and intervening at multiple levels simultaneously.
