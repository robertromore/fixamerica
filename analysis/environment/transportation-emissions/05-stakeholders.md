# Transportation Emissions: Stakeholders

## Overview

Transportation emissions policy involves diverse stakeholders with competing interests: vehicle manufacturers transitioning to EVs, oil companies protecting existing markets, transit agencies seeking funding, environmental groups demanding rapid action, labor unions concerned about job impacts, and communities bearing pollution burdens. Understanding these stakeholders and their interests is essential for building coalitions and navigating opposition.
