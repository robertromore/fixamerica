# Transportation Emissions: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Decarbonizing transportation requires coordinated action over multiple timeframes: immediate standards and incentives, medium-term infrastructure and market transformation, and long-term land use and system change. This roadmap aligns federal, state, and local actions with technology trajectories and political windows to maximize emissions reductions while ensuring equity.
