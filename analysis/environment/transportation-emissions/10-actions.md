# Transportation Emissions: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can reduce transportation emissions through personal choices, civic engagement, and community organizing. Individual actions like driving less or choosing an EV matter, but collective action to change policy has far greater impact. This guide provides concrete steps for different situations and commitment levels.

## Personal Transportation Choices

### Vehicle Decisions

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Choose EV for next vehicle** | Eliminates tailpipe emissions |
| **Choose hybrid** | Reduces fuel use |
| **Right-size your vehicle** | Smaller is more efficient |
| **Maintain current vehicle** | Extend useful life |
| **Keep vehicle longer** | Avoid embedded emissions |
