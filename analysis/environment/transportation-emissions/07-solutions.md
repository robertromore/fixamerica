# Transportation Emissions: Solutions

## Overview

Reducing transportation emissions requires a comprehensive strategy combining vehicle electrification, improved efficiency standards, mode shift to transit and active transportation, land use reform, and carbon pricing. No single solution is sufficient; all approaches must be pursued simultaneously to achieve climate targets while ensuring equitable outcomes.
