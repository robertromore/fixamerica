# Transportation Emissions: Current State

## Overview

Transportation remains America's largest source of greenhouse gas emissions, surpassing electricity generation in 2017. Despite improvements in vehicle efficiency and growth of electric vehicles, total emissions remain stubbornly high due to increased driving and consumer preference for larger vehicles. The transition to clean transportation is underway but faces significant challenges in scale, equity, and infrastructure.
