# Waste Management: Stakeholders

## Overview

Waste management involves diverse stakeholders with often competing interests. Producers, consumers, waste management companies, local governments, and affected communities all have stakes in how materials flow through the economy. Understanding stakeholder dynamics is essential for designing effective reforms that balance competing interests while protecting public health and the environment.
