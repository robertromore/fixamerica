# Waste Management: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can advance waste reform through personal waste reduction, community engagement, and policy advocacy. Individual actions reduce personal impact while collective action drives systemic change toward a circular economy. Both are necessary for meaningful progress.

## Personal Actions

### Reduce First

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Buy less** | Fundamental waste reduction |
| **Choose durable** | Quality over quantity |
| **Avoid single-use** | Refuse disposables |
| **Repair instead of replace** | Extend product life |
| **Borrow/share** | Reduce individual ownership |
