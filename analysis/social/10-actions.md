## For People Navigating Benefits

### Accessing Benefits

| Action | How | Impact |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Check eligibility** | Benefits.gov, local agencies | Know what you qualify for |
| **Apply for multiple programs** | SNAP, Medicaid, LIHEAP, EITC | Layer supports |
| **Get help applying** | 211, community organizations | Navigate bureaucracy |
| **File taxes for credits** | Free tax prep, GetCTC.org | Claim EITC, CTC |
| **Appeal denials** | Request hearing, get help | Overturn wrong decisions |

### Protecting Your Benefits

| Action | How | Impact |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Report changes promptly** | Income, household changes | Avoid overpayments |
| **Keep documentation** | Pay stubs, letters, receipts | Prove eligibility |
| **Know your rights** | Due process, appeal rights | Protect against errors |
| **Meet deadlines** | Recertification, appointments | Maintain coverage |
| **Ask for accommodations** | Disability, language | Accessible process |

### Getting Help

| Resource | Contact |
|----------|---------|
| **211 Helpline** | Dial 211 |
| **Legal aid** | lawhelp.org |
| **Benefits assistance** | Benefits.gov |
| **Tax help** | IRS Free File, VITA sites |
| **Food assistance** | FNS.usda.gov |

## Quick Reference

### If You Have 5 Minutes

- Share information about benefits on social media
- Sign a petition supporting CTC or SNAP
- Call your representative about safety net legislation
- Donate to a direct giving organization
- Text a friend about free tax filing

### If You Have 1 Hour

- Help a neighbor apply for benefits
- Volunteer at a food pantry
- Attend a local advocacy meeting
- Write to your legislator about poverty
- Get certified as a VITA volunteer

### If You Have Ongoing Commitment

- Become a benefits navigator
- Join an anti-poverty coalition
- Organize in your community
- Run for local office on economic security
- Build relationships with people experiencing poverty
- Advocate for systemic change
