## Legal Resources

### Benefits Counseling

**Work Incentives Planning and Assistance (WIPA)**:

- Free counseling for SSI recipients considering work
- Funded by SSA
- Available through local providers
- Find at: ssa.gov/work/WIPA.html

**State Health Insurance Assistance Programs (SHIP)**:

- Benefits counseling for Medicare/Medicaid
- State-based programs
- Free assistance

**Benefits Access Programs**:

- NCOA Benefits Checkup (benefitscheckup.org)

- Local benefits counselors
