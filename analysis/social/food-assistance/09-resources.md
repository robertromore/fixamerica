# Food Assistance: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Government Agencies

- **USDA Food and Nutrition Service (FNS)**: [fns.usda.gov](https://www.fns.usda.gov) - Federal program administration
- **USDA Economic Research Service**: [ers.usda.gov](https://www.ers.usda.gov) - Food security research
- **Government Accountability Office (GAO)**: [gao.gov](https://www.gao.gov) - Program oversight and audits
- **Congressional Research Service**: Reports on nutrition programs
- **Congressional Budget Office (CBO)**: [cbo.gov](https://www.cbo.gov) - Fiscal analysis of program costs

### Research Organizations

- **Food Research & Action Center (FRAC)**: [frac.org](https://frac.org) - Leading anti-hunger policy research
- **Center on Budget and Policy Priorities**: [cbpp.org](https://www.cbpp.org) - SNAP and nutrition policy analysis
- **Urban Institute**: [urban.org](https://www.urban.org) - Poverty and nutrition research
- **Brookings Institution**: [brookings.edu](https://www.brookings.edu) - Policy research
- **Mathematica**: [mathematica.org](https://www.mathematica.org) - SNAP and WIC program evaluations
- **Feeding America Research**: [feedingamerica.org/research](https://www.feedingamerica.org/research) - Food bank network data

### Advocacy Organizations

- **Feeding America**: [feedingamerica.org](https://www.feedingamerica.org) - National food bank network
- **No Kid Hungry/Share Our Strength**: [nokidhungry.org](https://www.nokidhungry.org) - Child hunger focus
- **Bread for the World**: [bread.org](https://www.bread.org) - Faith-based anti-hunger advocacy
- **National WIC Association**: [nwica.org](https://www.nwica.org) - WIC program advocacy
- **School Nutrition Association**: [schoolnutrition.org](https://www.schoolnutrition.org) - School meals advocacy
- **Meals on Wheels America**: [mealsonwheelsamerica.org](https://www.mealsonwheelsamerica.org) - Senior nutrition
- **Food Bank News**: [foodbanknews.org](https://www.foodbanknews.org) - Industry news and analysis

### State-Level Organizations

- **State SNAP agencies**: Contact varies by state
- **State food bank associations**: Network through Feeding America
- **Anti-hunger coalitions**: Most states have coordinating bodies
- **State nutrition action networks**: FRAC coordinates state partners

### Reports and Analysis

- USDA ERS. "Household Food Security in the United States." Annual. [ers.usda.gov](https://www.ers.usda.gov) - Definitive food security data
- CBPP. "SNAP Helps Millions of Low-Income Americans Put Food on the Table." Annual updates. [cbpp.org](https://www.cbpp.org)
- FRAC. "State of School Meals." Annual. [frac.org](https://frac.org) - School meal participation data
- Feeding America. "Map the Meal Gap." Annual. [feedingamerica.org](https://www.feedingamerica.org) - County-level food insecurity data
- GAO. Reports on SNAP fraud, error rates, and program effectiveness. [gao.gov](https://www.gao.gov)
- USDA FNS. "Characteristics of SNAP Households." Annual. [fns.usda.gov](https://www.fns.usda.gov)

## Data Sources

### Federal Data

- **USDA FNS Program Data**: [fns.usda.gov/data-research](https://www.fns.usda.gov/data-research) - Participation, costs, demographics
- **USDA ERS Food Security Data**: [ers.usda.gov/topics/food-nutrition-assistance/food-security-in-the-us](https://www.ers.usda.gov/topics/food-nutrition-assistance/food-security-in-the-us/)
- **Census CPS Food Security Supplement**: Annual food security measurement
- **SNAP Quality Control Data**: Error rates and characteristics
- **School Meals Data**: [fns.usda.gov/pd/child-nutrition-tables](https://www.fns.usda.gov/pd/child-nutrition-tables)

### Non-Federal Data

- **Feeding America Map the Meal Gap**: [map.feedingamerica.org](https://map.feedingamerica.org) - County-level estimates
- **Food Bank Client Data**: Feeding America network statistics
- **University Food Pantry Data**: College student food insecurity
- **Local food security surveys**: Various metropolitan and state surveys

### Economic Data

- **BLS Consumer Price Index**: Food price inflation
- **USDA Food Prices**: [ers.usda.gov/data-products/food-price-outlook](https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/food-price-outlook/)
- **CBO SNAP Projections**: Budget outlook
- **Economic stimulus estimates**: SNAP multiplier research

## Legal Resources

### Statutes

- 7 U.S.C. Chapter 51 (Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 - SNAP)
- 42 U.S.C. Chapter 13 (National School Lunch Act)
- 42 U.S.C. Chapter 13A (Child Nutrition Act - WIC, School Breakfast)
- 42 U.S.C. Chapter 35 (Older Americans Act - Senior Nutrition)
- 7 U.S.C. Chapter 102 (Emergency Food Assistance - TEFAP)

### Regulations

- 7 C.F.R. Parts 271-283 (SNAP regulations)
- 7 C.F.R. Part 246 (WIC regulations)
- 7 C.F.R. Parts 210, 220, 225, 226 (Child nutrition regulations)
- 45 C.F.R. Part 1321 (Older Americans Act nutrition)

### Key Cases

- *King v. Smith*, 392 U.S. 309 (1968) - Welfare eligibility
- *Goldberg v. Kelly*, 397 U.S. 254 (1970) - Due process in benefit termination
- *Atkins v. Parker*, 472 U.S. 115 (1985) - SNAP benefit reduction notice
