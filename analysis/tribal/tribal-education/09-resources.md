# Tribal Education: Resources

## Overview

This document provides curated resources for understanding tribal education issues, including academic research, government reports, legal authorities, books, organizations, and data sources.

## Legal Resources

### Key Statutes

| Statute | Citation | Subject |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Snyder Act | 25 U.S.C. 13 | General federal authority for Indian services including education |
| Johnson-O'Malley Act | 25 U.S.C. 5342-5348 | Federal contracts with states for AI/AN student education |
| Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act | 25 U.S.C. 5301 et seq. | Tribal contracting for educational services |
| Tribally Controlled Schools Act | 25 U.S.C. 2501 et seq. | Tribal operation of BIE schools |
| Tribally Controlled Colleges and Universities Assistance Act | 25 U.S.C. 1801 et seq. | Federal funding for tribal colleges |
| Native American Languages Act | 25 U.S.C. 2901 et seq. | Federal policy to preserve Indigenous languages |
| Esther Martinez Native American Languages Preservation Act | P.L. 109-394 (2006); reauthorized P.L. 116-101 (2019) | Language immersion program funding |
| Every Student Succeeds Act (tribal provisions) | 20 U.S.C. 6301 et seq. (Title VI, Part A) | Indian education programs; tribal consultation |
| Equity in Educational Land-Grant Status Act | 7 U.S.C. 301 note | TCU land-grant designation |
