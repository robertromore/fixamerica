# Federal Recognition: Resources

## Government Sources

### Federal Agencies

- **Bureau of Indian Affairs, Office of Federal Acknowledgment**: <https://www.bia.gov/as-ia/ofa> -- Official petition status, guidance documents, and final determinations
- **Government Accountability Office**: GAO-2012-348, "Indian Issues: Federal Funding for Non-Federally Recognized Tribes" (2012); GAO-2001-550, "Indian Issues: Improvements Needed in Tribal Recognition Process" (2001)
- **Congressional Research Service**: "Federal Recognition of Indian Tribes: Frequently Asked Questions" (updated periodically)
- **National Archives**: Treaty documents and historical records at <https://www.archives.gov/research/native-americans>

### Regulations

- **25 C.F.R. Part 83**: Procedures for Federal Acknowledgment of Indian Tribes (current version, as amended 2015)
- **80 Fed. Reg. 37862** (July 1, 2015): Final rule revising Part 83

### Congressional Materials

- **Senate Committee on Indian Affairs**: Hearing records on recognition reform
- **House Natural Resources Committee**: Subcommittee on Indian and Insular Affairs hearing records
- **Indian Gaming Regulatory Act**: 25 U.S.C. Sections 2701-2721

## Legal Sources

### Landmark Cases

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|-------------|
| *Johnson v. M'Intosh* | 21 U.S. 543 (1823) | Discovery doctrine; tribal occupancy rights |
| *Cherokee Nation v. Georgia* | 30 U.S. 1 (1831) | Tribes as "domestic dependent nations" |
| *Worcester v. Georgia* | 31 U.S. 515 (1832) | Federal exclusivity in Indian affairs |
| *Montoya v. United States* | 180 U.S. 261 (1901) | Definition of "tribe" |
| *Lone Wolf v. Hitchcock* | 187 U.S. 553 (1903) | Congressional plenary power |
| *Morton v. Mancari* | 417 U.S. 535 (1974) | Indian classification as political, not racial |
| *Santa Clara Pueblo v. Martinez* | 436 U.S. 49 (1978) | Tribal sovereignty in membership decisions |
| *Carcieri v. Salazar* | 555 U.S. 379 (2009) | Land-into-trust limited to pre-1934 tribes |
| *McGirt v. Oklahoma* | 591 U.S. ___ (2020) | Treaty reservations not disestablished absent clear intent |
| *Haaland v. Brackeen* | 599 U.S. 255 (2023) | Indian classification is political; ICWA upheld |

### Key Statutes

- Indian Reorganization Act of 1934: 25 U.S.C. Sections 5101-5145
- Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act of 1975: 25 U.S.C. Sections 5301-5423
- Indian Gaming Regulatory Act of 1988: 25 U.S.C. Sections 2701-2721
- Thomasina E. Jordan Indian Tribes of Virginia Federal Recognition Act of 2018: P.L. 115-121
- Little Shell Tribe of Chippewa Indians Restoration Act of 2019: P.L. 116-94, Division P, Title VII
