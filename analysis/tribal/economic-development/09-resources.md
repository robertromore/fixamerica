# Tribal Economic Development: Resources

## Overview

This document provides resources for understanding, researching, and supporting tribal economic development. Resources are organized by type for accessibility.

## Key Reports and Publications

### Federal Government Reports

| Report | Publisher | Year | Key Finding |
|--------|----------|------|-------------|
| *Broken Promises* | U.S. Commission on Civil Rights | 2018 | Federal underfunding of tribal programs by tens of billions |
| *Access to Capital in Indian Country* | CDFI Fund | 2023 | Banking deserts serve 86% of Native communities |
| Broadband Deployment Report (tribal) | FCC | 2023 | 65% of tribal lands lack broadband |
| BIA Labor Force Report | BIA | 2023 | 23% unemployment; 50%+ on poorest reservations |
| Indian Housing Block Grant Needs Assessment | HUD | 2023 | 68,000-unit housing shortage |
| Tribal Energy Resources | DOE/EIA | 2023 | 5.4% of U.S. renewable potential on tribal lands |
