# Natural Resources: Resources

## Overview

This document provides curated resources for understanding tribal natural resource issues, including academic research, government reports, legal databases, books, organizations, and data sources. Resources are organized by type and cover the full range of tribal natural resource topics: water rights, energy and minerals, forestry, fish and wildlife, environmental justice, climate change, and trust management.
