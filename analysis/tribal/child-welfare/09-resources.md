# Tribal Child Welfare: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding tribal child welfare policy, the Indian Child Welfare Act, and the systems affecting Native children and families. Resources are organized by type and annotated with relevance to specific aspects of the issue.

### Key Court Decisions

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|-------------|
| *Haaland v. Brackeen* | 599 U.S. 255 (2023) | Upheld ICWA constitutionality; political classification, not racial |
| *Mississippi Band of Choctaw Indians v. Holyfield* | 490 U.S. 30 (1989) | Broad interpretation of ICWA jurisdiction |
| *Adoptive Couple v. Baby Girl* | 570 U.S. 637 (2013) | Narrowed ICWA for non-custodial parent cases |
| *Morton v. Mancari* | 417 U.S. 535 (1974) | Indian preference is political, not racial classification |
