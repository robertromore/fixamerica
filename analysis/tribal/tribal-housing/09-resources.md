# Tribal Housing: Resources

## Overview

This document provides curated resources for understanding tribal housing issues, including academic research, government reports, legal references, books, organizations, and data sources.

| NAHASDA Title VI | 25 U.S.C. 4191 et seq. | Federal loan guarantees for TDHEs |
| Section 184 Indian Home Loan Guarantee | 12 U.S.C. 1715z-13a | HUD mortgage guarantee for Native borrowers |
| Indian Reorganization Act | 25 U.S.C. 5101 et seq. | Trust land framework affecting housing |
| HEARTH Act | P.L. 112-151 | Tribal leasing authority without BIA approval |
