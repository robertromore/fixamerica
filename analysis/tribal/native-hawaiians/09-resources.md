# Native Hawaiians: Resources

## Government Sources

### Federal

- **Department of the Interior**: Native Hawaiian relations, 2016 rule (43 C.F.R. Part 50) -- <https://www.doi.gov/hawaiian>
- **Apology Resolution**: P.L. 103-150 (1993) -- Full text available through Congress.gov
- **Native Hawaiian Health Care Improvement Act**: P.L. 100-579 (1988)
- **Hawaiian Homes Commission Act**: 42 Stat. 108 (1921)
- **Congressional Research Service**: "The Political Status of Native Hawaiians" (updated periodically)
- **Government Accountability Office**: Reports on DHHL administration and Native Hawaiian programs
- **U.S. Census Bureau**: Native Hawaiian and Other Pacific Islander population data

### State of Hawaii

- **Office of Hawaiian Affairs (OHA)**: <https://www.oha.org> -- Publications, data, advocacy
- **Department of Hawaiian Home Lands (DHHL)**: <https://dhhl.hawaii.gov> -- Waitlist data, annual reports, land inventory
- **Hawaii State Legislature**: Recognition-related bills and resolutions
- **Hawaii DOH**: Native Hawaiian health data and reports

## Legal Sources

### Landmark Cases

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|-------------|
| *Rice v. Cayetano* | 528 U.S. 495 (2000) | Struck down Hawaiian-only OHA voting; raised political vs. racial classification question |
| *Morton v. Mancari* | 417 U.S. 535 (1974) | Indian-specific programs are political, not racial (not yet formally extended to Hawaiians) |
| *Haaland v. Brackeen* | 599 U.S. 255 (2023) | Reaffirmed political classification of Indian status; potential support for Hawaiian classification |
| *Doe v. Kamehameha Schools* | 470 F.3d 827 (9th Cir. 2006) (en banc) | Upheld Hawaiian-preference admissions as remedial |
| *Hawaii v. Office of Hawaiian Affairs* | 556 U.S. 163 (2009) | Limited scope of Apology Resolution regarding ceded lands claims |
| *Arakaki v. Lingle* | 477 F.3d 1048 (9th Cir. 2007) | Upheld constitutionality of OHA and DHHL programs |

### Key Statutes

- Hawaiian Homes Commission Act (1921): 42 Stat. 108
- Hawaii Admission Act (1959): P.L. 86-3
- Apology Resolution (1993): P.L. 103-150
- Native Hawaiian Health Care Improvement Act (1988): P.L. 100-579
- Native Hawaiian Education Act: Title VI, Part B of ESEA
- NAHASDA (amended for Native Hawaiians): 25 U.S.C. Section 4221 et seq.

### International Law

- United Nations Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples (UNDRIP), 2007
- International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights, Article 1 (self-determination)
- UN General Assembly Resolution 1514 (XV), Declaration on the Granting of Independence to Colonial Countries and Peoples (1960)
- Permanent Court of Arbitration: Hawaiian Kingdom arbitration materials
