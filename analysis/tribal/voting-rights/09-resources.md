# Native American Voting Rights: Resources

## Government Sources

### Federal Agencies

- **Department of Justice, Voting Section**: <https://www.justice.gov/crt/voting-section> -- VRA enforcement, Section 2 and Section 203 compliance, federal observer program
- **Election Assistance Commission**: <https://www.eac.gov> -- Election administration guidance, HAVA funding, voter registration standards
- **U.S. Commission on Civil Rights**: *An Assessment of Minority Voting Rights Access in the United States* (2018); *Broken Promises: Continuing Federal Funding Shortfall for Native Americans* (2018)
- **Government Accountability Office**: GAO-14-556, "Elections: Issues Related to State Voter Identification Laws" (2014)
- **Census Bureau**: Section 203 language minority coverage determinations; American Indian and Alaska Native population data

### Congressional Materials

- **Senate Committee on Indian Affairs**: Hearing records on NAVRA and Native voter access
- **Senate and House Judiciary Committees**: Hearing records on VRA reauthorization and the John R. Lewis Act
- **Congressional Research Service**: "The Voting Rights Act of 1965: Background and Overview" (updated periodically); "Native American Voting Rights" (2020)

### Statutes and Regulations

- **Voting Rights Act of 1965**: 52 U.S.C. Sections 10301-10314 (formerly 42 U.S.C. Sections 1973 et seq.)
- **Section 2 (vote dilution and discrimination)**: 52 U.S.C. Section 10301
- **Section 203 (language minority provisions)**: 52 U.S.C. Section 10503
- **Indian Citizenship Act of 1924**: 8 Stat. 253
- **Help America Vote Act of 2002**: 52 U.S.C. Sections 20901-21145
- **National Voter Registration Act of 1993**: 52 U.S.C. Sections 20501-20511

## Legal Sources

### Landmark Cases

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|-------------|
| *Elk v. Wilkins* | 112 U.S. 94 (1884) | Native Americans not citizens under Fourteenth Amendment |
| *Harrison v. Laveen* | 67 Ariz. 337 (1948) | Arizona reservation residents entitled to vote |
| *Thornburg v. Gingles* | 478 U.S. 30 (1986) | Established three preconditions for Section 2 vote dilution claims |
| *Windy Boy v. Big Horn County* | 647 F. Supp. 1002 (D. Mont. 1986) | First major Native VRA Section 2 victory; at-large elections invalidated |
| *Buckanaga v. Sisseton School District* | 804 F.2d 469 (8th Cir. 1986) | At-large school board elections diluting Native vote |
| *United States v. Blaine County* | 363 F.3d 897 (9th Cir. 2004) | County at-large elections violating Section 2 |
| *Bone Shirt v. Hazeltine* | 461 F.3d 1011 (8th Cir. 2006) | South Dakota redistricting diluting Native vote |
| *Shelby County v. Holder* | 570 U.S. 529 (2013) | Section 4(b) coverage formula struck down; preclearance effectively eliminated |
| *Spirit Lake Tribe v. Benson County* | No. 2:10-cv-095 (D.N.D.) | Challenge to North Dakota voter ID law |
| *Brakebill v. Jaeger* | 932 F.3d 671 (8th Cir. 2019) | Ongoing challenge to North Dakota voter ID law as applied to reservation residents |
| *Navajo Nation v. San Juan County* | 929 F.3d 1270 (10th Cir. 2019) | At-large county elections diluting Navajo voting strength |
| *Brnovich v. DNC* | 594 U.S. 647 (2021) | Section 2 enforcement standards tightened; Arizona ballot collection ban upheld |

### Key Legal Concepts

- **Gingles Preconditions**: Three requirements for a Section 2 vote dilution claim: (1) the minority group is sufficiently large and geographically compact to constitute a majority in a single-member district; (2) the minority group is politically cohesive; (3) the white majority votes as a bloc to usually defeat the minority's preferred candidates
- **Section 2 "Results" Test**: Plaintiffs must show that a voting practice "results in" a denial or abridgment of the right to vote; discriminatory intent is not required
- **Section 203 Triggering**: A jurisdiction is covered when a language minority group exceeds 5% of voting-age citizens or 10,000 persons and the illiteracy rate exceeds the national rate

## Data Sources

- **U.S. Census Bureau**: American Indian and Alaska Native population data; Section 203 coverage determinations; American Community Survey
- **Native American Rights Fund**: Native American Voting Rights database tracking Section 2 litigation outcomes
- **Election Administration and Voting Survey (EAVS)**: Biennial data on election administration practices by jurisdiction
- **Cooperative Election Study (CES)**: Academic survey data on voter registration and turnout, including AI/AN oversamples in some years
- **State election offices**: Voter registration and turnout data (most states do not track by race; estimates require ecological inference or survey data)
- **Bureau of Indian Affairs**: Tribal directory, reservation land data, road infrastructure data
