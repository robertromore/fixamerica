# Language Preservation: Resources

## Key Federal Statutes

- **Native American Languages Act** (NALA), 25 U.S.C. 2901-2906 (1990)
- **Esther Martinez Native American Languages Programs Reauthorization Act**, P.L. 116-101 (2019; originally P.L. 109-394, 2006)
- **Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act**, P.L. 93-638 (1975)
- **Every Student Succeeds Act** (ESSA), P.L. 114-95 (2015), Title VI, Part A (Indian Education)
- **American Indian Religious Freedom Act**, 42 U.S.C. 1996 (1978)
- **Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act** (NAGPRA), 25 U.S.C. 3001 (1990)

## Key Court Cases

- *Haaland v. Brackeen*, 599 U.S. ___ (2023) -- Upheld Indian Child Welfare Act; affirmed Congress's authority under Indian Commerce Clause, relevant to federal power over Indigenous cultural programs
- *Morton v. Mancari*, 417 U.S. 535 (1974) -- Upheld hiring preferences for Indians in the BIA; established that classifications based on tribal membership are political, not racial, supporting differential treatment for language programs
