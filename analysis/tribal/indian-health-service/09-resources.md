# Indian Health Service: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key academic research, government reports, data sources, organizations, and legal authorities relevant to Indian Health Service reform and AI/AN health equity.

## Academic Research

### Health Disparities

- Espey, David K., et al. "Leading Causes of Death and All-Cause Mortality in American Indians and Alaska Natives." *American Journal of Public Health* 104, Suppl. 3 (2014): S303-S311.

- Gone, Joseph P., and Joseph E. Trimble. "American Indian and Alaska Native Mental Health: Diverse Perspectives on Enduring Disparities." *Annual Review of Clinical Psychology* 8 (2012): 131-160.

- Warne, Donald, and Siobhan Wescott. "Social Determinants of American Indian Nutritional Health." *Current Developments in Nutrition* 3, Suppl. 2 (2019): 12-18.

- Brockie, Teresa N., et al. "A Framework to Examine the Role of Epigenetics in Health Disparities among Native Americans." *Nursing Research and Practice* 2013: 1-9.

### Historical Trauma

- Brave Heart, Maria Yellow Horse, and Lemyra DeBruyn. "The American Indian Holocaust: Healing Historical Unresolved Grief." *American Indian and Alaska Native Mental Health Research* 8, no. 2 (1998): 56-78.

- Ehlers, Cindy L., et al. "Measuring Historical Trauma in an American Indian Community Sample: Contributions of Substance Dependence, Affective Disorder, Conduct Disorder and PTSD." *Drug and Alcohol Dependence* 133, no. 1 (2013): 180-187.

- Heart, Maria Yellow Horse Brave, et al. "Historical Trauma among Indigenous Peoples of the Americas: Concepts, Research, and Clinical Considerations." *Journal of Psychoactive Drugs* 43, no. 4 (2011): 282-290.

### Self-Determination and Health Outcomes

- Cornell, Stephen, and Joseph P. Kalt. "American Indian Self-Determination: The Political Economy of a Successful Policy." Harvard Project on American Indian Economic Development, 2010.

- Warne, Donald, and Linda Bane Frizzell. "American Indian Health Policy: Historical Trends and Contemporary Issues." *American Journal of Public Health* 104, Suppl. 3 (2014): S263-S267.

- Sequist, Thomas D., et al. "Trends in Quality of Care and Barriers to Improvement in the Indian Health Service." *Journal of General Internal Medicine* 26 (2011): 480-486.

### Behavioral Health

- Cwik, Mary F., et al. "Decreases in Suicide Deaths and Attempts Linked to the White Mountain Apache Suicide Surveillance and Prevention System, 2001-2012." *American Journal of Public Health* 106, no. 12 (2016): 2183-2189.

- Greenfield, Brenna L., et al. "Cultural Identity, Discrimination, and Psychological Well-Being in a First Nations Community." *Cultural Diversity and Ethnic Minority Psychology* 23, no. 1 (2017): 87-97.

## Government Reports

- U.S. Commission on Civil Rights. *Broken Promises: Continuing Federal Funding Shortfall for Native Americans*. 2018.

- Congressional Research Service. "The Indian Health Service (IHS): An Overview." R43330, updated 2024.

- Congressional Research Service. "Indian Health Service: Advance Appropriations." IF12187, 2023.

- Congressional Research Service. "The Special Diabetes Program for Indians." IF12361, 2023.

- Senate Committee on Indian Affairs. "In Critical Condition: The Urgent Need to Reform the Indian Health Service's Aberdeen Area." 2010.

### Executive Branch

- Government Accountability Office. "Indian Health Service: Agency Faces Ongoing Challenges Filling Provider Vacancies." GAO-18-580, 2018.

- Government Accountability Office. "Indian Health Service: Spending Levels and Characteristics of IHS and Three Other Federal Health Care Programs." GAO-19-74R, 2019.

- Government Accountability Office. "Indian Health Service: Actions Needed to Improve Oversight of Quality of Care." GAO-17-181, 2017.

- HHS Office of Inspector General. Various reports on IHS financial management, patient safety, and program integrity.

- Department of the Interior. *Federal Indian Boarding School Initiative Investigative Report*. Volume 1, 2022.

### IHS Publications

- Indian Health Service. *Indian Health Disparities Factsheet*. Updated annually.

- Indian Health Service. *IHS Profile*. Updated annually.

- Indian Health Service. *Trends in Indian Health*. Published periodically (last comprehensive edition: 2014; selected updates ongoing).

## Books for General Audience

- Warne, Donald. *The Indian Health Service and Traditional Indian Medicine*. In various edited volumes on Indigenous health.

- Jones, David S. *Rationalizing Epidemics: Meanings and Uses of American Indian Mortality since 1600*. Harvard University Press, 2004.

- Leal, Fermin. *What Ails Indian Health Care*. Investigative journalism series, various publications.

- Smith, Andrea. *Conquest: Sexual Violence and American Indian Genocide*. Duke University Press, 2015. (Chapter on health system impacts)

- Treuer, David. *The Heartbeat of Wounded Knee*. Riverhead Books, 2019. (Chapter on health and social conditions)
