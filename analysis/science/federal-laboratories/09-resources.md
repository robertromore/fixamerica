# Federal Laboratories: Resources

## Key Reports and Studies

### Government Reports

- **National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine.** *Securing Advanced Manufacturing in the United States: The Role of Manufacturing USA.* Washington, DC: The National Academies Press, 2022. <https://doi.org/10.17226/26547>

- **National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine.** *An Assessment of the Need for Native Seeds and the Capacity for Their Supply.* Washington, DC: The National Academies Press, 2023.

- **Secretary of Energy Advisory Board (SEAB).** *Task Force on the DOE National Laboratories.* Multiple reports, 2014-2015. Recommended governance reforms and reduced DOE oversight.

- **Galvin Commission.** *Alternative Futures for the Department of Energy National Laboratories.* 1995. Landmark review recommending fundamental lab restructuring.

- **Commission on Maintaining United States Nuclear Weapons Expertise (Chiles Commission).** Final report, 1999. Assessed weapons lab workforce challenges.

- **Government Accountability Office (GAO).** *Department of Energy: Actions Needed to Strengthen Oversight of Contractor Costs and Fees.* GAO-23-105398, 2023.

- **Government Accountability Office (GAO).** *National Laboratories: DOE Needs to Improve Oversight and Coordination of Contractor Travel.* GAO-19-178, 2019.

- **Department of Energy Inspector General.** Multiple reports on M&O contract management, technology transfer, and laboratory oversight. Available at: <https://www.energy.gov/ig/reports>

- **Congressional Research Service.** *DOE National Laboratories: Overview and Key Issues.* Updated regularly. Available at: <https://crsreports.congress.gov>

- **Congressional Research Service.** *Federal Research and Development (R&D) Funding: FY2025.* Available at: <https://crsreports.congress.gov>

- **National Science Board.** *Science and Engineering Indicators.* Published biennially. Comprehensive data on U.S. R&D including federal laboratory metrics. Available at: <https://ncses.nsf.gov/indicators>

### Academic and Think Tank Studies

- Westwick, Peter J. *The National Labs: Science in an American System, 1947-2022.* Cambridge, MA: Harvard University Press, 2003. Definitive history of the national laboratory system.

- Crow, Michael M. and Barry Bozeman. *Limited by Design: R&D Laboratories in the U.S. National Innovation System.* New York: Columbia University Press, 1998.

- Anadon, Laura Diaz et al. "Making Technological Innovation Work for Sustainable Development." *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences* 113, no. 35 (2016): 9682-9690.

- Bonvillian, William B. and Charles Weiss. *Technological Innovation in Legacy Sectors.* New York: Oxford University Press, 2015.

- Bonvillian, William B. "The New Model Innovation Agencies: An Overview." *Science and Public Policy* 41, no. 4 (2014): 425-437.

- Mowery, David C. and Bhaven N. Sampat. "Universities in National Innovation Systems." In *The Oxford Handbook of Innovation*, edited by Jan Fagerberg, David C. Mowery, and Richard R. Nelson, 209-239. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2005.

- Information Technology and Innovation Foundation (ITIF). *Turning the Page: Reimagining the National Labs in the 21st Century Innovation Economy.* Multiple reports, 2013-2024. Available at: <https://itif.org>

- Brookings Institution. *Blueprint for the Future: Framing the Issues of Women in Science in a Global Context.* 2019.

## Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Relevance |
|-----|----------|-----------|
| Atomic Energy Act of 1946 | Pub. L. 79-585 | Established civilian control of nuclear research and labs |
| Atomic Energy Act of 1954 | 42 U.S.C. 2011 et seq. | Current framework for nuclear energy and weapons programs |
| National Science Foundation Act of 1950 | 42 U.S.C. 1861 et seq. | Established NSF; complementary to lab system |
| Stevenson-Wydler Technology Innovation Act of 1980 | 15 U.S.C. 3710 | Mandated technology transfer as a federal lab mission |
| Bayh-Dole Act of 1980 | 35 U.S.C. 200-212 | Allowed labs and universities to patent federally funded inventions |
| Federal Technology Transfer Act of 1986 | 15 U.S.C. 3710a | Authorized CRADAs for technology transfer |
| National Competitiveness Technology Transfer Act of 1989 | 15 U.S.C. 3710a note | Extended CRADA authority to GOCO labs |
| Energy Policy Act of 2005 | Pub. L. 109-58 | Authorized DOE research programs and lab activities |
| NNSA Act of 2000 | 50 U.S.C. 2401 et seq. | Created NNSA as semi-autonomous agency within DOE |
| America COMPETES Act of 2007 | Pub. L. 110-69 | Authorized increases in basic research funding |
| CHIPS and Science Act of 2022 | Pub. L. 117-167 | Authorized major science funding increases; new DOE programs |
| Inflation Reduction Act of 2022 | Pub. L. 117-169 | Directed clean energy funding to DOE labs |
| Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act of 2021 | Pub. L. 117-58 | Included DOE facility upgrade funding |
