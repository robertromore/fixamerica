## Legal References

### Key Court Cases

- *Edwards v. Aguillard*, 482 U.S. 578 (1987) -- Struck down Louisiana's Balanced Treatment Act requiring creationism alongside evolution
- *Kitzmiller v. Dover Area School District*, 400 F. Supp. 2d 707 (M.D. Pa. 2005) -- Ruled intelligent design is not science and cannot be required in public school science classes
- *Epperson v. Arkansas*, 393 U.S. 97 (1968) -- Struck down Arkansas's ban on teaching evolution
- *McLean v. Arkansas Board of Education*, 529 F. Supp. 1255 (E.D. Ark. 1982) -- Struck down Arkansas's Balanced Treatment Act
- *Freiler v. Tangipahoa Parish Board of Education*, 185 F.3d 337 (5th Cir. 1999) -- Struck down disclaimer on evolution instruction

### Federal Statutes

- Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA), 20 U.S.C. ch. 70
- National Defense Education Act of 1958, Pub. L. 85-864
- America COMPETES Act, 42 U.S.C. ch. 79
- CHIPS and Science Act of 2022, Pub. L. 117-167

### State Legislation

- Tennessee Science Education Act (2012), Tenn. Code Ann. 49-6-1030

- Mississippi Science Education Act (2006), Miss. Code Ann. 37-11-77
