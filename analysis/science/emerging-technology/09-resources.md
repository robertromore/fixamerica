## Court Cases

- *Diamond v. Chakrabarty*, 447 U.S. 303 (1980) - Patentability of living organisms
- *Association for Molecular Pathology v. Myriad Genetics*, 569 U.S. 576 (2013) - Limits on gene patents
- *Alice Corp. v. CLS Bank International*, 573 U.S. 208 (2014) - Software patent limitations
- *Stanford v. Roche*, 563 U.S. 776 (2011) - Bayh-Dole does not automatically vest patent title in universities
- *Thaler v. Vidal*, 43 F.4th 1207 (Fed. Cir. 2022) - AI cannot be an "inventor" under patent law
