# Research Infrastructure: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key organizations, reports, data sources, and further reading on U.S. research infrastructure policy. Resources are organized by category for researchers, advocates, policymakers, and citizens seeking to understand and engage with research infrastructure issues.

## Relevant Legislation

### Current Law

| Law | Citation | Relevance |
|-----|----------|-----------|
| CHIPS and Science Act | Pub. L. 117-167 (2022) | Authorized major NSF and DOE infrastructure investment |
| Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act | Pub. L. 117-58 (2021) | DOE demonstration projects; lab modernization |
| DOE Organization Act | 42 U.S.C. ch. 84 | Establishes DOE and national laboratory system |
| National Science Foundation Act | 42 U.S.C. ch. 16 | Establishes NSF and authorizes infrastructure programs |
| America COMPETES Act (reauthorized) | Pub. L. 110-69 (2007) | R&D investment authorization |
| Stevenson-Wydler Technology Innovation Act | 15 U.S.C. ch. 63 | Technology transfer from federal laboratories |

### Historical Legislation

| Law | Year | Significance |
|-----|------|--------------|
| Atomic Energy Act | 1946 | Created the national laboratory system |
| National Defense Education Act | 1958 | Funded university science facilities |
| Higher Education Facilities Act | 1963 | Construction grants for university buildings |
| National Science Foundation Authorization Act | Various | MREFC account creation and reauthorization |
| American Recovery and Reinvestment Act | 2009 | One-time research infrastructure surge |
