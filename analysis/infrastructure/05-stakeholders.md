# Infrastructure Policy: Stakeholders

## Overview

Infrastructure policy involves a complex web of stakeholders across government, industry, labor, advocacy, and the public. Understanding their interests, power, and relationships is essential for building coalitions and advancing reform.

## Government Stakeholders

### Federal Agencies

| Agency | Role | Interest |
|--------|------|----------|
| **DOT** | Surface transportation, aviation, rail | Program administration |
| **EPA** | Water, environmental review | Standards, funding |
| **FCC** | Broadband, spectrum | Deployment, competition |
| **Army Corps** | Waterways, dams, flood control | Project authority |
| **FEMA** | Resilience, disaster recovery | Mitigation investment |
| **OMB** | Budget, cost-benefit | Fiscal constraint |

### Congressional Committees

| Committee | Jurisdiction |
|-----------|--------------|

## Power Analysis

### High Power, High Interest

| Stakeholder | Leverage |
|-------------|----------|
| **State DOTs** | Implementation control |
| **Construction industry** | Political donations, lobbying |
| **Congressional committees** | Legislative authority |
| **Large ISPs** | Market control, lobbying |

### High Power, Lower Interest

| Stakeholder | Occasional Engagement |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **President** | Priority setting |
| **General public** | Votes, but low attention |
| **Financial sector** | Bond markets |

### Low Power, High Interest

| Stakeholder | Needs Amplification |
|-------------|---------------------|
| **Transit riders** | Service dependent |
| **Rural communities** | Underserved |
| **Environmental justice** | Most impacted |
| **Future generations** | No voice |

## Coalition Dynamics

|-----------|---------|

### Tension Points

| Tension | Parties |
|---------|---------|
| **New vs. repair** | Contractors vs. maintenance advocates |
| **Roads vs. transit** | Highway lobby vs. transit advocates |
| **Federal vs. state** | National standards vs. flexibility |
| **Public vs. private** | Unions vs. privatization advocates |
| **Speed vs. review** | Industry vs. environmental groups |
