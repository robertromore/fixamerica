# Broadband Infrastructure: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can advance universal, affordable broadband through civic engagement, community organizing, and personal advocacy. While broadband policy can seem technical, public pressure shapes funding decisions, regulatory outcomes, and local investment. This guide provides concrete actions for different situations and commitment levels.
