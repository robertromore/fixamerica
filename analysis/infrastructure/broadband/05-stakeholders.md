# Broadband Infrastructure: Stakeholders

## Overview

Broadband policy involves powerful incumbent providers seeking to maintain market dominance, communities demanding service, advocates pushing for affordability and competition, and government agencies attempting to close the digital divide. Understanding these stakeholders and their interests is essential for building coalitions and navigating opposition to reform.
