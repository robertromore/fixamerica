# Broadband Infrastructure: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Closing the digital divide requires sustained action across infrastructure deployment, affordability, competition, and digital equity. This roadmap aligns BEAD implementation, regulatory reform, and state/local action to achieve universal, affordable broadband within a decade.
