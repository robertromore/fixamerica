# Rail Infrastructure: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming American rail requires sustained investment over decades, with near-term wins building momentum for long-term transformation. This roadmap aligns federal authorization cycles, IIJA implementation, and major project timelines to modernize the system.
