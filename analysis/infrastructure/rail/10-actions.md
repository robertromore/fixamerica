# Rail Infrastructure: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can improve rail infrastructure through advocacy, civic engagement, and community organizing. While rail policy may seem distant from daily life, it affects travel options, freight costs, safety, and environmental quality. This guide provides concrete actions for different situations and commitment levels.
