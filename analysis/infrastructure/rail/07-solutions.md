# Rail Infrastructure: Solutions

## Overview

Strengthening American rail requires distinct strategies for freight and passenger systems: maintaining freight rail's private investment model while dramatically increasing public investment in passenger infrastructure. Solutions span funding reform, infrastructure investment, operational improvements, and governance restructuring.
