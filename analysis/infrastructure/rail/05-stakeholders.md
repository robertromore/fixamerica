# Rail Infrastructure: Stakeholders

## Overview

Rail policy involves a complex web of stakeholders with sometimes aligned and sometimes conflicting interests. Understanding these relationships is essential for building coalitions and navigating reform.

## Government Stakeholders

### Federal Agencies

| Agency | Role | Priorities |
|--------|------|------------|
| **Federal Railroad Administration** | Safety, grants, standards | Safety, technology, NEC |
| **Surface Transportation Board** | Economic regulation, disputes | Competition, access, rates |
| **Department of Transportation** | Policy, budget | Modal balance, IIJA implementation |
| **Amtrak Board** | Oversight | Service, financial performance |
| **Federal Transit Administration** | Commuter rail grants | Urban transit |

## Industry Stakeholders

### Class I Railroads

| Railroad | Key Interests |
|----------|---------------|
| **BNSF** | Freight efficiency, limited passenger |
| **Union Pacific** | Western routes, intermodal |
| **Norfolk Southern** | Eastern network, safety after derailments |
| **CSX** | Eastern routes, intermodal growth |
| **Canadian railroads** | North American network |

**Common positions**:

- Protect freight capacity and schedules
- Limit unfunded mandates
- Support safety investment (on their terms)
- Skeptical of passenger expansion on their tracks

### Amtrak

| Interest | Description |
|----------|-------------|

| **NEC investment** | Modernize core asset |
| **Fleet renewal** | Replace aging equipment |
| **Expansion** | New corridors and frequencies |
| **Operating flexibility** | Reform constraints |

## Coalition Opportunities

### Potential Alliances

| Coalition | Common Interest |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Environmental + rail** | Climate, mode shift |
| **Business + rail** | Economic development |
| **Labor + safety advocates** | Worker protection |
| **Urban + suburban** | Commuter service |
| **Agricultural + waterway + rail** | Multimodal freight |

### Barriers to Coalition

| Barrier | Parties |
|---------|---------|
| **Crew size** | Railroads vs. labor |
| **Access/rates** | Shippers vs. Class I |
| **Track priority** | Amtrak vs. freight |
| **Route choices** | HSR supporters vs. NIMBY |
