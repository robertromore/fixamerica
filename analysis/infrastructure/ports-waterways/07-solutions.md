# Ports and Waterways: Solutions

## Overview

Strengthening maritime infrastructure requires increased investment in locks and channels, port capacity expansion, intermodal improvements, climate adaptation, and operational reforms. Solutions must address both immediate capacity needs and long-term resilience while balancing economic, environmental, and community interests.
