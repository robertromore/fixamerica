# Ports and Waterways: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can improve maritime infrastructure through civic engagement, advocacy, and community organizing. While ports and waterways may seem distant from daily life, they affect prices, supply chains, local communities, and the environment. This guide provides concrete actions for different situations and commitment levels.
