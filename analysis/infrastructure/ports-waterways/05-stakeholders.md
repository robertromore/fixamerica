# Ports and Waterways: Stakeholders

## Overview

Maritime infrastructure involves diverse stakeholders across federal and local government, private industry, labor, and communities. Shipping lines, port authorities, the Army Corps of Engineers, shippers, and unions each have distinct interests that shape infrastructure decisions. Understanding these dynamics is essential for building coalitions and advancing investment.
