# Ports and Waterways: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming maritime infrastructure requires sustained federal investment, process reform, climate adaptation, and operational improvements. This roadmap aligns WRDA authorization cycles, federal appropriations, and port authority action to modernize the system over the next decade.
