# Roads and Bridges: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming America's road and bridge infrastructure requires sustained action across funding, maintenance, safety, and resilience. This roadmap aligns federal reauthorization cycles, state implementation, and local action to achieve measurable improvements in condition, safety, and equity over the next decade.
