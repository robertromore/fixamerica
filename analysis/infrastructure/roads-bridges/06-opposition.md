# Roads and Bridges: Opposition

## Overview

Opposition to road and bridge reform comes from those protecting current funding structures, resisting design changes, opposing environmental requirements, or defending construction-focused spending. Understanding opposition arguments and interests is essential for building effective coalitions and crafting politically viable reforms.

## Opposition Tactics

### Legislative Tactics

| Tactic | Example |
|--------|---------|

### Regulatory Tactics

| Tactic | Example |
