# Roads and Bridges: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can improve road and bridge infrastructure through civic engagement, advocacy, and community organizing. While infrastructure may seem like a technical domain, public pressure shapes funding decisions, design choices, and safety priorities. This guide provides concrete actions for different situations and commitment levels.
