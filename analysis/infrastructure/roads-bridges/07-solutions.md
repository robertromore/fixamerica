# Roads and Bridges: Solutions

## Overview

Addressing America's road and bridge challenges requires sustainable funding, prioritizing maintenance over new construction, redesigning for safety, building climate resilience, and ensuring equitable outcomes. Solutions must work within political constraints while fundamentally reforming the system that created current problems.
