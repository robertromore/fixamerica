# Dams and Levees: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Addressing America's dam and levee infrastructure requires a multi-decade effort spanning federal, state, and private investment. This roadmap aligns WRDA authorization cycles, appropriations, and state action to systematically reduce risk while adapting to climate change.

### Assessment and Inventory

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Complete national dam inventory | Year 1-2 | FEMA/States |
| Complete levee inventory | Year 1-2 | USACE |

| Emergency action plan coverage | Year 1-2 | Dam owners |
