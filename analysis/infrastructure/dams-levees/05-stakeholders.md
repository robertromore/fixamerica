# Dams and Levees: Stakeholders

## Overview

Dam and levee policy involves a complex web of stakeholders with diverse and sometimes conflicting interests. Understanding these relationships is essential for building coalitions and navigating reform.

## Government Stakeholders

### Federal Agencies

| Agency | Role | Interest |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Army Corps of Engineers** | Flood control, navigation dams | Infrastructure funding, mission |
| **Bureau of Reclamation** | Western water supply, irrigation | Water delivery, hydropower |
| **FEMA** | Dam/levee safety, flood insurance | Risk reduction, mapping |
| **FERC** | Hydropower licensing | Safety, energy production |
| **Tennessee Valley Authority** | Regional dam system | Integrated management |
| **National Park Service** | Dam removal on park lands | Ecosystem restoration |
| **Fish and Wildlife Service** | Fish passage | Endangered species |
| **EPA** | Water quality | Environmental compliance |
