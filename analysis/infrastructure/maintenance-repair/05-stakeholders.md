# Infrastructure Maintenance and Repair: Stakeholders

## Overview

Infrastructure maintenance involves diverse stakeholders with different interests, resources, and influence. Understanding these stakeholders is essential for building coalitions for reform and addressing opposition. Key divisions exist between federal and local governments, public and private interests, and construction versus maintenance priorities.

## Stakeholder Alignment

### Support for Maintenance Investment

| Strong Support | Moderate Support | Conditional |
|----------------|------------------|-------------|
| Infrastructure advocacy | Construction industry | Fiscal conservatives |
| Unions | Engineering firms | Some environmental |

| Local governments | User groups | Political leadership |

### Key Conflicts

| Conflict | Parties | Issue |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **New vs. maintenance** | Industry vs. advocates | Spending allocation |
| **User fees** | Users vs. needed revenue | Funding mechanism |
| **Contracting** | Unions vs. governments | Service delivery |
| **Regulation** | Industry vs. government | Requirements burden |
