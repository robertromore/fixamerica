# Aviation Infrastructure: Solutions

## Overview

Modernizing aviation infrastructure requires increased investment through funding reform, accelerated ATC modernization, capacity expansion where needed, sustainability transformation, and improved regional connectivity. Solutions must balance airline, airport, community, and passenger interests while positioning the system for future demands.
