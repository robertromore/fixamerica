# Aviation Infrastructure: Root Causes

## Overview

Aviation infrastructure challenges stem from funding constraints that limit investment, political dynamics that delay modernization, fragmented decision-making across thousands of airports, and resistance to change from incumbent interests. Understanding these root causes is essential for designing effective reforms.

## Funding Structure Problems

### PFC Cap Erosion

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Cap frozen since 2000** | $4.50 unchanged |
| **Inflation erosion** | Worth ~$2.50 in 2000 dollars |
| **Airline opposition** | Block increases |
| **Purchasing power** | 50% decline |
| **Investment shortfall** | $1+ billion/year lost |
