# Aviation Infrastructure: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can improve aviation infrastructure through civic engagement, advocacy, and community organizing. While aviation may seem like a specialized domain, public input shapes funding decisions, expansion plans, and community impacts. This guide provides concrete actions for different situations and commitment levels.
