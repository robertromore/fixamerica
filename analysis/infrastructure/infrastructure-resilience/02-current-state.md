# Infrastructure Resilience: Current State

## Overview

American infrastructure faces mounting resilience challenges as climate change accelerates, cyber threats evolve, and aging systems become increasingly vulnerable. Current protection levels vary widely by sector, region, and owner, with significant gaps in preparedness for future conditions.
