# Infrastructure Resilience: Solutions

## Overview

Building infrastructure resilience requires a comprehensive approach: updated design standards, increased investment, improved governance, better information, and aligned incentives. Solutions must address the full range of threats while recognizing different sector needs and regional vulnerabilities.
