# Infrastructure Finance: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transitioning to sustainable infrastructure finance requires careful sequencing: near-term reforms to existing mechanisms, medium-term development of new revenue sources, and long-term structural change. This roadmap aligns federal reauthorization cycles with revenue reform.
