# Alzheimer's and Dementia Care

Alzheimer's disease and related dementias represent one of the most significant health challenges facing America's aging population. Over 6.7 million Americans age 65 and older currently live with Alzheimer's disease, a number projected to reach 12.7 million by 2050.
