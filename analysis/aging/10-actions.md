# Aging Policy: Individual Actions

## Overview

Citizens can influence aging policy through personal planning, caregiving, civic engagement, and advocacy. This document outlines concrete steps individuals can take to protect themselves and their families while pushing for systemic reform.

## Personal Planning

### Retirement Security

| Action | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Maximize Social Security** | Delay claiming if possible (benefits increase 8%/year from 62-70) |
| **Employer retirement plans** | Contribute at least to employer match; increase annually |
| **Auto-IRA enrollment** | If employer doesn't offer 401(k), use state auto-IRA if available |
| **Catch-up contributions** | If 50+, contribute additional $7,500/year to 401(k) |
| **Social Security estimate** | Review my Social Security statement annually at ssa.gov |

### Medicare Preparation

| Action | Timeline |
|--------|----------|
| **Initial enrollment** | 3 months before/after 65th birthday |
| **Plan comparison** | Medicare.gov Plan Finder annually |
| **Part D enrollment** | Evaluate drug coverage during open enrollment |
| **Medigap consideration** | Best options within 6 months of Part B enrollment |
| **Preventive services** | Annual wellness visit, screenings |

### Long-Term Care Planning

| Action | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Assess needs** | Consider family history, health status |
| **Evaluate insurance** | LTC insurance, hybrid policies, WA Cares (if in Washington) |
| **Explore alternatives** | HCBS options, aging-in-place modifications |
| **Legal documents** | Advance directive, healthcare proxy, financial POA |
| **Family conversations** | Discuss preferences, resources, expectations |

### Housing Preparation

| Action | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Assess home accessibility** | Evaluate for aging in place |
| **Consider modifications** | Grab bars, ramp access, single-floor living |
| **Research alternatives** | CCRC, assisted living, senior housing options |
| **Evaluate location** | Proximity to health care, transportation, family |

## Caregiving Actions

### If You're a Caregiver

| Action | Resource |
|--------|----------|
| **Assess your own health** | Caregiver stress screenings |
| **Find respite** | ARCH National Respite Network; local AAA |
| **Join support groups** | Alzheimer's Association, Family Caregiver Alliance |
| **Document care hours** | Track for potential Social Security credits (future) |
| **Know your rights** | FMLA eligibility, state family leave |
| **Seek training** | Medicare covers caregiver education for some conditions |

### Supporting Family Caregivers

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Offer specific help** | "I can stay with Mom Tuesday afternoons" |

| **Participate in planning** | Family meetings, shared decision-making |
| **Financial contribution** | Share costs equitably if able |
| **Respite support** | Cover care so primary caregiver can rest |

## Advocacy Actions

### Effective Communication

| Method | Tips |
|--------|------|
| **Phone calls** | Most effective for Congress; brief, specific |
| **Personal stories** | How aging issues affect you and your family |
| **Specific asks** | Name bills, specific reforms |
| **Local connection** | How it affects your community |
| **Follow-up** | Multiple contacts on same issue |

### Sample Messages

**On Social Security:**

> "I urge you to protect Social Security by lifting the payroll tax cap on high earners rather than cutting benefits. Millions of seniors depend on these earned benefits for basic needs."

**On Medicare Expansion:**

> "Please support adding dental, vision, and hearing coverage to Medicare. These are essential health services that seniors need and currently cannot afford."

**On Caregiving:**

> "I urge you to support caregiver tax credits and paid family leave. Family caregivers provide $600 billion in unpaid care annually. We need recognition and support."

**On Nursing Home Quality:**

> "Please support strong nursing home staffing requirements. Understaffing leads to preventable deaths and suffering. Facilities must be held accountable."

**On HCBS:**

> "I support expanding home and community-based services so seniors can age in place with dignity. Please fund HCBS expansion and eliminate waitlists."
