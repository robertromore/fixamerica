# Aging Policy: Political Perspectives Analysis

## Overview

Aging policy touches on fundamental questions about government's role, individual responsibility, and social obligations across generations. Different political perspectives approach these questions from distinct philosophical foundations, leading to varied policy prescriptions for Social Security, Medicare, long-term care, and caregiving support.

This document analyzes aging policy through multiple political perspectives, examining how different ideological frameworks shape views on retirement security, healthcare for seniors, long-term care financing, and support for family caregivers.
