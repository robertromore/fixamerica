### Essential Research

**Prosthetic Technology**

- Hafner, B.J., et al. "Evaluation of Function, Performance, and Preference as Transfemoral Amputees Transition from Mechanical to Microprocessor Control of the Prosthetic Knee." *Archives of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation* 88, no. 2 (2007): 207-217
- Highsmith, M.J., et al. "Safety, Energy Efficiency, and Cost Efficacy of the C-Leg for Transfemoral Amputees." *Journal of Rehabilitation Research and Development* 47, no. 1 (2010): 1-14
- Resnik, L., et al. "Advanced Upper Limb Prosthetic Devices: Implications for Upper Limb Prosthetic Rehabilitation." *Archives of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation* 93, no. 4 (2012): 710-717
- Resnik, L., et al. "The DEKA Arm: Its Features, Functionality, and Evolution During the Veterans Affairs Study to Optimize the DEKA Arm." *Prosthetics and Orthotics International* 38, no. 6 (2014): 492-504

**Osseointegration**

- Branemark, R., et al. "Osseointegrated Percutaneous Prosthetic System (OPPS): 11-Year Follow-Up of First Human Treatment." *Bone and Joint Journal* 101-B, no. 5 (2019): 522-529
- Atallah, R., et al. "Safety and Efficacy of Bone-Anchored Prostheses in Transfemoral Amputees: A Systematic Review." *Journal of Bone and Joint Surgery* 100, no. 15 (2018): e105

**Rehabilitation Outcomes**

- Sporner, M.L., et al. "Quantifying Physical Activity in Veterans with Physical Disabilities." *Disability and Rehabilitation* 31, no. 22 (2009): 1826-1830

**3D Printing**

- Manero, A., et al. "Implementation of 3D Printing Technology in the Field of Prosthetics." *Expert Review of Medical Devices* 16, no. 12 (2019): 1029-1040

**Spinal Cord Injury**

- VA SCI/D System of Care Annual Reports, 2020-2024
- National Spinal Cord Injury Statistical Center, Annual Statistical Report, 2024

**Polytrauma and TBI**

## Educational Resources

### For Veterans and Families

- **Amputee Coalition Resources**: Education library, peer visitor program, support groups (amputee-coalition.org)
- **PVA Publications**: Disability rights, wheelchair access guides, benefits information (pva.org)
- **Wounded Warrior Project Resource Center**: Benefits navigation, physical health programs, peer support (woundedwarriorproject.org)
- **VA Prosthetics Information**: Device options, eligibility, how to request services (va.gov/health/prosthetics)
- **VA Caregiver Support Line**: 1-855-260-3274 for caregivers of veterans with prosthetic needs

### For Providers

- **VA Prosthetics Clinical Practice Guidelines**: Evidence-based prescribing guidelines (healthquality.va.gov)
- **AAOP Education Programs**: Continuing education for prosthetists and orthotists (oandp.org)
- **VA ECHO Prosthetics**: Training connecting community providers with VA prosthetic specialists
- **ACRM Education**: Rehabilitation medicine continuing education (acrm.org)

### For Researchers

- **VA Rehabilitation Research and Development** (rehab.research.va.gov): Funding opportunities, published research, center information
- **VA Center for Limb Loss and MoBility (CLiMB)**: Advanced prosthetic research (limbloss.research.va.gov)
- **NIH National Institute of Biomedical Imaging and Bioengineering**: Prosthetic technology research funding (nibib.nih.gov)
- **DARPA Biological Technologies Office**: Advanced prosthetic and neural interface research (darpa.mil/about-us/offices/bto)
