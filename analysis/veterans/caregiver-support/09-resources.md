## Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Description |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Caregivers and Veterans Omnibus Health Services Act | P.L. 111-163 (2010) | Established PCAFC and PGCSS; originally limited to post-9/11 veterans |
| VA MISSION Act (Section 161) | P.L. 115-182 (2018) | Mandated PCAFC expansion to all eras of service |
| PACT Act | P.L. 117-168 (2022) | Expanded toxic exposure presumptions, increasing PCAFC-eligible population |
| National Defense Authorization Act (FMLA provisions) | Various years | Military caregiver leave provisions |
| Family and Medical Leave Act | 29 U.S.C. Section 2601 et seq. (1993) | General family caregiving leave (limited) |
| Elizabeth Dole Home and Community Based Services Act | Proposed (2024) | Expanded home-based services and caregiver support |
| Caregiver COLA Act | Proposed (2024) | Caregiver-specific cost-of-living adjustment for stipends |
| Military and Veteran Caregiver Services Improvement Act | Proposed (2024) | Social Security credits for caregivers |
