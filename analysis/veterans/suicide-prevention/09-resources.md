## Crisis Resources

- **Veterans Crisis Line**: Dial 988, then press 1 (24/7)
- **Veterans Crisis Line Text**: Text 838255 (24/7)
- **Veterans Crisis Line Chat**: veteranscrisisline.net/get-help-now/chat (24/7)
- **COMPACT Act Emergency Care**: Any emergency department for acute suicidal crisis (no VA enrollment required)
- **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline**: Dial 988 (24/7)
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741 (24/7)
