## Research and Reports

### Essential Government Reports

- **VA Advisory Committee on Women Veterans**. "Report and Recommendations." Published annually. Provides official recommendations to the VA Secretary. Available at va.gov/advisory-committee-on-women-veterans.
- **Government Accountability Office**. "VA Health Care: Actions Needed to Improve Care for Women Veterans." GAO-24-106321, 2024. Comprehensive assessment of gaps in women's VA healthcare.
- **VA National Suicide Prevention Annual Report**. Published annually. Contains gender-disaggregated suicide data. Available at mentalhealth.va.gov/suicide_prevention.
- **DoD Annual Report on Sexual Assault in the Military**. Published annually. Primary data source on MST prevalence and response. Available at sapr.mil.
- **Independent Review Commission on Sexual Assault in the Military**. "Hard Truths and the Duty to Change." 2021. Landmark recommendations that drove military justice reform.
- **HUD Annual Homeless Assessment Report (AHAR)**. Published annually. Includes women veteran homelessness data. Available at huduser.gov.
- **Congressional Research Service**. "Women in the Military: Background and Issues for Congress." Updated regularly. Comprehensive overview of policy issues.

## Tools and Guides

### For Women Veterans

- **VA Women Veterans Health Care Eligibility Fact Sheet** - Explains healthcare eligibility and enrollment. Available at va.gov/womenvet.
- **eBenefits** - Online portal for managing VA benefits. Available at ebenefits.va.gov.
- **VA MST Fact Sheet** - Information on MST-related services and eligibility. Available at mentalhealth.va.gov/msthome.
- **Make the Connection** - Mental health resources and veteran stories. Available at maketheconnection.net.
- **IAVA Rapid Response Referral Program** - Connects veterans to services. Available at iava.org.

### For Advocates and Policymakers

- **GAO Women Veterans Reports** - Searchable database of accountability reports. Available at gao.gov.
- **Congressional Research Service Reports** - Available through member offices or everycrsreport.com.
- **VA Office of Inspector General Reports** - Inspection and audit findings. Available at va.gov/oig.

### For Researchers

- **VA Information Resource Center (VIReC)** - Data access for researchers. Available at virec.research.va.gov.
- **Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research (ICPSR)** - Survey data archives. Available at icpsr.umich.edu.
- **National Archive of Criminal Justice Data** - MST-related criminal justice data. Available at icpsr.umich.edu/icpsrweb/NACJD.
