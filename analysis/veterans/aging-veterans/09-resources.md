## Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Description |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Caregivers and Veterans Omnibus Health Services Act | P.L. 111-163 (2010) | Established PCAFC for post-9/11 veteran caregivers |
| VA MISSION Act | P.L. 115-182 (2018) | Expanded PCAFC to pre-9/11 veterans; community care framework |
| PACT Act | P.L. 117-168 (2022) | Expanded toxic exposure presumptions; Camp Lejeune Justice Act |
| Agent Orange Act | P.L. 102-4 (1991) | Established presumptive service connection for Agent Orange diseases |
| Millennium Health Care Act | P.L. 106-117 (1999) | Required VA long-term care in all regions; authorized adult day health care |
| Elizabeth Dole Home and Community-Based Services Act | Proposed | Expand HCBS and caregiver support (pending 119th Congress) |
| VA Employee Fairness Act | Proposed | Address VA staffing shortages affecting geriatric care |
