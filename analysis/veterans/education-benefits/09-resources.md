# Education Benefits: Resources

## Government Reports and Data Sources

### VA Reports

- **VA Education Service Annual Benefits Report** -- Comprehensive annual data on GI Bill utilization, spending, and program participation. Available at [va.gov/education/](https://www.va.gov/education/)
- **VA GI Bill Comparison Tool** -- School-level data on veteran enrollment, graduation rates, and complaints. Available at [va.gov/gi-bill-comparison-tool/](https://www.va.gov/gi-bill-comparison-tool/)
- **VA GI Bill Feedback System** -- Veteran complaint data by institution. Available at [va.gov/education/submit-school-feedback/](https://www.va.gov/education/submit-school-feedback/)
- **VA STEM Scholarship Dashboard** -- Data on Edith Nourse Rogers STEM Scholarship recipients and outcomes
- **VA VET-TEC Program Reports** -- Enrollment and outcomes data for the Veteran Employment Through Technology Education Courses program
- **VA Yellow Ribbon Program Participating Schools List** -- Updated annually with contribution amounts by institution

### Congressional and Government Oversight

- **GAO Reports on VA Education Benefits** -- Key recent reports:
    - GAO-24-106293, "VA Education Benefits: Actions Needed to Improve Oversight of Schools," 2024
    - GAO-21-452, "Post-9/11 GI Bill: VA Should Strengthen Its Approach for Monitoring Schools," 2021
    - GAO-20-547, "For-Profit Schools: Education Should Strengthen Oversight of Schools Receiving GI Bill Funds," 2020
    - GAO-14-855, "VA Education Benefits: Student Characteristics and Outcomes," 2014
- **Congressional Research Service Reports** -- Regularly updated analyses:
    - "The Post-9/11 Veterans Educational Assistance Act of 2008 (Post-9/11 GI Bill)" (updated annually)
    - "The GI Bill: A Brief History and Issues" (updated periodically)
    - "90/10 Rule: Counting Military and VA Education Benefits as Federal Funds," 2023
    - "GI Bill Housing Allowance: Current Policy and Proposals," 2024
- **VA Office of Inspector General Reports** -- Audits of education benefit delivery:
    - VA OIG Report 22-00145-126, "Education Benefit Payment Accuracy and Timeliness," 2024
    - VA OIG Report 21-02972-231, "GI Bill Overpayments and Debt Collection," 2023
    - VA OIG Report 20-01127-175, "State Approving Agency Oversight of GI Bill Programs," 2022
- **Department of Education Reports**:
    - National Center for Education Statistics (NCES), *Digest of Education Statistics* (annual)
    - Federal Student Aid data on cohort default rates by institution
    - Gainful Employment rule compliance data

### Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Year | Relevance |
|-----|----------|------|-----------|
| Servicemen's Readjustment Act (Original GI Bill) | Public Law 78-346 | 1944 | Created veteran education benefits |
| Montgomery GI Bill | Public Law 98-525 | 1984 | Established Chapter 30 education benefits |
| Post-9/11 Veterans Educational Assistance Act | Public Law 110-252 | 2008 | Created the Post-9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33) |
| Post-9/11 GI Bill Transfer Act | Public Law 111-377 | 2010 | Expanded transferability and Yellow Ribbon |
| Harry W. Colmery Veterans Educational Assistance Act (Forever GI Bill) | Public Law 115-48 | 2017 | Eliminated delimiting dates; expanded benefits |
| Johnny Isakson and David P. Roe Veterans Health Care and Benefits Improvement Act | Public Law 116-315 | 2021 | Strengthened school oversight; veteran protections |
| American Rescue Plan Act (90/10 provision) | Public Law 117-2 | 2021 | Closed GI Bill loophole in 90/10 rule |
