# Veteran Disability Compensation: Resources

## Government Reports and Data Sources

### VA Reports

- **VA Monday Morning Workload Reports** -- Weekly snapshot of pending claims, backlog, and completions. Available at [va.gov/disability/](https://www.va.gov/disability/)
- **VA Benefits Administration Annual Benefits Reports** -- Comprehensive yearly data on claims volume, ratings distribution, compensation spending, and workforce
- **VA Performance and Accountability Reports** -- Annual performance metrics including processing times and accuracy rates
- **VA Systematic Technical Accuracy Review (STAR) Program** -- Internal accuracy review methodology and results
- **VA PACT Act Implementation Dashboard** -- Real-time tracking of PACT Act claims filed, completed, and pending
- **VA Appeals Modernization Act Dashboard** -- Appeals data by review lane (Supplemental Claim, Higher-Level Review, BVA)
- **Board of Veterans' Appeals Annual Reports** -- BVA caseload, decisions, timeliness, and remand rates
- **VA Veteran Experience Office Annual Surveys** -- Veteran satisfaction with claims process, trust in VA, and complaint data
- **VA FY Budget Submissions** -- Annual budget requests detailing compensation spending projections and workforce needs
- **VA Advisory Committee on Disability Compensation Reports** -- Recommendations on rating schedule updates and system modernization (2015, 2018, 2022)

### Congressional and Government Oversight

- **GAO High-Risk List: VA Disability Benefits** -- Updated biennially; tracks progress on claims processing improvements. GAO-23-106203, 2023
- **GAO Reports on VA Claims Processing** -- Multiple reports covering processing delays, consistency, IT modernization, and workforce:
    - GAO-23-106059, "VA Claims Processing: Resource Requirements Assessment," 2023
    - GAO-24-106741, "VA Disability Compensation: Actions Needed to Address Processing Delays," 2024
    - GAO-22-104526, "VA IT Modernization: Actions Needed to Improve Management," 2022
- **Congressional Research Service Reports** -- Regularly updated non-partisan analyses:
    - "Veterans' Disability Compensation: Trends and Policy Options," 2024
    - "VBA Funding and Staffing" (annually updated)
    - "PACT Act Implementation Funding," 2024
    - "VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities: Background and Issues," 2023
    - "Appeals Modernization Act: Implementation Status," 2024
- **VA Office of Inspector General Reports** -- Audits and investigations of claims processing:
    - VA OIG Report 22-02122-153, "Quality Assurance in Claims Processing," 2023
    - VA OIG Report 22-00827-166, "Contracted Disability Examination Quality," 2023
    - VA OIG Report 23-01847-189, "Claims Processing Rework Analysis," 2024
    - VA OIG Report 21-03439-119, "Musculoskeletal Claims Accuracy Review," 2022
    - VA OIG Annual Report, 2024 (fraud statistics and program integrity)
- **Congressional Budget Office** -- Cost estimates for veteran benefits legislation:
    - "Estimated Budgetary Effects of the PACT Act," 2022
    - "Veterans' Disability Compensation: Long-Term Spending Projections," 2024

### Court Decisions

- **Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims (CAVC) Annual Reports** -- Case volume, outcomes, reversal/remand rates, and average case duration
- Key CAVC and Federal Circuit decisions affecting disability compensation:
    - *Dingess v. Nicholson*, 19 Vet. App. 473 (2006) -- notice requirements for disability ratings
    - *Vazquez-Flores v. Peake*, 22 Vet. App. 37 (2008) -- increased rating notice
    - *Gilbert v. Derwinski*, 1 Vet. App. 49 (1990) -- benefit-of-the-doubt standard
    - *Nehmer v. United States Veterans Administration*, N.D. Cal. -- Agent Orange class action
    - *Procopio v. Wilkie*, 913 F.3d 1371 (Fed. Cir. 2019) -- Blue Water Navy Agent Orange exposure
