# Survivors Benefits: Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap sequences reforms from the most immediately achievable (administrative changes, executive action) to the most structurally ambitious (legislative overhauls, systemic redesign). Timing assumes standard congressional and regulatory cycles.

---

## Phase 1: Immediate Actions (0-12 Months)

### Executive and Administrative Actions (No Legislation Required)

| Action | Authority | Timeline | Responsible |
|--------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Automatic DIC for survivors of 100% P&T veterans (10+ years) | VA rulemaking under 38 U.S.C. 1318 | 0-6 months | VBA |
| Proactive outreach to eligible non-enrollees | VA administrative discretion | 0-3 months | VBA |
| Fast-track DIC for combat deaths (14-day target) | VA processing guidance | 0-6 months | VBA |
| Improve CHAMPVA claims processing (target: 21 days) | VA administrative action | 0-12 months | VHA |
| Publish survivor outcome data (poverty rate, employment, access) | VA data collection authority | 6-12 months | VA Office of Policy and Planning |
| Designate VA Survivor Benefits Coordinator at each Regional Office | VA organizational authority | 0-6 months | VBA |

### Congressional Quick Wins

| Action | Estimated Cost | Vehicle | Likelihood |
|--------|---------------|---------|------------|
| Eliminate DIC remarriage penalty (lower age to 55) | ~$100M/year | Veterans' affairs authorization bill or NDAA | High |
| Extend Fry Scholarship spouse eligibility from 15 to 20 years | ~$30M/year | Education provisions in veterans bill | Moderate-High |
| Extend DEA child eligibility from age 26 to 30 | ~$45M/year | Education provisions in veterans bill | Moderate |

## Phase 2: Legislative Reforms (12-36 Months)

### Primary Legislation: Veterans Survivors Equity Act

**Components**:

1. **Full remarriage penalty elimination**: DIC continues regardless of remarital status at any age
2. **DIC rate increase**: Phase in DIC indexed to 55% of E-7 base pay over 3 years
3. **Enhanced child add-on**: Increase per-child DIC to $750/month
4. **Survivors Pension remarriage protection**: Extend remarriage protections to pension beneficiaries
5. **Unified survivor application**: Mandate interagency single application within 24 months

**Legislative Strategy**:

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Bill introduction | Month 1 | Introduce in both chambers with bipartisan cosponsors |
| Committee hearings | Months 2-4 | Survivor testimony, VA and DoD witnesses, cost analysis |
| Committee markup | Months 5-7 | Address budget scoring with phased implementation |
| Floor action | Months 8-12 | If standalone fails, attach to NDAA or VA authorization |
| Conference and passage | Months 12-18 | Negotiate between chambers |
| Implementation | Months 18-36 | VA and DoD rulemaking and system updates |

**Budget Strategy**: Phased implementation reduces first-year costs. The SBP-DIC offset elimination model--building supermajority cosponsorship to overcome budget resistance--is the proven path.

### CHAMPVA Modernization

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| GAO study on network feasibility | Months 0-12 | Congress directs GAO to assess CHAMPVA-TRICARE network integration |
| Pilot program authorization | Months 12-18 | Authorize CHAMPVA beneficiary access to TRICARE Select network in 3 regions |
| Pilot evaluation | Months 18-30 | Assess provider participation, cost, and beneficiary satisfaction |
| Full implementation | Months 30-48 | Nationwide rollout if pilot demonstrates feasibility |

### Education Benefit Equalization

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| DEA-Fry equalization study | Months 0-12 | VA and CBO estimate cost of full equalization |
| Legislative proposal | Months 12-18 | Convert DEA to Fry Scholarship-equivalent benefits |
| Implementation | Months 18-24 | Effective for academic terms beginning after enactment |

## Phase 3: Structural Reforms (36-60 Months)

### Survivor Benefits Ombudsman

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Legislative authorization | Months 36-42 | Establish office within VA, independent of VBA |
| Staffing and organization | Months 42-48 | Hire ombudsman and staff; establish complaint intake |
| Operational capability | Months 48-54 | Begin investigating complaints and recommending reforms |
| First annual report | Month 60 | Report to Congress on systemic issues and recommendations |

### Periodic Adequacy Review

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Mandate in legislation | Phase 2 (Months 12-18) | Require VA to conduct DIC adequacy review every 5 years |
| First review | Months 36-48 | VA and BLS assess DIC adequacy against cost-of-living benchmarks |
| Report to Congress | Month 48 | Recommendations for rate adjustments |
| Automatic adjustment mechanism | Month 48+ | If Congress approves, DIC adjusts based on adequacy findings |

### Unified Benefits Platform

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Interagency MOU | Months 12-24 | VA, DoD, SSA agree on data sharing and joint processing |
| Technology development | Months 24-42 | Build unified intake system |
| Pilot launch | Months 42-48 | Test in 5 states with high survivor populations |
| Nationwide rollout | Months 48-60 | Full deployment of single application system |

## Risk Factors

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Budget scoring blocks legislation | High | Delays DIC increase and remarriage reform | Use NDAA vehicle; build supermajority cosponsorship |
| VA IT system cannot support unified application | Medium | Delays administrative simplification | Use existing interagency platforms; pilot before mandating |
| CHAMPVA-TRICARE integration increases costs beyond projections | Medium | Slows CHAMPVA modernization | Phase through pilot program; cap cost growth |
| Congressional attention diverted by other issues | High | Delays all legislative reforms | Maintain sustained advocacy through VSOs and TAPS |
| PACT Act claims overwhelm VBA capacity | Medium | Increases DIC processing times | Prioritize VBA staffing and automation |
