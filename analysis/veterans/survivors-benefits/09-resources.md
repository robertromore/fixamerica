### Key Court Decisions

| Case | Subject | Significance |
|------|---------|--------------|
| *Nehmer v. U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs* (1986) | Agent Orange presumptive conditions | Established framework for toxic exposure survivor claims |
| *Sharp v. United States* (Fed. Cir., 2008) | SBP entitlement | Clarified SBP as earned benefit, not gratuity |
| *Haas v. Peake* (Fed. Cir., 2008) | Agent Orange service-connection | Defined requirements for herbicide exposure claims |
