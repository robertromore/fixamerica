## Research and Reports

### Essential Research

**Epidemiology and Prevalence**

- SAMHSA. *National Survey on Drug Use and Health: Veteran Supplement*. Annual. The most comprehensive national data on veteran substance use patterns
- VA National Suicide Data Report (annual). Includes substance-related mortality data
- CDC WONDER Database. Multiple Cause of Death files with veteran overdose data

**Clinical Evidence**

- National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine. *Medications for Opioid Use Disorder Save Lives*. Washington, DC: National Academies Press, 2019. Definitive review of MAT evidence
- Norman, S.B., et al. "The Role of Trauma-Related Distress in Comorbid PTSD and Substance Use Disorder Treatment." *Clinical Psychology Review* 32, no. 8 (2012): 770-784
- Back, S.E., et al. "Concurrent Treatment of PTSD and SUD Using Prolonged Exposure (COPE)." *Journal of Substance Abuse Treatment* 46, no. 2 (2014): 174-179
- Seal, K.H., et al. "Trends and Risk Factors for Mental Health Diagnoses Among Iraq and Afghanistan Veterans Using VA Healthcare." *JAMA* 301, no. 14 (2009): 1480-1487

**Military Culture and Substance Use**

- Bray, R.M., et al. "Trends in Alcohol Use Among Active Duty Military Personnel." *Military Medicine* 178, no. 9 (2013): 1015-1022
- Jacobson, I.G., et al. "Longitudinal Assessment of Binge Drinking and Alcohol-Related Problems Among Military Personnel." *Drug and Alcohol Dependence* 213 (2020): 108088
- Robins, L.N., et al. "Drug Use by U.S. Army Enlisted Men in Vietnam." *American Journal of Epidemiology* 99, no. 4 (1974): 235-249

**Opioid Crisis**

- VA Opioid Safety Initiative Dashboard. Quarterly reports on VA opioid prescribing trends
- Manhapra, A., et al. "Complex Persistent Opioid Dependence with Long-Term Opioids." *Journal of General Internal Medicine* 35, suppl. 3 (2020): S888-S893
- Shah, A., et al. "Characteristics of Initial Prescription Episodes and Likelihood of Long-Term Opioid Use." *BMJ* 357 (2017): j1648

**Harm Reduction**

- Walley, A.Y., et al. "Opioid Overdose Rates and Implementation of Overdose Education and Nasal Naloxone Distribution in Massachusetts." *BMJ* 346 (2013): f174
- Lancet Commission on Drug Policy and Health. Reports and recommendations, 2024

**Justice System**

- Bureau of Justice Statistics. "Veterans in State and Federal Prisons, 2022." 2023
- Knudsen, K.J. and Wingenfeld, S. "A Specialized Treatment Court for Veterans with Trauma Exposure." *Criminal Justice and Behavior* 43, no. 7 (2016): 911-927

## Educational Resources

### For Veterans and Families

- **Make the Connection** (maketheconnection.net): VA resource featuring veteran stories of recovery from substance abuse
- **AboutFace** (ptsd.va.gov/apps/aboutface): Video testimonials from veterans about PTSD and substance abuse recovery
- **VA Self-Help Resources**: Mobile apps including PTSD Coach, CBT-i Coach, and Mindfulness Coach
- **National Military Family Association**: Resources for family members of veterans with SUDs (militaryfamily.org)

### For Providers

- **VA ECHO (Extension for Community Healthcare Outcomes)**: Training program connecting civilian providers with VA SUD specialists
- **ASAM Courses**: Addiction medicine certification programs
- **PsychArmor Institute** (psycharmor.org): Free military cultural competency training for civilian providers
- **VA Academic Detailing Service**: Evidence-based prescribing education for VA providers

### For Researchers

- **VA Health Services Research and Development** (hsrd.research.va.gov): Funding opportunities and published research
- **NIDA Clinical Trials Network**: Multi-site addiction treatment research infrastructure
- **VA Cooperative Studies Program**: Large-scale clinical trials within the VA system
