# Toxic Exposure: Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines a three-phase implementation plan for comprehensive toxic exposure reform. Phase 1 focuses on immediate actions to improve PACT Act implementation and address urgent needs. Phase 2 builds systemic reforms to close coverage gaps and prevent future harm. Phase 3 establishes long-term institutional capacity and prevention infrastructure.

## Phase 1: Immediate Implementation (0-18 Months)

### Objective

Maximize the impact of existing legislation, accelerate claims processing, and reach unserved veteran populations.

### Actions

| Action | Lead Agency | Timeline | Status |
|--------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Launch national PACT Act outreach campaign | VA + VSOs | 0-3 months | Partially implemented |
| Deploy mobile claims processing units to 50 underserved areas | VBA | 3-12 months | Planning |
| Establish dedicated toxic exposure claims processing centers | VBA | 6-12 months | In progress |
| Hire 1,500 additional toxic exposure claims examiners | VBA | 6-18 months | Hiring ongoing |
| Deploy automated decision support for presumptive claims | VBA + OIT | 12-18 months | Development |
| Require DOJ to process Camp Lejeune claims within 180 days | DOJ + Congress | 6-12 months | Pending legislation |
| Create expedited Camp Lejeune track for terminal claimants | DOJ | 3-6 months | Pending |
| Initiate rulemaking for K2 and Fort McClellan presumptive conditions | VA Secretary | 6-12 months | Pending |
| Expand VA toxic exposure screening to all enrollment sites | VHA | 0-6 months | Mostly implemented |
| Fund VSO claims assistance capacity expansion ($100M) | Congress | 0-6 months | Pending appropriation |

| Complete alternative waste disposal infrastructure for all installations | DOD | 60-120 months | Planning |

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Congressional funding cuts to PACT Act | Medium | High | Build bipartisan coalitions; maintain public awareness |
| VA claims system overwhelmed by volume | High | High | Technology investment; staffing; simplified processes |
| DOD resistance to environmental mandates | High | Medium | Commander accountability; IG oversight; congressional mandates |
| Research findings lag behind emerging diseases | Medium | High | Fund longitudinal studies now; use "reasonable hypothesis" standard |
| Camp Lejeune litigation delays extend beyond claimant lifespans | High | High | Expedited terminal track; structured settlements; DOJ staffing |
| New exposure cohorts emerge faster than system can respond | Medium | Medium | Standing advisory board; concessions authority; rolling review cycle |
| Political will erodes after initial PACT Act momentum | Medium | High | Veterans advocacy; media engagement; annual progress reporting |
