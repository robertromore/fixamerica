# Veteran Employment: Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap presents a phased implementation strategy for the veteran employment reforms described in [Solutions](07-solutions.md). The three phases--Foundation (Years 1-2), Expansion (Years 3-4), and Consolidation (Years 5-7)--build sequentially, with earlier investments enabling later reforms. Success requires coordinated action across DOD, VA, DOL, SBA, state governments, and the private sector.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)

### Objective

Establish the institutional infrastructure, data systems, and pilot programs needed for comprehensive reform.

## Risk Mitigation

### Implementation Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Congressional funding shortfall** | High | High | Phase implementation; demonstrate ROI to secure bipartisan support |
| **DOD resistance to extended transition** | Medium | High | Align with readiness messaging; commander education |
| **State resistance to credential reciprocity** | Medium | Medium | Federal grants as incentive; competency-based framing |
| **Employer program fatigue** | Medium | Medium | Demonstrate ROI; simplify participation requirements |
| **Data privacy concerns** | Medium | Medium | Robust consent framework; privacy-by-design architecture |
| **Interagency coordination failure** | High | High | Establish joint governance council with White House leadership |
| **Economic recession impact** | Medium | High | Countercyclical funding provisions; automatic stabilizers |

### Political Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Administration change** | Medium | Medium | Embed reforms in statute rather than executive orders |
| **Veterans' preference backlash** | Low | Medium | Modernize rather than expand; competency-based framing |
| **Budget sequestration** | Low-Medium | High | Secure bipartisan support; veterans' programs historically protected |
| **Mission creep** | Medium | Medium | Clear outcome metrics; sunset provisions for underperforming programs |

## Governance Structure

### Proposed Coordination

| Body | Composition | Role |
|------|-------------|------|
| **White House Veterans Employment Council** | NSC, DOD, VA, DOL, SBA, OMB | Strategic direction and interagency coordination |
| **Interagency Implementation Team** | Working-level officials from all agencies | Day-to-day coordination and problem-solving |
| **Veteran Employment Advisory Board** | VSOs, employers, nonprofits, veterans | Stakeholder input and accountability |
| **Congressional oversight** | SVAC, HVAC, SASC, HASC | Authorization, appropriations, oversight |
| **State coordination council** | State veterans affairs directors, licensing boards | State implementation guidance |
