# Veteran Employment: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding, researching, and advocating for veteran employment reform. Resources are organized by type and include academic research, government reports, books, think tank publications, and advocacy organizations.

---

## Academic Research

|-------|-----------|-------------|------|-----------------|
| "Veteran Employment Outcomes: A Review of the Literature" | Keeling, M. et al. | *Journal of Veterans Studies* | 2023 | Comprehensive synthesis of employment outcome research |

| "Military Service and Civilian Earnings" | Angrist, J. | *American Economic Review* | 2020 (updated) | Econometric analysis of service impact on lifetime earnings |

| "The Impact of PTSD on Employment" | Smith, M. et al. | *Journal of Traumatic Stress* | 2023 | Mental health effects on veteran employment outcomes |
| "Women Veterans in the Labor Force" | Prokos, A. & Cabage, L. | *Armed Forces & Society* | 2022 | Gender-specific employment barriers analysis |
