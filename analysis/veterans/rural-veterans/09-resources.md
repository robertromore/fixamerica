## Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Description |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Veterans Benefits, Health Care, and Information Technology Act | P.L. 109-461 (2006) | Created Office of Rural Health |
| Veterans Access, Choice, and Accountability Act (Choice Act) | P.L. 113-146 (2014) | First community care eligibility framework |
| VA MISSION Act | P.L. 115-182 (2018) | Current community care framework with access standards |
| COMPACT Act | P.L. 116-214 (2020) | Emergency suicidal crisis care at non-VA facilities |
| PACT Act | P.L. 117-168 (2022) | Toxic exposure eligibility expansion |
| PRESCRIBE Act | Proposed | Cross-state VA telehealth licensing preemption |
