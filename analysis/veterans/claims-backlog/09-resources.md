# VA Claims Backlog: Resources

## Government Reports and Data Sources

### VA Reports

- **VA Monday Morning Workload Reports** -- Weekly snapshot of pending claims, backlog, and completions. Available at [va.gov/disability/](https://www.va.gov/disability/)
- **VA Benefits Administration Annual Performance Reports** -- Comprehensive yearly data on claims processing metrics, accuracy, and workforce
- **VA Systematic Technical Accuracy Review (STAR) Program** -- Accuracy review methodology and results
- **VA PACT Act Implementation Dashboard** -- Real-time tracking of PACT Act claims filed and processed
- **VA Appeals Modernization Act Dashboard** -- Appeals data by review lane
- **Board of Veterans' Appeals Annual Reports** -- BVA caseload, decisions, and timeliness data

### Congressional and Government Oversight

- **GAO High-Risk List: VA Disability Benefits** -- Updated biennially; tracks progress on claims processing improvements. GAO-23-106203, 2023
- **GAO Reports on VA Claims Processing** -- Multiple reports dating to 2010. Key recent reports:
    - GAO-23-106059, "VA Claims Processing: Resource Requirements Assessment," 2023
    - GAO-12-846, "VA Disability Benefits: Timely Processing Remains a Challenge," 2012
- **Congressional Research Service Reports** -- Regularly updated analyses:
    - "Veterans' Benefits: Claims Backlog" (multiple editions)
    - "VBA Funding and Staffing" (annually updated)
    - "PACT Act Implementation Funding," 2024
    - "VBA IT Modernization," 2023
- **VA Office of Inspector General Reports** -- Audits and investigations of claims processing:
    - VA OIG Report 22-02122-153, "Quality Assurance," 2023
    - VA OIG Report 22-00827-166, "Contract Medical Examination Quality," 2023
    - VA OIG Report 23-01847-189, "Claims Processing Rework Analysis," 2024
    - VA OIG Report 21-03439-119, "Musculoskeletal Claims Accuracy," 2022

### Court Decisions

- **Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims (CAVC) Annual Reports** -- Case volume, outcomes, and remand rates
- Key CAVC and Federal Circuit decisions affecting claims processing:
    - *Dingess v. Nicholson*, 19 Vet. App. 473 (2006) -- notice requirements
    - *Vazquez-Flores v. Peake*, 22 Vet. App. 37 (2008) -- increased rating notice
    - *Nehmer v. United States Veterans Administration*, N.D. Cal. -- Agent Orange class action
