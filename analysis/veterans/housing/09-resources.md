# Veteran Housing: Resources

## Government Reports and Data

### VA Reports

- VA Office of Mental Health and Suicide Prevention. *National Veteran Suicide Prevention Annual Report*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.mentalhealth.va.gov/suicide_prevention/data.asp>
- Department of Veterans Affairs. *Homeless Veterans Programs Annual Report*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.va.gov/homeless/>
- Department of Veterans Affairs. *VA Home Loan Guaranty Annual Report*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.benefits.va.gov/homeloans/>
- Department of Veterans Affairs. *SSVF Program Annual Report*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.va.gov/homeless/ssvf/>
- Department of Veterans Affairs. *Grant and Per Diem Program Annual Report*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.va.gov/homeless/gpd.asp>
- Department of Veterans Affairs. *Specially Adapted Housing Annual Report*. Published annually.
- VA Office of Inspector General. Housing program audits and evaluations. Various years.
- VA FY 2022-2025 Budget Submissions. Available at: <https://www.va.gov/budget/>

### HUD Reports

- U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development. *Annual Homeless Assessment Report (AHAR)*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.huduser.gov/portal/datasets/ahar.html>
- HUD Office of Public and Indian Housing. *HUD-VASH Program Data*. Published annually. Available at: <https://www.hud.gov/program_offices/public_indian_housing/programs/hcv/vash>
- HUD Office of Policy Development and Research. *Worst Case Housing Needs Report*. Published biennially.
- HUD Office of Fair Housing and Equal Opportunity. Fair housing enforcement data. Available at: <https://www.hud.gov/program_offices/fair_housing_equal_opp>

### Congressional Reports

- Government Accountability Office. *VA Homeless Programs: Actions Needed to Improve Oversight*. Various years.
- Government Accountability Office. *VA Home Loan Program: Actions Needed to Improve Oversight of Lenders*. GAO-22-104466, 2022.
- Congressional Research Service. "Veterans and Homelessness." R40407, updated regularly.
- Congressional Research Service. "VA Housing: Guaranteed Loans, Direct Loans, and Specially Adapted Housing Grants." R42504, updated regularly.
- Congressional Research Service. "HUD-VASH: Veteran Homelessness." R45382, updated regularly.
- VA Office of Inspector General. "Audit of HUD-VASH Voucher Utilization." Various years.

### Other Federal Reports

- U.S. Interagency Council on Homelessness. *Federal Strategic Plan to Prevent and End Homelessness*. Available at: <https://www.usich.gov/>
- Census Bureau. American Community Survey: Veteran Housing Data. Published annually.
- Federal Housing Finance Agency. House Price Index. Published quarterly.
- Mortgage Bankers Association. National Delinquency Survey. Published quarterly.
- Federal Reserve. Survey of Consumer Finances (veteran household wealth data). Published triennially.

## Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Description |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Servicemen's Readjustment Act (GI Bill) | P.L. 78-346 (1944) | Created VA Home Loan Guaranty program |
| McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act | P.L. 100-77 (1987) | First comprehensive federal homeless assistance law |
| HUD-VASH Authorization | P.L. 102-590 (1992) | Created HUD-VA Supportive Housing program |
| Homeless Veterans Comprehensive Assistance Act | P.L. 107-95 (2001) | Expanded VA homeless programs |
| HEARTH Act | P.L. 111-22 (2009) | Reauthorized McKinney-Vento; created coordinated entry |
| SSVF Authorization | P.L. 111-275 (2010) | Created Supportive Services for Veteran Families |
| Blue Water Navy Vietnam Veterans Act | P.L. 116-23 (2019) | Removed VA Home Loan limits |
| Johnny Isakson and David P. Roe Veterans Health Care and Benefits Improvement Act | P.L. 116-315 (2021) | Expanded SAH grants and homeless program authorities |
| PACT Act | P.L. 117-168 (2022) | Expanded eligibility for housing programs for toxic-exposed veterans |
| Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA) | 50 U.S.C. 3901 et seq. | Protections against foreclosure and eviction during military service |
