# VA Reform: Resources

## Government Reports and Data Sources

### VA Reports

- **VA FY 2025 Budget Submission** -- Comprehensive budget request including VHA, VBA, NCA, and IT spending breakdowns
- **VA Strategic Analytics for Improvement and Learning (SAIL)** -- Quality and performance metrics for all VA medical centers, updated quarterly
- **VA Access to Care Dashboard** -- Real-time wait-time and access data by facility and care type
- **VA Office of Community Care Annual Reports** -- Community care utilization, spending, and performance data
- **VA All Employee Survey** -- Annual survey covering employee satisfaction, burnout, engagement, and organizational culture
- **VA Veterans Experience Office Reports** -- Veteran trust scores, satisfaction data, and patient experience metrics
- **VA Survey of Healthcare Experiences of Patients (SHEP)** -- Inpatient and outpatient satisfaction data comparable to HCAHPS
- **VA Human Resources Staffing Reports** -- Vacancy rates, hiring data, and workforce demographics by facility and specialty
- **VA Office of Construction and Facilities Management** -- Infrastructure condition assessments, construction backlog estimates, and facility planning data
- **VHA Facility Directory** -- Complete listing of VA medical centers, CBOCs, vet centers, and other facilities

### Congressional and Government Oversight

- **GAO High-Risk List: VA Health Care** -- Updated biennially; tracks progress on VA healthcare management and modernization. GAO-23-106203, 2023
- **GAO Reports on VA Reform** -- Key recent reports:
    - GAO-24-106605, "VA Electronic Health Record Modernization," 2024
    - GAO-23-106059, "VA Community Care: Spending Growth and Oversight," 2023
    - GAO-22-105065, "VA Health Care: Workforce Planning and Recruitment," 2022
- **Congressional Research Service Reports** -- Regularly updated analyses:
    - "VA Budget and Appropriations" (annually updated)
    - "VA MISSION Act Implementation," 2024
    - "VA Electronic Health Record Modernization: Status and Issues," 2024
    - "VA Workforce: Hiring, Retention, and Compensation," 2023
    - "Impact of Continuing Resolutions on VA," 2024
- **VA Office of Inspector General Reports** -- Audits, investigations, and program evaluations:
    - VA OIG Report 23-01239-167, "Patient Safety Issues in Oracle Health EHR," 2024
    - VA OIG EHR Modernization Reports, 2020-2024 (multiple reports)
    - VA OIG Staffing Report, 2024
    - VA OIG Workforce Analysis, 2024
    - VA OIG Report 19-00041-241, "Office of Accountability and Whistleblower Protection," 2020

### Independent Assessments

- **National Academy of Public Administration (NAPA), "VA Governance Assessment," 2023** -- Comprehensive evaluation of VA organizational structure, leadership stability, and governance reform options
- **Independent Assessment of VA Health Care (Section 201 Report), 2015** -- Mandated by the 2014 Choice Act; comprehensive external evaluation of VHA operations
- **RAND Corporation, "Assessment of VA Healthcare Quality," 2023** -- Comparison of VA and private-sector clinical outcomes across multiple conditions

## Key Legislation

| Law | Citation | Year | Relevance |
|-----|----------|------|-----------|
| Department of Veterans Affairs Act | Public Law 100-527 | 1988 | Elevated VA to cabinet-level department |
| Veterans' Judicial Review Act | Public Law 100-687 | 1988 | Created judicial review of VA decisions |
| Veterans Access, Choice, and Accountability Act | Public Law 113-146 | 2014 | Created Veterans Choice Program after wait-time scandal |
| VA Accountability and Whistleblower Protection Act | Public Law 115-41 | 2017 | Employee accountability and whistleblower protections |
| VA MISSION Act | Public Law 115-182 | 2018 | Permanent community care framework; AIR Commission |
| PACT Act | Public Law 117-168 | 2022 | Largest VA benefits expansion; toxic exposure |
| RAISE Act | Public Law 118-81 | 2024 | Increased clinical pay authorities |
| VA Advance Appropriations Act | Public Law 111-81 | 2009 | Advance appropriations for VA medical care |
