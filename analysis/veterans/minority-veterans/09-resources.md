# Minority Veterans: Resources

## Overview

This document provides a curated bibliography of the most important resources for understanding minority veteran disparities, including academic research, government reports, books, data sources, and organizations. Resources are organized by category and annotated with descriptions of their relevance.

---

## Foundational Academic Research

### Racial Disparities in VA Benefits and Healthcare

- Spoont, M., et al. "Racial and Ethnic Disparities in PTSD Assessment and Service Use Among Veterans." *Journal of Traumatic Stress* 33, no. 4 (2020): 547-558. -- Key study demonstrating that cultural variation in PTSD symptom presentation affects assessment outcomes, contributing to racial rating gaps
- Elixhauser, A., et al. "Racial Disparities in VA Healthcare Utilization." *Medical Care* 57, Suppl 2 (2019): S112-S118. -- Analysis of utilization patterns showing lower engagement among Hispanic and AAPI veterans
- Saha, S., et al. "Racial and Ethnic Disparities in the VA Health Care System: A Systematic Review." *Journal of General Internal Medicine* 23, no. 5 (2008): 654-671. -- Comprehensive literature review establishing the evidence base for VA healthcare disparities
- Murdoch, M., et al. "Race Disparities in VA Service Connection for PTSD." *Medical Care* 55, no. 9 (2017): 940-949. -- Study finding that Black veterans received lower PTSD disability ratings than white veterans after controlling for symptom severity
- Washington, D.L., et al. "Racial Variations in Veterans Affairs Ambulatory Care." *American Journal of Public Health* 95, no. 12 (2005): 2231-2237. -- Early documentation of racial disparities in VA outpatient care utilization

### Military Justice and Discharge Disparities

- Christensen, D.S., and Tsilker, Y. "Racial Disparities in Military Justice." Protect Our Defenders, 2017 (updated 2023). -- Comprehensive analysis of court-martial, nonjudicial punishment, and discharge characterization data by race
- DOD Task Force on the Administration of Military Justice. Report, 1972. -- The original government study documenting systemic racial bias in Vietnam-era military justice
- Seamone, E.R. "Reclaiming the Rehabilitative Ethic in Military Justice: The Path to Discharge Reform." *Military Law Review* 226 (2018): 1-114. -- Legal scholarship examining the discharge system's impact on minority veterans and pathways for reform
- RAND Corporation. "Racial Disparities in Military Discipline." Research Brief RB-10196-AF, 2020. -- Analysis finding that Black service members receive harsher discipline even after controlling for rank, offense type, and prior record

### Historical Scholarship

- Katznelson, Ira. *When Affirmative Action Was White: An Untold History of Racial Inequality in Twentieth-Century America*. New York: W.W. Norton, 2005. -- Landmark work documenting how the GI Bill and other New Deal programs were administered in racially discriminatory ways
- Herbold, Hilary. "Never a Level Playing Field: Blacks and the GI Bill." *Journal of Blacks in Higher Education* 6 (1994): 104-108. -- Analysis of GI Bill exclusion of Black veterans from homeownership and higher education
- Westheider, James. *Fighting on Two Fronts: African Americans and the Vietnam War*. New York: New York University Press, 2005. -- History of Black military service in Vietnam, including draft disparities and military justice discrimination
- Buckley, Gail. *American Patriots: The Story of Blacks in the Military*. New York: Random House, 2001. -- Comprehensive history of African American military service from the Revolution through the present
- Humes, Edward. *Over Here: How the GI Bill Transformed the American Dream*. Orlando: Harcourt, 2006. -- Includes analysis of racial disparities in GI Bill implementation

### AIAN Veterans

- National Indian Health Board. "AI/AN Veterans Health Report." 2023. -- Data on healthcare access, suicide rates, and service utilization among AIAN veterans
- Johnson-Jennings, M., et al. "American Indian Veterans and Mental Health." *American Indian and Alaska Native Mental Health Research* 27, no. 1 (2020): 1-23. -- Study of mental health barriers and cultural factors affecting AIAN veteran treatment
- Kramer, B.J., et al. "Health Care for American Indian and Alaska Native Veterans." *Journal of General Internal Medicine* 24, Suppl 3 (2009): S514-S519. -- Analysis of IHS-VA coordination challenges

## Government Reports and Data Sources

### VA Reports

- VA Office of Inspector General. "Racial Disparities in VA Disability Compensation." Report No. 21-01052-57, 2022. -- The definitive OIG report documenting racial disparities in disability rating outcomes
- VA National Center for Veterans Analysis and Statistics. "Veteran Population Projections Model." Updated annually. -- Demographic projections showing the growing minority share of the veteran population
- VA Office of Health Equity. Annual Report, FY 2024. -- Assessment of healthcare disparities and equity interventions
- VA Office of Health Equity. Cultural Competency Assessment, 2023. -- Evaluation of VA provider training and patient satisfaction by race/ethnicity
- VA Office of Health Equity. Language Access Assessment, 2024. -- Data on interpretation services availability across VA facilities
- VA Center for Minority Veterans. Annual Report, FY 2024. -- Programs, outreach, and recommendations for minority veteran services
- VA Advisory Committee on Minority Veterans. Biennial Report, 2024. -- Statutory recommendations to the Secretary of Veterans Affairs
- VA Office of Diversity and Inclusion. FY 2023 Annual Report. -- Workforce diversity data by grade, position, and race/ethnicity
- VA National Suicide Data Report, 2023 (data through 2021). -- Suicide rates disaggregated by race and ethnicity

### DOD Reports

- DOD Demographic Profile of the Military Community, 2023. -- Active duty, reserve, and veteran demographics by race, ethnicity, and gender
- DOD Population Representation Reports, 2023. -- Recruitment, retention, and promotion data by race
- DOD Military Justice Panel Report, 2023. -- Analysis of military justice outcomes by race

### Congressional and GAO Reports

- Congressional Research Service. "Minority Veterans: Demographics and VA Benefit Usage." Updated 2024. -- Overview of minority veteran demographics and program utilization
- Government Accountability Office. Study of Racial Disparities in VA Claims Processing (pending, expected 2025). -- Major ongoing investigation
- Congressional Black Caucus Veterans Task Force. Reports and hearings, various years. -- Legislative activity on minority veteran issues

### Census and Statistical Sources

- U.S. Census Bureau. American Community Survey, 2023. -- Veteran population data by race, ethnicity, geography, and socioeconomic characteristics
- Bureau of Labor Statistics. "Employment Situation of Veterans." Annual report, 2024. -- Employment and unemployment data by veteran status and race
- HUD Annual Homeless Assessment Report, 2024. -- Veteran homelessness data by race and ethnicity
- OPM FedScope, 2023. -- Federal workforce demographics including VA employees

## Books

- Alexander, Michelle. *The New Jim Crow: Mass Incarceration in the Age of Colorblindness*. New York: The New Press, 2010. -- While not veteran-specific, essential context for understanding how criminal justice disparities affect minority veterans
- Nalty, Bernard C. *Strength for the Fight: A History of Black Americans in the Military*. New York: Free Press, 1986. -- Comprehensive military history of African Americans
- Moore, Brenda L. *Serving Our Country: Japanese American Women in the Military During World War II*. New Brunswick: Rutgers University Press, 2003. -- History of intersecting racial and gender experiences in military service
- Appy, Christian G. *Working-Class War: American Combat Soldiers and Vietnam*. Chapel Hill: University of North Carolina Press, 1993. -- Analysis of class and race in Vietnam-era military service
- Towne, Allen. *Doctors in Blue: The Medical History of the Union Army*. Baton Rouge: Louisiana State University Press, 1952. -- Includes early documentation of racial disparities in military medical care

## Multimedia and Journalism

### Documentaries

- *The Tuskegee Airmen* (HBO, 1995) -- Dramatization of the 332nd Fighter Group's WWII service
- *The War* (Ken Burns, PBS, 2007) -- WWII documentary including minority veteran perspectives
- *Brothers in War* (PBS, 2019) -- The 761st Tank Battalion in WWII
- *Go for Broke: An Origin Story* (PBS, 2017) -- Japanese American WWII service

### Investigative Journalism

- "Inequitable Discharges" series, *Military Times*, 2020-2023. -- Reporting on racial disparities in military justice and discharge outcomes
- "Black Veterans and the VA" series, *ProPublica*, 2021-2022. -- Investigation of disability rating disparities
- "The Deported Veterans" series, *The Guardian*, 2019-2022. -- Reporting on deported U.S. military veterans in Tijuana
