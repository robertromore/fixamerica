## Legal Resources

### Key Statutes

- Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, 42 U.S.C. Sections 12101-12213
- Help America Vote Act of 2002, 52 U.S.C. Sections 20901-21145
- Voting Rights Act of 1965, 52 U.S.C. Sections 10301-10702
- National Voter Registration Act of 1993, 52 U.S.C. Sections 20501-20511
- Voting Accessibility for the Elderly and Handicapped Act of 1984, 52 U.S.C. Sections 20101-20107
- Rehabilitation Act of 1973, Section 504, 29 U.S.C. Section 794

### Key Regulations

- 28 C.F.R. Part 35 (ADA Title II regulations, applicable to state and local election administration)
- 52 U.S.C. Section 21081(a)(3) (HAVA accessible voting equipment requirement)
- EAC Voluntary Voting System Guidelines (VVSG), Version 2.0

### Key Cases

- *O'Brien v. Skinner*, 414 U.S. 524 (1974) (states cannot deny absentee ballots to institutionalized voters)
- *Tennessee v. Lane*, 541 U.S. 509 (2004) (Title II damages claims valid under 14th Amendment Section 5)
- *Olmstead v. L.C.*, 527 U.S. 581 (1999) (unnecessary institutionalization violates ADA)
- *Shelby County v. Holder*, 570 U.S. 529 (2013) (Section 5 preclearance invalidated)
- *National Federation of the Blind v. Lamone*, No. 1:14-cv-01631 (D. Md.) (accessible ballot delivery required)
- *National Federation of the Blind v. Volusia County* (Fla. 2006) (accessible voting equipment settlement)
- *Disability Rights New Jersey v. Commissioner, New Jersey Department of Human Services* (2014) (guardianship voting rights)
