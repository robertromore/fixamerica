# Voting Accessibility: Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation Building (Year 1-2)

### Executive and Administrative Actions

| Action | Lead Actor | Timeline | Prerequisites |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Issue Executive Order directing federal agencies to prioritize voting accessibility | White House | Month 1-3 | Presidential commitment |
| Direct DOJ Voting Section and Disability Rights Section to jointly prioritize polling place accessibility enforcement | Attorney General | Month 1-6 | AG confirmation |
| Instruct EAC to update VVSG accessibility standards | EAC | Month 3-12 | Commissioner appointments |
| Direct DOJ to issue guidance on mail voting accessibility under ADA Title II | DOJ | Month 3-12 | Interagency coordination |
| Issue updated polling place accessibility survey tool and checklist | DOJ, EAC | Month 6-12 | Survey tool development |
| Initiate rulemaking on accessible electronic ballot delivery standards | EAC, NIST | Month 6-18 | Standards research |

### Legislative Actions

| Action | Lead Actor | Timeline | Prerequisites |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Introduce Accessible Voting Act (comprehensive federal legislation) | Congress | Month 1-6 | Bill drafting |
| Introduce Guardianship Voting Rights Restoration Act | Congress | Month 3-6 | State law survey |
| Secure appropriation for voting accessibility improvement grants | Appropriations Committee | Annual cycle | Budget proposal |
| Secure committee hearings on disability voting gap | Senate Rules, House Administration | Month 6-12 | Committee leadership support |
| Introduce RAVBM Expansion Act (remote accessible vote-by-mail) | Congress | Month 3-12 | Security analysis |

### Coalition Building

| Action | Lead Actor | Timeline | Prerequisites |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Convene national disability voting accessibility coalition | AAPD, NFB, NDRN | Month 1-3 | Organizational commitment |
| Develop unified reform platform with election integrity organizations | Coalition + Brennan Center, Common Cause | Month 3-6 | Coalition agreement |
| Launch REV UP Accessibility campaign focused on polling place barriers | AAPD | Month 6-12 | Funding secured |
| Engage bipartisan congressional champions | Coalition | Month 1-12 | Relationship building |
| Build alliance with aging organizations (AARP) on shared accessibility concerns | Coalition | Month 3-12 | Identify shared priorities |

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Voting restriction laws undermine accessibility gains | High | High | Frame disability accessibility separately from broader voting access debates; pursue ADA litigation |
| Congressional appropriation shortfall | Medium | High | Phase implementation; prioritize highest-impact reforms; leverage existing DOJ authority |
| State resistance to guardianship voting reform | Medium | Medium | Model legislation approach; ABA and judicial support; incremental state-by-state strategy |
| Voting equipment vendors fail to meet new accessibility standards | Medium | High | Extended vendor compliance timelines; competitive procurement requirements; open-source alternatives |
| Administration change reverses enforcement priorities | Medium | High | Embed reforms in statute rather than executive orders; build bipartisan support |
| Election security backlash against RAVBM | High | Medium | Paper return requirement addresses security concerns; security certification framework; separate from online voting debate |
| Poll worker training mandates reduce volunteer pool | Low | Medium | Integrate training into existing programs; compensate for additional training time; online training options |
| Litigation challenging new accessibility mandates | Low | Medium | Strong constitutional basis under 14th Amendment and Commerce Clause; ADA precedent |
