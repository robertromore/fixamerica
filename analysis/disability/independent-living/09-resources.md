## Legal Resources

### Key Statutes

| Law | Citation | Independent Living Relevance |
|-----|----------|------------------------------|
| Americans with Disabilities Act | 42 U.S.C. Section 12101 et seq. | Community integration mandate (Olmstead basis) |
| Medicaid Act | 42 U.S.C. Section 1396 et seq. | LTSS financing; HCBS waiver authority |
| Rehabilitation Act, Title VII | 29 U.S.C. Section 796 et seq. | CIL authorization and funding |
| Rehabilitation Act, Section 504 | 29 U.S.C. Section 794 | Disability discrimination in federally funded programs |
| Social Security Act, Section 1915(c) | 42 U.S.C. Section 1396n(c) | HCBS waiver authority |
| Social Security Act, Section 1915(i) | 42 U.S.C. Section 1396n(i) | State plan HCBS option |
| Social Security Act, Section 1915(k) | 42 U.S.C. Section 1396n(k) | Community First Choice |
| Affordable Care Act | P.L. 111-148 | CFC, Balancing Incentive Program, MFP extension |
| Fair Housing Act | 42 U.S.C. Section 3601 et seq. | Accessible housing; anti-discrimination |
| American Rescue Plan Act | P.L. 117-2, Section 9817 | Enhanced HCBS FMAP (temporary) |
| RAISE Family Caregivers Act | P.L. 115-119 | Family caregiver support strategy |

### Key Court Cases

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|--------------|
| *Olmstead v. L.C.* | 527 U.S. 581 (1999) | Unjustified institutionalization violates ADA; community integration right |
| *Pennhurst State School v. Halderman* | 451 U.S. 1 (1981) | Rights of institutionalized people; Spending Clause limits |
| *City of Cleburne v. Cleburne Living Center* | 473 U.S. 432 (1985) | Zoning discrimination against group homes |
| *NFIB v. Sebelius* | 567 U.S. 519 (2012) | Spending Clause limits on Medicaid mandates |
| *Ligas v. Maram* | No. 05-C-4331 (N.D. Ill.) | Illinois IDD deinstitutionalization consent decree |
| *Lane v. Brown* | No. 3:12-cv-00138 (D. Or.) | Olmstead applied to sheltered workshops |
| *United States v. Rhode Island* | No. 14-175 (D.R.I.) | Olmstead applied to day services integration |
