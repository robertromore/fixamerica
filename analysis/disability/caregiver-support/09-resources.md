# Caregiver Support: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Federal Agencies

- **Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS)** - <https://www.cms.gov/> - Administers Medicaid HCBS programs
- **Administration for Community Living (ACL)** - <https://acl.gov/> - Administers NFCSP and aging/disability programs
- **Department of Labor, Wage and Hour Division** - <https://www.dol.gov/agencies/whd> - Enforces FLSA for home care workers
- **Department of Veterans Affairs, Caregiver Support** - <https://www.caregiver.va.gov/> - VA caregiver programs

### Disability Rights and Advocacy

- **National Council on Independent Living (NCIL)** - <https://ncil.org/> - Self-direction and community integration advocacy
- **ADAPT** - <https://adapt.org/> - Direct action for community-based services
- **The Arc of the United States** - <https://thearc.org/> - Intellectual/developmental disability advocacy
- **Autistic Self Advocacy Network (ASAN)** - <https://autisticadvocacy.org/> - Autistic self-advocate organization
- **National Disability Rights Network (NDRN)** - <https://www.ndrn.org/> - Protection and advocacy system
- **Disability Rights Education and Defense Fund (DREDF)** - <https://dredf.org/> - Legal advocacy and *Olmstead* enforcement

### Caregiver Support Organizations

- **AARP** - <https://www.aarp.org/caregiving/> - Caregiver resources and policy advocacy
- **National Alliance for Caregiving** - <https://www.caregiving.org/> - Research and advocacy
- **Caregiver Action Network** - <https://www.caregiveraction.org/> - Direct caregiver support
- **Family Caregiver Alliance** - <https://www.caregiver.org/> - Services and policy research
- **Rosalynn Carter Institute for Caregivers** - <https://www.rosalynncarter.org/> - Caregiver health research

### Workforce Organizations

- **PHI National** - <https://www.phinational.org/> - Direct care workforce policy and research
- **National Domestic Workers Alliance** - <https://www.domesticworkers.org/> - Worker organizing and advocacy
- **Caring Across Generations** - <https://caringacross.org/> - Care policy coalition
- **SEIU** - <https://www.seiu.org/> - Home care worker union representation
- **Applied Self-Direction** - <https://www.appliedselfdirection.com/> - Self-directed services resources

### Research and Policy

- **Kaiser Family Foundation** - <https://www.kff.org/medicaid/> - Medicaid and LTSS research
- **Urban Institute** - <https://www.urban.org/> - LTSS financing and policy research
- **HCBS Advocacy Coalition** - <https://hcbsadvocacy.org/> - HCBS waiting list data and advocacy
- **National Academy for State Health Policy** - <https://nashp.org/> - State health policy analysis
- **Center for Health Care Strategies** - <https://www.chcs.org/> - Medicaid innovation
