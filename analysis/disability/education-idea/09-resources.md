## Legal Resources

### Key Statutes

- Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA), 20 U.S.C. Sections 1400-1482
- Rehabilitation Act of 1973, Section 504, 29 U.S.C. Section 794
- Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, 42 U.S.C. Sections 12101-12213
- Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA), 20 U.S.C. Sections 6301-6578
- Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA), 29 U.S.C. Sections 3101-3361

### Key Regulations

- 34 C.F.R. Part 300 (IDEA Part B regulations)
- 34 C.F.R. Part 303 (IDEA Part C regulations)
- 34 C.F.R. Part 104 (Section 504 regulations)

### Key Cases

- *Board of Education v. Rowley*, 458 U.S. 176 (1982) -- established "some educational benefit" FAPE standard
- *Endrew F. v. Douglas County School District*, 580 U.S. 386 (2017) -- raised FAPE standard to "appropriately ambitious" progress
- *PARC v. Commonwealth of Pennsylvania*, 334 F. Supp. 1257 (E.D. Pa. 1971) -- right to education for children with intellectual disabilities
- *Mills v. Board of Education*, 348 F. Supp. 866 (D.D.C. 1972) -- right to education for all children with disabilities
- *Daniel R.R. v. State Board of Education*, 874 F.2d 1036 (5th Cir. 1989) -- two-part LRE test
- *Sacramento City Unified v. Rachel H.*, 14 F.3d 1398 (9th Cir. 1994) -- four-factor LRE test
- *Oberti v. Board of Education*, 995 F.2d 1204 (3d Cir. 1993) -- strong presumption of inclusion
- *Burlington School Committee v. Department of Education*, 471 U.S. 359 (1985) -- parental right to reimbursement for private placement
- *Florence County School District Four v. Carter*, 510 U.S. 7 (1993) -- reimbursement even if private school not approved
- *Schaffer v. Weast*, 546 U.S. 49 (2005) -- burden of proof on party challenging IEP (usually parent)
- *Arlington Central School District v. Murphy*, 548 U.S. 291 (2006) -- no reimbursement for expert witness fees under IDEA
- *Fry v. Napoleon Community Schools*, 580 U.S. 154 (2017) -- exhaustion of IDEA administrative remedies
