# SSI Reform: Resources

## Government Sources

### Social Security Administration

- **SSI Annual Statistical Report**: [ssa.gov/policy/docs/statcomps/ssi_asr/](https://www.ssa.gov/policy/docs/statcomps/ssi_asr/) - Comprehensive annual data on SSI recipients, benefits, and program characteristics
- **SSI Monthly Statistics**: [ssa.gov/policy/docs/statcomps/ssi_monthly/](https://www.ssa.gov/policy/docs/statcomps/ssi_monthly/) - Monthly recipient counts and benefit data
- **Annual Report of the SSI Program**: [ssa.gov/OACT/ssir/](https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/ssir/) - Chief Actuary's annual assessment of SSI finances and projections
- **SSI Federal Payment Amounts**: [ssa.gov/oact/cola/SSIamts.html](https://www.ssa.gov/oact/cola/SSIamts.html) - Current and historical benefit levels
- **Understanding Supplemental Security Income**: [ssa.gov/ssi/](https://www.ssa.gov/ssi/) - Program overview and eligibility information

### Congressional Research Service

- **"Supplemental Security Income (SSI): An Overview"**: Comprehensive CRS report updated regularly; covers eligibility, benefits, administration
- **"SSI: Income and Resource Eligibility Requirements"**: Detailed analysis of income-counting and asset rules
- **"SSI: Children and Age-18 Redeterminations"**: Analysis of childhood SSI and transition to adult standard

### Government Accountability Office

- **GAO-23-106178**: "SSI: Opportunities to Reduce Administrative Burden and Improve Service Delivery" (2023)
- **GAO-23-105480**: "SSA Disability: Actions Needed to Reduce Prolonged Wait Times" (2023)
- **GAO-21-341**: "SSI: Better Data and Processes Could Help SSA Reduce Overpayments" (2021)
- **GAO-14-494R**: "SSI Marriage Penalty" (2014)

### SSA Office of Inspector General

- **SSI Overpayment Reports**: Regular audits of overpayment causes, amounts, and recovery
- **Program Integrity Reviews**: Assessments of eligibility determination accuracy

## Policy Research Organizations

### Center on Budget and Policy Priorities (CBPP)

- *"SSI: A Lifeline for Children and Adults with Disabilities and Older Adults"* (2024)
- *"Supplemental Security Income Benefits Fall Short of the Poverty Line in Every State"* (2024)
- *"It's Time to Modernize SSI"* (2023)
- CBPP SSI Policy Page: [cbpp.org/topics/social-security/supplemental-security-income](https://www.cbpp.org/topics/social-security/supplemental-security-income)

### National Academy of Social Insurance (NASI)

- *"Modernizing SSI for People with Disabilities"* report series
- Panel discussions and policy briefs on SSI reform options

### National Council on Disability (NCD)

- *"The Social Security Administration's Efforts to Promote Employment for People with Disabilities"* (2005)
- *"National Disability Policy: A Progress Report"* (annual)
- NCD recommendations on SSI benefit adequacy and program design

### Urban Institute

- Microsimulation analyses of SSI reform proposals
- *"How Would SSI Reform Affect Poverty Among People with Disabilities?"*
- TRIM3 model estimates of SSI policy changes

### Brookings Institution

- Hamilton Project proposals on disability benefit reform
- Economic analyses of work incentives in means-tested programs

## Advocacy Organizations

### Disability Rights

- **The Arc of the United States**: [thearc.org](https://www.thearc.org) - Advocacy for people with intellectual and developmental disabilities; SSI reform is a legislative priority
- **National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)**: [nami.org](https://www.nami.org) - Mental health advocacy; SSI is critical for members with severe mental illness
- **Autistic Self Advocacy Network (ASAN)**: [autisticadvocacy.org](https://www.autisticadvocacy.org) - Self-advocacy; SSI marriage penalty and asset limit reform priorities
- **National Disability Rights Network (NDRN)**: [ndrn.org](https://www.ndrn.org) - Protection and advocacy organizations in every state

### Anti-Poverty

- **Justice in Aging**: [justiceinaging.org](https://www.justiceinaging.org) - SSI for aged population; legal advocacy
- **National Women's Law Center**: [nwlc.org](https://www.nwlc.org) - SSI's disproportionate importance for women with disabilities
- **Shriver Center on Poverty Law**: Legal advocacy on SSI overpayment and recovery issues

### Legal Advocacy

- **National Organization of Social Security Claimants' Representatives (NOSSCR)**: [nosscr.org](https://www.nosscr.org) - Attorneys and advocates representing SSI applicants
- **Legal Aid organizations**: Local legal aid offices that represent SSI applicants and beneficiaries in administrative proceedings

## Data Sources

- **SSA Data and Statistics**: [ssa.gov/policy/](https://www.ssa.gov/policy/) - Comprehensive program data
- **Census Bureau Current Population Survey**: Poverty data including SSI recipients
- **Supplemental Poverty Measure (SPM)**: Accounts for SSI and other transfers in poverty measurement
- **HUD Point-in-Time Count**: Homelessness data with disability status
- **National Health Interview Survey (NHIS)**: Health status of people with disabilities
- **USDA Food Security Survey**: Food security among SSI recipients
