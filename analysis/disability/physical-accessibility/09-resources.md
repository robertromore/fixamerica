# Physical Accessibility: Resources

## Federal Government Resources

### Laws and Regulations

- **Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990** (42 U.S.C. Section 12101 et seq.) - <https://www.ada.gov/law-and-regs/ada/>
- **ADA Standards for Accessible Design (2010)** - <https://www.ada.gov/law-and-regs/design-standards/2010-stds/>
- **Architectural Barriers Act of 1968** (42 U.S.C. Section 4151 et seq.)
- **Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973** (29 U.S.C. Section 794)
- **Fair Housing Act** (42 U.S.C. Section 3601 et seq.) - Disability-related design and construction requirements
- **ADA Amendments Act of 2008** (P.L. 110-325)

### Federal Agencies

- **U.S. Access Board** - <https://www.access-board.gov/> - Accessibility standards development, technical assistance, ABA enforcement
- **DOJ ADA Information Line** - <https://www.ada.gov/> - (800) 514-0301 (voice), (800) 514-0383 (TTY)
- **DOJ Disability Rights Section** - <https://www.justice.gov/crt/disability-rights-section>
- **GSA Accessibility Program** - <https://www.gsa.gov/real-estate/facilities-management/design-and-construction/accessibility>
- **DOT Accessibility Program** - <https://www.transportation.gov/accessibility>

### Technical Guidance

- **ADA Title III Technical Assistance Manual** - DOJ guidance on public accommodations requirements
- **ADA Best Practices Tool Kit for State and Local Governments** - <https://www.ada.gov/pcatoolkit/toolkitmain.htm>
- **Revised ADA Requirements: Accessible Pools** - DOJ guidance on swimming pool accessibility
- **ADA Primer for Small Businesses** - <https://www.ada.gov/resources/small-business/>

## Legal Resources

### Key Court Cases

- *Olmstead v. L.C.*, 527 U.S. 581 (1999) - Right to community integration
- *Tennessee v. Lane*, 541 U.S. 509 (2004) - ADA Title II enforcement against states
- *PGA Tour, Inc. v. Martin*, 532 U.S. 661 (2001) - ADA Title III and reasonable modification
- *Acheson Hotels v. Laufer*, 601 U.S. 1 (2023) - ADA tester standing (vacated on mootness)
- *Cummings v. Premier Rehab Keller, PLLC*, 596 U.S. 212 (2022) - No emotional distress damages under Section 504

### Litigation Trackers

- Seyfarth Shaw ADA Title III Lawsuit Tracker - <https://www.adatitleiii.com/> - Comprehensive data on federal ADA physical accessibility lawsuits
