# ADA Enforcement: Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation Building (Year 1-2)

### Executive and Administrative Actions

| Action | Lead Actor | Timeline | Prerequisites |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Issue Executive Order establishing Interagency ADA Enforcement Council | White House | Month 1-3 | Presidential commitment |
| Appoint ADA Enforcement Coordinator within Domestic Policy Council | White House | Month 1-3 | None |
| Direct DOJ to prioritize ADA enforcement within existing resources | Attorney General | Month 1-6 | AG confirmation |
| Initiate Title III web accessibility rulemaking (NPRM) | DOJ | Month 3-12 | Interagency coordination |
| Increase proactive compliance reviews using existing DOJ authority | DOJ DRS | Month 3-12 | Staff reallocation |
| Issue updated ADA guidance documents for businesses and governments | DOJ, Access Board | Month 6-12 | Interagency review |
| Launch shared interagency complaint intake portal | DOJ, EEOC, HHS | Month 6-18 | Technology procurement |

### Legislative Actions

| Action | Lead Actor | Timeline | Prerequisites |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Introduce ADA Enforcement Enhancement Act (increased DOJ funding) | Congress | Month 1-6 | Bill drafting |
| Introduce Online Accessibility Act (web accessibility standards) | Congress | Month 1-6 | Industry consultation |
| Introduce State ADA Enforcement Grants Act | Congress | Month 3-12 | State AG coordination |
| Defeat ADA Education and Reform Act (pre-suit notice) | Disability coalition | Ongoing | Coalition mobilization |
| Secure committee hearings on ADA enforcement gaps | Judiciary Committee | Month 6-12 | Committee leadership support |
| Appropriations request for DRS expansion | OMB, Appropriations | Annual cycle | Budget proposal |

### Coalition Building

| Action | Lead Actor | Timeline | Prerequisites |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Convene national ADA enforcement reform coalition | DREDF, NDRN, AAPD | Month 1-3 | Organizational commitment |
| Develop unified reform platform and messaging | Coalition | Month 3-6 | Coalition agreement |
| Launch public education campaign on enforcement gaps | Coalition | Month 6-12 | Funding secured |
| Engage bipartisan congressional champions | Coalition | Month 1-12 | Relationship building |
| Build business allies for compliance-based approach | Coalition, Chamber outreach | Month 6-18 | Identify sympathetic businesses |

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| ADA Education and Reform Act passes | Medium | Very high | Aggressive lobbying; presidential veto commitment |
| Congressional appropriation shortfall | Medium | High | Phase implementation; prioritize highest-impact reforms |
| Judicial decisions further restrict enforcement | Medium | High | Legislative overrides; emphasize statutory clarity |
| Administration change reverses executive actions | Medium | High | Embed reforms in legislation rather than executive orders where possible |
| Business backlash to expanded enforcement | High | Medium | Pair enforcement with technical assistance; certification safe harbor |
| State resistance to grant program conditions | Low | Medium | Flexible grant design; strong state AG relationships |
| Technology outpaces accessibility standards | Medium | Medium | Rapid standards update process; performance-based standards |
| Disability community fragmentation | Low | Medium | Inclusive coalition-building; cross-disability leadership |
