# Healthcare Access for People with Disabilities: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding, researching, and advocating for healthcare access improvements for people with disabilities. Resources span academic research, government reports, legal authorities, advocacy organizations, and practical guides.

## Academic Sources

| Source | Authors/Publisher | Year | Key Findings |
|--------|------------------|------|-------------|
| "Physicians' Perceptions of People with Disability and Their Health Care" | Iezzoni, L.I. et al., *Health Affairs* 40(2) | 2021 | 82.4% of physicians had little or no disability training; many express discomfort treating disabled patients |
| "Physical Accessibility of Primary Care Offices" | Mudrick, N.R. et al., *Health Affairs* 31(7) | 2012 | Only 8.4% of offices had accessible exam tables; 3.6% had accessible weight scales |
| "Disability Competency Training in Medical Education" | Seidel, E. et al., *Academic Medicine* 95(5) | 2020 | Fewer than 20% of medical schools require disability curriculum; average less than 5 hours |
| "COVID-19 Outcomes Among People with IDD" | Landes, S.D. et al., *Disability and Health Journal* 14(2) | 2021 | IDD population experienced 2-3x COVID-19 mortality rate |
| "Diagnostic Overshadowing in Primary Care" | Javaid, A. et al., *British Journal of General Practice* 69(679) | 2019 | 40-80% of health complaints by people with IDD attributed to disability without investigation |
| "Health Disparities Among Adults with Disabilities" | Courtney-Long, E.A. et al., *Disability and Health Journal* 7(4) | 2014 | Women with mobility disabilities 30% less likely to receive mammograms |
| "Health Care Expenditures for Adults with Disabilities" | Anderson, W.L. et al., *Medical Care* 59(10) | 2021 | Adults with disabilities account for 36% of total healthcare spending |
| "Prior Authorization and Disability" | Mulcahy, A.W. et al., *JAMA Internal Medicine* | 2023 | Prior authorization delays disproportionately affect people with complex care needs |
| "Telehealth Accessibility for People with Disabilities" | Lazar, J. et al., *Journal of Medical Internet Research* 25 | 2023 | 40-60% of telehealth platforms have significant accessibility barriers |
| "Community Health Workers and Disability" | Perry, H. et al., *Annual Review of Public Health* 35 | 2014 | CHW models effective for improving healthcare access in underserved populations |
