# Institutionalization: Resources

## Key Legal Authorities

### Federal Statutes

- **Americans with Disabilities Act**, 42 U.S.C. Sections 12101-12213 (1990, as amended)
- **ADA Title II Integration Regulation**, 28 C.F.R. Section 35.130(d)
- **Medicaid Act**, 42 U.S.C. Section 1396 et seq.
- **Section 1915(c) HCBS Waiver**, 42 U.S.C. Section 1396n(c)
- **Section 1915(i) State Plan HCBS**, 42 U.S.C. Section 1396n(i)
- **Section 1915(k) Community First Choice**, 42 U.S.C. Section 1396n(k)
- **Money Follows the Person Demonstration**, Section 6071 of the Deficit Reduction Act of 2005
- **Rehabilitation Act, Section 504**, 29 U.S.C. Section 794
- **Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA)**, 20 U.S.C. Section 1400 et seq.
- **ABLE Act**, 26 U.S.C. Section 529A (2014)
- **Fair Housing Act**, 42 U.S.C. Sections 3601-3619

### Key Supreme Court Cases

- ***Olmstead v. L.C.***, 527 U.S. 581 (1999) -- Unjustified institutionalization constitutes ADA discrimination
- ***O'Connor v. Donaldson***, 422 U.S. 563 (1975) -- Cannot confine non-dangerous individuals who can live safely in community
- ***Pennhurst State School v. Halderman***, 451 U.S. 1 (1981) -- Developmental Disabilities Act does not create enforceable rights; but Medicaid and Section 504 do
- ***Buck v. Bell***, 274 U.S. 200 (1927) -- Upheld forced sterilization (largely repudiated but never formally overruled)
- ***City of Cleburne v. Cleburne Living Center***, 473 U.S. 432 (1985) -- Struck down zoning exclusion of group home for people with intellectual disabilities
- ***Cummings v. Premier Rehab Keller***, 596 U.S. 212 (2022) -- No emotional distress damages under Spending Clause statutes (Section 504, ADA Title II where funding-conditioned)

### Key Circuit Court Decisions

- ***Helen L. v. DiDario***, 46 F.3d 325 (3d Cir. 1995) -- Early application of ADA integration mandate to institutional placement
- ***Pashby v. Delia***, 709 F.3d 307 (4th Cir. 2013) -- Olmstead applies to individuals "at risk" of institutionalization
- ***Steimel v. Wernert***, 823 F.3d 902 (7th Cir. 2016) -- Olmstead applies to transitions between types of residential settings
- ***Lane v. Brown***, 166 F. Supp. 3d 1180 (D. Or. 2015) -- Olmstead applies to employment and day services
