## Legal Resources

### Key Statutes

- Fair Housing Act (as amended 1988), 42 U.S.C. Sections 3601-3619
- Fair Housing Act design requirements, 42 U.S.C. Section 3604(f)(3)(C)
- Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, 29 U.S.C. Section 794
- Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, 42 U.S.C. Sections 12101-12213
- Section 811 Supportive Housing for Persons with Disabilities, 42 U.S.C. Section 8013
- Section 202 Supportive Housing for the Elderly, 12 U.S.C. Section 1701q
- Frank Melville Supportive Housing Investment Act of 2010, Pub. L. 111-374
- Low-Income Housing Tax Credit, 26 U.S.C. Section 42

### Key Regulations

- 24 C.F.R. Part 100 (Fair Housing Act regulations)
- 24 C.F.R. Part 8 (Section 504 regulations for HUD programs)
- 28 C.F.R. Part 35 (ADA Title II regulations)
- ADA Standards for Accessible Design (2010)
- Uniform Federal Accessibility Standards (UFAS)
- CMS HCBS Settings Rule, 42 C.F.R. Section 441.301(c)(4)

### Key Cases

- *Olmstead v. L.C.*, 527 U.S. 581 (1999) (unnecessary institutionalization violates ADA)
- *City of Edmonds v. Oxford House, Inc.*, 514 U.S. 725 (1995) (FHA overrides local zoning for group homes)
- *Texas Department of Housing and Community Affairs v. Inclusive Communities Project*, 576 U.S. 519 (2015) (disparate impact claims cognizable under FHA)
- *Trafficante v. Metropolitan Life Insurance Co.*, 409 U.S. 205 (1972) (broad standing under FHA)
- *Baltimore Neighborhoods, Inc. v. Rommel Builders*, 40 F. Supp. 2d 700 (D. Md. 1999) (FHA design and construction violations)
- *United States v. Quality Built Construction*, No. 1:06-cv-237 (E.D. Tenn. 2011) (FHA design violations in multifamily construction)
- *Giebeler v. M&B Associates*, 343 F.3d 1143 (9th Cir. 2003) (reasonable accommodation in housing)
