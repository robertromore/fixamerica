### Finding Your State AT Program

- **National directory**: <https://catada.info/> (Center on Assistive Technology Act Data Assistance)
- Every state and territory has a federally funded AT program providing free device demonstrations and short-term loans
- Contact your state program to try devices before purchase

## Legal Resources

### Key Statutes

| Law | Citation | AT Relevance |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| Assistive Technology Act | 29 U.S.C. Section 3001 et seq. | Core AT program authorization |
| ADA | 42 U.S.C. Section 12101 et seq. | Accommodations including AT |
| Rehabilitation Act Section 504 | 29 U.S.C. Section 794 | Accommodations in federally funded programs |
| IDEA | 20 U.S.C. Section 1400 et seq. | AT in education |
| 21st Century CVAA | 47 U.S.C. Section 617 et seq. | Communications technology accessibility |
| Medicare DME | 42 U.S.C. Section 1395x(n) | Medicare AT coverage |
| OTC Hearing Aid Act | P.L. 115-52, Section 709 | OTC hearing aid category |

### Key Court Cases

| Case | Citation | AT Significance |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| *Heckler v. Ringer* | 466 U.S. 602 (1984) | Medicare coverage determination procedures |
| *Olmstead v. L.C.* | 527 U.S. 581 (1999) | Community integration; AT as alternative to institutionalization |
| *Toyota Motor Mfg. v. Williams* | 534 U.S. 184 (2002) | Disability definition affecting AT access (narrowed; later broadened by ADAAA) |
