## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| SSDI/SSI reform blocked by Social Security politics | High | Very high | Phase reform; start with SSI (lower political salience) |
| Employer backlash to AI regulation | High | Medium | Build technology industry allies; frame as quality assurance |
| Section 14(c) phase-out without adequate transition funding | Medium | Very high | Condition phase-out on appropriation of transition services |
| EEOC enforcement capacity eroded by administration change | Medium | High | Embed in statute; build bipartisan investigator base |
| Provider organizations resist competitive employment transition | High | Medium | Fund transition; engage progressive providers as champions |
| Tax credit abuse (hire-and-fire schemes) | Medium | Medium | Retention requirements; audit mechanisms |
| Benefit reform perceived as benefit cuts | Medium | High | Frame as "work support reform"; ensure no current beneficiary worse off |
| Economic recession reduces disability employment gains | Medium | High | Countercyclical employment programs; protected job supports |
