# Disability Rights History: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding, researching, and advocating for the recognition and preservation of disability rights history. Resources span academic scholarship, government reports, legal authorities, archival collections, organizations, and educational materials.

## Legal Authorities

| Authority | Citation | Relevance |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Americans with Disabilities Act | 42 U.S.C. Sections 12101-12213 (1990) | Comprehensive disability civil rights law |
| ADA Amendments Act | Public Law 110-325 (2008) | Restored broad ADA coverage after judicial narrowing |
| Section 504, Rehabilitation Act | 29 U.S.C. Section 794 (1973) | First federal disability civil rights protection |
| Education for All Handicapped Children Act (now IDEA) | Public Law 94-142 (1975); 20 U.S.C. Sections 1400-1482 | Right to free appropriate public education |
| Fair Housing Amendments Act | 42 U.S.C. Sections 3601-3619 (1988) | Housing discrimination protection for disability |
| Air Carrier Access Act | 49 U.S.C. Section 41705 (1986) | Air travel nondiscrimination |
| Civil Rights of Institutionalized Persons Act (CRIPA) | 42 U.S.C. Section 1997 (1980) | DOJ authority over institutional conditions |
| *Buck v. Bell* | 274 U.S. 200 (1927) | Supreme Court upheld forced sterilization (never overruled) |
| *Olmstead v. L.C.* | 527 U.S. 581 (1999) | Community integration mandate under ADA |
| *PARC v. Commonwealth of Pennsylvania* | 334 F. Supp. 1257 (E.D. Pa. 1971) | Right to education for children with intellectual disabilities |
| *Wyatt v. Stickney* | 325 F. Supp. 781 (M.D. Ala. 1971) | Constitutional right to adequate institutional conditions |
| *City of Cleburne v. Cleburne Living Center* | 473 U.S. 432 (1985) | Rational basis review for disability discrimination |
| *Tennessee v. Lane* | 541 U.S. 509 (2004) | ADA Title II upheld as applied to court access |
| *Sutton v. United Air Lines* | 527 U.S. 471 (1999) | Narrowed ADA disability definition (overridden by ADAAA) |
| *Toyota v. Williams* | 534 U.S. 184 (2002) | Narrowed "substantially limits" (overridden by ADAAA) |
| *Board of Trustees v. Garrett* | 531 U.S. 356 (2001) | Sovereign immunity bars Title I damages against states |
| *Cummings v. Premier Rehab Keller* | 596 U.S. 212 (2022) | No emotional distress damages under Section 504 |
| UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities | A/RES/61/106 (2006) | International disability rights treaty (U.S. signed, not ratified) |
