# Special Operations: Resources

## Government Sources

### SOCOM Official Documents

- **SOCOM Posture Statement** (Annual) - The SOCOM commander's annual statement to Congress on force structure, budget, and operational priorities. Available through the Senate Armed Services Committee website.
- **SOCOM Factbook** (Annual) - Unclassified overview of SOCOM organization, component commands, and mission areas. Published by SOCOM Public Affairs.
- **DoD Budget Justification: MFP-11** (Annual) - SOCOM's annual budget request, providing line-item detail on SOF-peculiar funding. Available through the DoD Comptroller website.

### Congressional Research Service

- Robinson, Linda, et al. "U.S. Special Operations Forces (SOF): Background and Issues for Congress." Congressional Research Service Report RS21048 (updated regularly).
- Feickert, Andrew. "U.S. Special Operations Forces: Background and Issues for Congress." Congressional Research Service, 2024.
- "Section 127e Authority for Support of Special Operations to Combat Terrorism." Congressional Research Service In Focus, IF11959 (updated regularly).

### Government Accountability Office

- "Special Operations Forces: Additional Actions Needed to Improve Transparency of Funding and Assess Potential to Lessen the Burden on the Force." GAO-15-571 (2015).
- "Military Personnel: DoD Should Improve Its Oversight of Special and Incentive Pay Programs." GAO-17-39 (2017).
- "Special Operations Forces: Several Human Capital Challenges Must Be Addressed to Meet Expanded Role." GAO-06-812 (2006).

### DoD Inspector General

- "Evaluation of the Department of Defense's Handling of Civilian Casualties." DoD OIG, 2023.
- Reports on specific SOF operations and oversight processes (various, some classified).
