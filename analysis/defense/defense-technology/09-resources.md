# Defense Technology: Resources

## Key Government Documents

- **National Defense Strategy (2022)**: Outlines the strategic imperative for innovation and the concept of "integrated deterrence."
- **PPBE Reform Commission Final Report (2024)**: Comprehensive recommendations for fixing the budget process.
- **National Security Commission on Artificial Intelligence (NSCAI) Final Report (2021)**: The blueprint for U.S. AI strategy in national security.
- **DoD Data, Analytics, and AI Adoption Strategy (2023)**: The Pentagon's plan for becoming an AI-enabled organization.

## Books

- **"The Kill Chain: Defending America in the Future of High-Tech Warfare"** by Christian Brose. (The definitive critique of the current system and argument for autonomous systems).
- **"Unit X: How the Pentagon and Silicon Valley Are Transforming the Future of War"** by Raj M. Shah and Christopher Kirchhoff. (Inside story of DIU).
- **"Four Battlegrounds: Power in the Age of Artificial Intelligence"** by Paul Scharre. (Analysis of the AI competition with China).
- **"Army of None: Autonomous Weapons and the Future of War"** by Paul Scharre. (Deep dive into the ethics and strategy of LAWS).

## Think Tanks and Research Organizations

- **Center for Security and Emerging Technology (CSET)**: Georgetown-based think tank providing data-driven analysis on AI and advanced tech.
- **Center for a New American Security (CNAS)**: Leading work on the "American Way of War" and defense technology strategy.
- **Hudson Institute**: Center for Defense Concepts and Technology.
- **Special Competitive Studies Project (SCSP)**: Successor to the NSCAI, focused on long-term competitiveness.
- **Atlantic Council**: Defense Program.

## News and Analysis

- **Defense One**: "Tech & Grim" section.
- **Breaking Defense**: Coverage of networks, cyber, and space.
- **War on the Rocks**: Expert commentary on strategy and technology.
- **C4ISRNET**: Dedicated coverage of defense IT and networks.
