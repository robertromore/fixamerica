## Legal References

### Statutes

- Defense Base Closure and Realignment Act of 1990, Pub. L. No. 101-510, Part A of Title XXIX (10 U.S.C. ch. 159 note).
- Defense Authorization Amendments and Base Closure and Realignment Act of 1988, Pub. L. No. 100-526.
- 10 U.S.C. Section 2687: Base closures and realignments (threshold and process requirements).
- Comprehensive Environmental Response, Compensation, and Liability Act (CERCLA), 42 U.S.C. Sections 9601-9675.
- National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), 42 U.S.C. Sections 4321-4347.
- Community Environmental Response Facilitation Act (CERFA), 42 U.S.C. Section 9620(h).
- Defense Environmental Restoration Program (DERP), 10 U.S.C. Sections 2700-2710.
- Pacific Deterrence Initiative (PDI), authorized in NDAA FY2021, Section 1251.

### Key Legal Decisions

- *Dalton v. Specter*, 511 U.S. 462 (1994): Supreme Court held that BRAC commission decisions are not subject to judicial review under the Administrative Procedure Act.
- *National Federation of Federal Employees v. Cheney*, 883 F.2d 1038 (D.C. Cir. 1989): Upheld the constitutionality of the BRAC process against separation of powers challenges.
