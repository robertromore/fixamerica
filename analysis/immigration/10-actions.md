# Immigration: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Immigration policy affects millions of people and shapes American society. Citizens can influence policy through advocacy, electoral participation, direct service, and community building. Whether supporting reform or defending immigrant neighbors, engagement matters.

## Counter Misinformation

### Common Myths

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| **"Open borders"** | Extensive enforcement system |
| **"Just come legally"** | Decades-long waits, no line for most |
| **"Criminals"** | Lower crime rates |
| **"Taking jobs"** | Complementary roles |
| **"Don't pay taxes"** | $400+ billion in taxes |

### Effective Communication

| Approach | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **Personal stories** | Humanize issue |
| **Economic facts** | Counter cost arguments |
| **Local examples** | Make it relevant |
| **Shared values** | Family, work, community |
| **Avoid jargon** | Accessible language |
