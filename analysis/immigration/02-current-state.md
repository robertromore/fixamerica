# Immigration: Current State

## Overview

The U.S. immigration system operates far outside its designed capacity, with every component showing signs of severe strain. Legal immigration faces multi-decade backlogs, border processing is overwhelmed, immigration courts have 3.5+ million pending cases, and 11 million people live without legal status. Recent years have seen record border encounters alongside policy whiplash between administrations.
