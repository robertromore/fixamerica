# Immigration: Solutions

## Overview

Fixing the immigration system requires addressing multiple dimensions simultaneously: clearing backlogs and modernizing legal immigration, creating a functional asylum system, resolving the status of unauthorized immigrants, establishing effective but humane enforcement, and building integration capacity. Comprehensive reform has failed repeatedly, but the components are well-understood.
