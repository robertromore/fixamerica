# Legal Immigration: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can influence legal immigration policy through advocacy, electoral engagement, direct support for immigrants navigating the system, and counter-misinformation efforts. Whether supporting family reunification, employment-based reform, or system modernization, informed engagement makes a difference.

## Counter Misinformation

### Common Myths

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| **"They can just come legally"** | Decades-long waits, no line for most |
| **"Taking American jobs"** | Fill gaps, create jobs |
| **"Don't pay taxes"** | Pay billions in taxes |
| **"Burden on services"** | Net fiscal positive long-term |
| **"Don't assimilate"** | Integration proceeding well |

|----------|--------|
