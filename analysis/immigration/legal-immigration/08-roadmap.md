# Legal Immigration: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing legal immigration reform requires navigating intense political opposition, building coalitions across traditional divides, and sequencing changes to build trust. This roadmap outlines phases from immediate administrative improvements through comprehensive legislative reform, recognizing that different political scenarios enable different approaches.

## Scenario Planning

### Scenario A: Unified Pro-Reform Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative improvements | Months 1-6 |
| **Near-term** | Targeted legislation | Year 1 |
| **Medium-term** | Comprehensive reform | Years 1-2 |
| **Implementation** | System transformation | Years 2-5 |

### Scenario B: Divided Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative actions | Months 1-6 |
| **Ongoing** | Bipartisan negotiations | Years 1-4 |
| **Opportunistic** | Targeted bills when possible | As opportunities arise |

### Scenario C: Restrictionist Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Defensive** | Prevent rollbacks | Immediate |
| **Litigation** | Challenge restrictions | Ongoing |
| **State action** | State-level support | Ongoing |
| **Preparation** | Position for next opportunity | Long-term |
