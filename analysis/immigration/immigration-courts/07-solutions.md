# Immigration Courts: Solutions

## Overview

Comprehensive immigration court reform requires structural independence, adequate resources, due process protections, and procedural improvements. The most fundamental reform—creating an independent Article I immigration court—would address the root cause of political control while other reforms can provide incremental improvements.

## Structural Independence

### Article I Immigration Court

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Constitutional basis** | Article I tribunal |
| **Independence** | Separate from DOJ |
| **Judicial status** | True judges |
| **Tenure** | Fixed terms or lifetime |
| **Appointments** | Merit-based selection |

### Court Structure

| Component | Design |
|-----------|--------|
| **Trial division** | Immigration judges |
| **Appellate division** | Appeals board |

### Appointment Process
