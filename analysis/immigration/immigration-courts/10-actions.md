# Immigration Courts: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can engage with immigration court reform through advocacy for structural change, support for representation programs, electoral participation, and direct assistance. Whether supporting court independence or volunteering as a court observer, informed engagement can improve this system.

## Counter Misinformation

### Common Misconceptions

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| **"Fair courts"** | Massive due process problems |
| **"Just follow law"** | Political influence |
| **"Plenty of time"** | Years of backlog |
| **"Lawyers available"** | Many unrepresented |

### Effective Responses

| Approach | Method |
|----------|--------|
| **Data** | Backlog, outcomes |
| **Stories** | Real cases |
| **Comparisons** | Other court systems |
| **Expert voices** | Judges, lawyers |
