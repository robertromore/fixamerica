# Asylum and Refugees: Current State

## Overview

The US asylum and refugee system faces unprecedented pressure, with record asylum applications, a massive court backlog, inconsistent adjudication, and refugee admissions below authorized ceilings. The system struggles to provide timely, fair decisions while managing border arrivals and meeting international protection obligations.
