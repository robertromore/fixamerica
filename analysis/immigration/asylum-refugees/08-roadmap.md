# Asylum and Refugees: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing asylum and refugee reform requires immediate action to address the crisis, investment in capacity, structural reforms, and international cooperation. This roadmap outlines phases recognizing different political scenarios and the urgency of the protection mission.

## Scenario Planning

### Scenario A: Pro-Protection Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Processing surge, refugee ceiling | Months 1-6 |
| **Near-term** | Representation, court capacity | Years 1-2 |
| **Medium-term** | Court independence, regional | Years 2-4 |
| **Long-term** | Sustainable system | Years 5-10 |

### Scenario B: Divided Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative improvements | Months 1-6 |
| **Ongoing** | Bipartisan negotiations | Years 1-4 |
| **Opportunistic** | Targeted reforms | As possible |

### Scenario C: Restrictionist Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Defensive** | Protect programs | Immediate |
| **Litigation** | Challenge restrictions | Ongoing |
| **Preparation** | Position for future | Long-term |
