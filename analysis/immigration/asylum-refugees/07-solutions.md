# Asylum and Refugees: Solutions

## Overview

Fixing the asylum and refugee system requires addressing the backlog crisis, ensuring timely and fair adjudication, expanding legal representation, meeting refugee admission commitments, and developing regional alternatives that reduce border pressure while maintaining protection. Solutions must balance protection obligations with orderly processing.
