# Immigration Detention: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming the immigration detention system requires phased implementation addressing immediate humanitarian concerns, building alternatives, transitioning away from private facilities, and establishing sustainable oversight.

## Phase 1: Immediate Actions (Year 1)

### Emergency Reforms

| Action | Timeline | Lead Agency |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Medical standards** | 0-3 months | ICE Health Service Corps |
| **Release vulnerable** | 0-3 months | ICE ERO |
| **Bond reform** | 0-6 months | DOJ/EOIR |
| **Solitary limits** | 0-6 months | ICE Detention Management |
| **Grievance process** | 3-6 months | DHS CRCL |

### Alternative Expansion

| Program | Timeline | Target |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Case management pilots** | 0-6 months | 5 cities |
| **NGO partnerships** | 3-9 months | 20 organizations |
| **Legal orientation** | 0-12 months | All detained |
| **Family programs** | 6-12 months | All families |

### Population Reduction

| Mechanism | Timeline | Goal |
|-----------|----------|------|
| **Parole presumption** | 0-3 months | Asylum seekers |
| **Bond reviews** | 0-6 months | All detained |
| **Time limit policy** | 6-12 months | 90-day max |
| **ATD enrollment** | 0-12 months | Double capacity |

## Phase 2: System Transformation (Years 2-3)

### Contract Phase-Out

| Step | Timeline | Impact |
|------|----------|--------|
| **No new contracts** | Year 2 | Immediate cap |
| **Non-renewal priority** | Year 2-3 | Worst facilities first |
| **Transition planning** | Year 2 | Workforce support |
| **Capacity reduction** | Year 2-3 | 50% reduction |

### Standards Enforcement

## Phase 3: Structural Reform (Years 4-5)

### Legislative Changes

| Reform | Timeline | Vehicle |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **End mandatory detention** | Year 4 | INA amendment |
| **Right to counsel** | Year 4-5 | Authorization |
| **Judicial review** | Year 4 | INA amendment |
| **Time limits** | Year 4-5 | Statutory cap |

### System Redesign

| Element | Timeline | Approach |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Minimum detention** | Year 4-5 | Policy shift |
| **Civil processing** | Year 4-5 | Non-custodial default |
| **Community-based** | Year 4-5 | Primary model |
| **Detention exceptions** | Year 4-5 | Narrow use |
