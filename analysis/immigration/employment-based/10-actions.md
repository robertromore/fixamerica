# Employment-Based Immigration: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can engage with employment-based immigration through advocacy for reform, employer practices, support for affected workers, and electoral participation. Whether advocating for green card backlog relief or better worker protections, informed engagement shapes policy.

## Understanding the Issues

### Learn the Basics

| Topic | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| **H-1B program** | Main temporary visa |
| **Green card backlog** | Decades-long waits |
| **Per-country caps** | Source of disparity |
| **Worker protections** | Exploitation concerns |
| **STEM immigration** | Innovation, competitiveness |

### Key Distinctions

| Distinction | Importance |
|-------------|------------|
| **Temporary vs. permanent** | Different pathways |
| **Employer vs. worker** | Who is protected |
| **Cap vs. cap-exempt** | Availability |
| **Lottery vs. merit** | Selection method |
