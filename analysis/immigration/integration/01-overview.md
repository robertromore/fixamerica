# Immigrant Integration: Overview

## Summary

Immigrant integration encompasses the policies, programs, and processes through which immigrants become full participants in American society. Effective integration benefits both immigrants and receiving communities through economic contributions, civic participation, and social cohesion.

## Scope

This subtopic covers:

- **Naturalization** - Citizenship pathways and barriers
- **Civic integration** - Political participation and rights
- **Economic integration** - Workforce and entrepreneurship
- **Language access** - English learning and multilingual services
- **Social integration** - Community ties and belonging
- **Educational integration** - Access and attainment
- **Health access** - Healthcare for immigrant populations
