## Advocacy Actions

### Contact Elected Officials

| Target | Message Focus |
|--------|---------------|
| **Congress** | Federal integration funding, citizenship reform |
| **State legislators** | Tuition equity, driver's licenses, benefits access |
| **Local officials** | Welcoming policies, language access |
| **School boards** | ELL support, family engagement |

### Effective Communication

| Method | Tips |
|--------|------|
| **Phone calls** | Most impactful, be specific |
| **Constituent meetings** | Request in-district |
| **Personal stories** | Humanize issues |
| **Town halls** | Public questions |

### Sample Messages

**On Citizenship Access:**

> "I urge you to support reducing naturalization fees and processing times. Citizenship strengthens our communities and economy. Please support the New Americans Act."

**On English Learning:**

> "Thousands of immigrants want to learn English but face years-long waitlists. Please support increased funding for adult education to meet this demand."

**On Welcoming Policies:**

> "Our community benefits when immigrants can fully participate. Please support welcoming policies including language access and municipal ID programs."
