# Unauthorized Immigration: Stakeholders

## Primary Stakeholders

### Unauthorized Individuals

| Segment | Characteristics | Interests |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Long-term residents** | 15+ years, deep roots | Stability, legalization |
| **DACA recipients** | Young, educated, working | Path to citizenship |
| **Recent arrivals** | Less integrated | Status, work |
| **TPS holders** | Designated country origins | Permanent solution |
| **Visa overstays** | Often professionals | Adjustment of status |

### Specific Populations

| Population | Number | Key Concerns |
|------------|--------|--------------|
| **DACA recipients** | 530,000 | Program continuity |
| **US citizen children** | 4.4 million | Family unity |
| **Unauthorized parents** | 3+ million | Deportation fear |
| **Essential workers** | 2+ million | Workplace protection |
| **Elderly unauthorized** | 1+ million | Healthcare, aging |

### Family Members

| Family Type | Stake |
|-------------|-------|
| **US citizen children** | Keep parents |
| **US citizen spouses** | Family unity |
| **LPR family** | Protect relatives |
| **Unauthorized families** | Shared fate |
| **Extended family** | Separation |
