# Unauthorized Immigration: Solutions

## Overview

Addressing the unauthorized population requires a multi-faceted approach: protecting vulnerable populations through executive action, pursuing legislative legalization, strengthening enforcement systems, and creating adequate legal pathways to reduce future unauthorized migration. No enforcement-only solution is practical for 11 million people; comprehensive reform remains necessary.
