# Unauthorized Immigration: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing solutions for the unauthorized population requires different strategies depending on political conditions. This roadmap outlines approaches for various scenarios, from comprehensive legislative reform to defensive administrative protection.

## Scenario Planning

### Scenario A: Reform-Friendly Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative protection | Months 1-6 |
| **Near-term** | Legislative push | Years 1-2 |
| **Medium-term** | Program implementation | Years 2-5 |
| **Long-term** | Full integration | Years 5-10 |

### Scenario B: Divided Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative action | Months 1-6 |
| **Near-term** | Targeted legislation | Years 1-4 |
| **Ongoing** | Bipartisan negotiation | Continuous |
| **Opportunistic** | Piecemeal reform | As possible |

### Scenario C: Hostile Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Defensive** | Litigation, protection | Immediate |
| **State action** | Blue state policies | Ongoing |
| **Community** | Support networks | Ongoing |
| **Preparation** | Position for future | Long-term |
