# Immigration Enforcement: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing enforcement reform requires immediate administrative changes, medium-term program development, and long-term system transformation through comprehensive immigration reform. This roadmap outlines phases recognizing different political scenarios and the need for both improved enforcement and humane treatment.

## Scenario Planning

### Scenario A: Reform-Oriented Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Priorities, protections | Months 1-6 |
| **Near-term** | Program development | Years 1-2 |
| **Medium-term** | Legalization | Years 2-4 |
| **Long-term** | Sustainable system | Years 5-10 |

### Scenario B: Divided Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative changes | Months 1-6 |
| **Ongoing** | Bipartisan negotiation | Years 1-4 |

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
