# Immigration Enforcement: Stakeholders

## Overview

Enforcement stakeholders include ICE personnel conducting operations, unauthorized immigrants subject to enforcement, their families and communities, local law enforcement with cooperation decisions, employers facing accountability, and advocacy organizations on all sides. Their competing interests drive the ongoing policy debates.

## Power Analysis

### High Influence, High Interest

| Stakeholder | Source of Power |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **ICE/DHS** | Implementation authority |
| **Congress** | Funding, legislation |
| **Courts** | Policy constraints |
| **Employers** | Economic influence |

| Stakeholder | Limitation |
|-------------|------------|
| **Unauthorized immigrants** | No political voice |
| **Mixed families** | Fear of visibility |

| **Legal services** | Overwhelmed capacity |

| Actor | Veto Power |
