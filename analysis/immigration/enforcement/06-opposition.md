# Immigration Enforcement: Opposition

## Overview

Opposition to current enforcement approaches comes from multiple directions: those who believe enforcement is too aggressive and harmful, those who believe it's insufficient, those with constitutional and civil liberties concerns, and communities resisting specific tactics. Understanding these perspectives is essential for developing sustainable policy.

| Concern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Family separation** | US citizen children |
| **Community fear** | Chilling effects |
