# Immigration Enforcement: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can engage with enforcement policy through advocacy, community support, electoral participation, and direct action. Whether supporting stronger enforcement or protecting vulnerable neighbors, informed engagement shapes policy outcomes.

## Understanding the Issues

### Learn the Basics

| Topic | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| **ICE operations** | Understand how enforcement works |
| **Priorities** | Who is being targeted |
| **Rights** | What protections exist |
| **Local policies** | Your community's approach |
| **Data** | Counter misinformation |

### Key Distinctions

| Distinction | Importance |
|-------------|------------|
| **Civil vs. criminal** | Different processes |
| **ERO vs. HSI** | Different functions |
| **Detention vs. release** | Different consequences |
| **Priorities vs. all** | Policy differences |

## Counter Misinformation

### Common Myths

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| **"Criminals"** | Most are not |
| **"Open borders"** | Extensive enforcement |
| **"Invasion"** | Migration, not military |
| **"All being deported"** | Resource limits |

### Effective Responses

| Approach | Method |
|----------|--------|
| **Data** | Actual statistics |
| **Stories** | Real people affected |
| **Law** | What rights exist |
| **Questions** | Encourage reflection |
