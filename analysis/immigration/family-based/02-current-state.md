### Impact of Expiration

| Effect | Description |
|--------|-------------|

| **No current option** | For F2A waiting |

## Aging Out Problem

### Child Status Protection Act (CSPA)

| Element | Protection |
|---------|------------|
| **Purpose** | Prevent aging out at 21 |
| **Calculation** | Subtract petition processing time |
| **Limitation** | Must seek to acquire within 1 year |
| **Gaps** | Doesn't cover all situations |

### Continuing Problem

| Situation | Risk |
|-----------|------|
| **Long waits** | Children turn 21 |
| **Category change** | F2A to F2B (longer wait) |
| **Lost eligibility** | No longer qualifies |
| **Separate petitions** | Restart process |
