# Family-Based Immigration: Stakeholders

## Primary Stakeholders

### Prospective Immigrants

| Category | Characteristics |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Spouses of LPRs** | F2A, years waiting |
| **Adult children** | F1, F2B, decades waiting |
| **Parents** | IR-5, relatively quick |
| **Siblings** | F4, longest waits |

### US Sponsors

| Type | Interest |
|------|----------|
| **Citizens** | Reunite with family |
| **LPRs** | Bring spouse, children |
| **Naturalized** | Origin country family |
| **Native-born** | Spouse's family |

### Mixed-Status Families

| Family Type | Stake |
|-------------|-------|
| **Citizen children** | Keep parents |
| **LPR with unauthorized spouse** | Legalize spouse |
| **Unauthorized in family** | Path to status |
