# Family-Based Immigration: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can engage with family-based immigration through advocacy for reform, supporting affected families, electoral participation, and navigating the system for their own families. Whether advocating for backlog relief or helping a neighbor understand their options, informed engagement makes a difference.

## Counter Misinformation

### Common Myths

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| **"Chain migration"** | Limited categories, decades wait |
| **"Unlimited"** | Strict numerical caps |
| **"Easy process"** | Complex, lengthy |
| **"Bypass line"** | No line-jumping |
