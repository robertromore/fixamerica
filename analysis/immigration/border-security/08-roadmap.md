# Border Security: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing border security reform requires immediate action to address the current crisis, medium-term investment in infrastructure and personnel, and long-term transformation of the migration management system. This roadmap outlines phases recognizing different political scenarios and resource requirements.

## Scenario Planning

### Scenario A: Unified Pro-Reform Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Processing surge, diplomacy | Months 1-6 |
| **Near-term** | Infrastructure, personnel | Years 1-2 |
| **Medium-term** | System transformation | Years 2-4 |
| **Long-term** | Sustainable management | Years 5-10 |

### Scenario B: Divided Government

| Phase | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Immediate** | Administrative actions | Months 1-6 |
| **Ongoing** | Bipartisan negotiations | Years 1-4 |
