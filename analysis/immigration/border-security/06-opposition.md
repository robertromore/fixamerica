# Border Security: Opposition

## Overview

Opposition to border security reform comes from multiple directions: those who oppose any increase in enforcement, those who believe current measures are insufficient, those who oppose specific approaches like barriers or technology, and those who prioritize other values over security. Understanding these perspectives is essential for developing effective, sustainable policy.
