# Misinformation: Stakeholders

## Who Is Harmed

### General Public

| Harm | Manifestation |
|------|---------------|
| **Health decisions** | Vaccine refusal, fake treatments |
| **Political decisions** | Misinformed voting |
| **Financial harm** | Scams, bad investments |
| **Social division** | Family, community conflict |
| **Mental health** | Anxiety, conspiracy thinking |

### Targeted Communities

| Community | Specific Harms |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Immigrants** | False claims fuel discrimination |
| **LGBTQ+** | "Groomer" narratives |
| **Religious minorities** | Conspiracy theories |
| **Racial minorities** | False crime narratives |
| **Election workers** | Harassment, threats |

### Democracy

| Harm | Mechanism |
|------|-----------|
| **Voter manipulation** | False claims affect votes |
| **Delegitimization** | Election denial |
| **Polarization** | Different factual realities |
| **Violence** | January 6, targeted attacks |

### Institutions

| Institution | Harm |
|-------------|------|
| **Public health** | Undermined pandemic response |
| **Elections** | Questioned legitimacy |
| **Science** | Climate, vaccine denial |
| **Journalism** | "Fake news" attacks |

## Who Benefits

### Domestic Misinformation Actors

| Actor | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| **Politicians** | Electoral advantage |
| **Partisan media** | Audience, revenue |
| **Grifters** | Money (donations, sales) |
| **Influencers** | Attention, followers |

### Foreign Actors

| Actor | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| **Russia** | Weakened U.S., division |
| **China** | Distraction from own issues |
| **Iran** | Middle East narratives |

### Platforms (Partially)

| Benefit | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Engagement** | Misinformation engages |
| **Avoiding regulation** | Claim neutrality |
| **Low costs** | Moderation expensive |

### Industries

| Industry | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Fossil fuels** | Climate doubt |
| **Tobacco (historically)** | Health doubt |
| **Pharmaceuticals (some)** | Competitor attacks |

## Stakeholder Dynamics

### Power Analysis

| Stakeholder | Power Level | Direction |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Platforms** | Very high | Mixed (profit vs. reputation) |
| **Federal government** | High (unused) | Constrained by 1A |
| **Misinformation actors** | Medium | Pro-misinformation |
| **Fact-checkers** | Low | Anti-misinformation |
| **General public** | Low | Harmed |

### Tensions

| Tension | Stakes |
|---------|--------|
| **Free speech vs. truth** | Fundamental values |
| **Platform profit vs. responsibility** | Business models |
| **Speed vs. accuracy** | Information dynamics |
| **Government role vs. 1A** | Constitutional limits |
