### Foundational Studies

| Study | Authors | Finding |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **"False news spreads faster"** | Vosoughi et al. (2018) | Lies spread 6x faster |

| **"The Spreading of Misinformation"** | Del Vicario et al. | Echo chamber effects |
