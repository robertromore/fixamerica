# Local Journalism: Solutions

## Tax Incentives

### Subscriber Tax Credit

**Local Journalism Sustainability Act Model**

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Amount** | Up to $250/year tax credit for subscriptions |
| **Eligible outlets** | Local newspapers and digital news |
| **Requirements** | Primary coverage of local news |
| **Limitation** | Must be paying subscribers |
| **Duration** | 5 years with review |

**Expected Impact**

- Estimated $1.7 billion annually in subscriber support
- Could stabilize thousands of local outlets
- No government content involvement

### Employer Payroll Tax Credit

**Local Journalist Jobs Act Model**

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Amount** | 50% of first $50,000 wages (Year 1), declining |
| **Eligible employers** | News organizations with <750 employees |
| **Requirements** | Journalists covering local news |
| **Duration** | 5-year phase-out |

**Expected Impact**

- Subsidize ~35,000 journalist positions
- Particularly help small/medium outlets
- Encourage hiring over cuts

### Small Business Advertising Credit

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Amount** | Tax credit for small business local news advertising |
| **Cap** | Up to $5,000/year per business |
| **Eligible** | Businesses under $5 million revenue |
| **Media** | Local newspapers, broadcast, digital |

**Expected Impact**

- Restore local advertising incentive
- Help small businesses and local news together
- Estimated $2 billion in advertising restoration

## Platform Accountability

### Journalism Competition and Preservation Act (JCPA)

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Mechanism** | Allow publishers to collectively negotiate with platforms |
| **Antitrust exemption** | Temporary safe harbor for joint bargaining |
| **Arbitration** | Binding arbitration if negotiations fail |
| **Coverage** | Digital platforms with significant market power |

**Expected Impact**

- Enable news industry to negotiate as bloc
- Platform payments to publishers
- Australian model generated $200+ million/year
