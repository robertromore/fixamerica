## Getting Started Checklist

### Week One

- [ ] Subscribe to your local newspaper (print or digital)
- [ ] Find and follow local news on social media
- [ ] Identify your local nonprofit newsrooms
- [ ] Learn who your state legislators are

### Month One

- [ ] Make a donation to local journalism nonprofit
- [ ] Attend one local government meeting
- [ ] Share three local stories on social media
- [ ] Write or call one elected official about local news

### Year One

- [ ] Become a sustaining member of local nonprofit
- [ ] Recruit three friends/family to subscribe
- [ ] Testify at a public hearing or write op-ed
- [ ] Join or start a local news support organization
