| Feature | U.S. | Peer Countries |
|---------|------|----------------|
| **Dedicated funding** | No | Often license fee |
| **Political insulation** | Limited | Generally stronger |

## Governance

### CPB Board

| Characteristic | Current Status |
|----------------|----------------|
| **Members** | 9 |
| **Appointment** | Presidential |
| **Terms** | 6 years |
| **Balance requirement** | No more than 5 from one party |
| **Independence** | Statutorily required |

### Editorial Independence

| Safeguard | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| **Statutory prohibition** | No government editorial control |
| **Firewall** | CPB cannot produce content |
| **Decentralization** | Stations make programming decisions |
| **Professional standards** | Journalistic ethics |

### Challenges to Independence

| Challenge | Source |
|-----------|--------|
| **Political appointments** | Board politicization |
| **Funding threats** | Political leverage |
| **Public perception** | Accusations of bias |
| **Self-censorship** | Avoiding controversy |
