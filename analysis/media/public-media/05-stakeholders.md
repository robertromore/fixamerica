## Power Analysis

### Most Powerful Stakeholders

| Rank | Stakeholder | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 1 | **Congress** | Appropriations power |
| 2 | **President** | Board appointments, veto |
| 3 | **CPB Board** | Distribution decisions |
| 4 | **Major stations** | Influence, resources |
| 5 | **NPR/PBS** | Programming, brand |

### Least Powerful Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Why Weak |
|-------------|----------|
| **Small stations** | Limited resources, voice |
| **General public** | Diffuse, unorganized |
| **Staff** | Limited advocacy role |
| **Communities served** | No direct power |

| Issue | Tension |
