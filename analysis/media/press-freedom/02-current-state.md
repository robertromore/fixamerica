## Legal Landscape

### Federal Protections

| Protection | Status |
|------------|--------|
| **First Amendment** | Strong constitutional protection |
| **Federal shield law** | Does not exist |
| **Reporter's privilege** | Limited; varies by circuit |
| **FOIA** | Exists but weakly enforced |
| **Privacy Act** | Some journalist exemptions |

### State Shield Laws

| Category | States |
|----------|--------|
| **Absolute shield** | 10 states (CA, NY, etc.) |
| **Qualified shield** | 30 states |
| **No shield law** | 10 states |
| **Federal courts** | No uniform protection |

### Recent Legal Developments

| Development | Impact |
|-------------|--------|
| **Branzburg v. Hayes (1972)** | No constitutional reporter's privilege |
| **DOJ guidelines (revised 2021)** | Limits on subpoenaing journalists |
| **Apple/Google data demands (2021)** | Revealed surveillance of journalists |
| **Assange prosecution** | Potential precedent for journalists |
