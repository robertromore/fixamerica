# Press Freedom: Implementation Roadmap

## Strategic Framework

### Core Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **First Amendment foundation** | Build on constitutional protection |
| **Bipartisan approach** | Press freedom not partisan |
| **Comprehensive protection** | Legal, physical, economic |
| **Adaptive strategy** | Respond to new threats |

### Theory of Change

```
Legal protections + Safety measures + Economic support + Public understanding
                              ↓
              Journalists can work without fear
                              ↓
               Accountability journalism thrives
                              ↓
                  Democracy strengthened
```

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Partisan opposition** | Bipartisan framing |
| **DOJ resistance** | Negotiate narrow exceptions |
| **Administration change** | Codify in statute |
| **Public distrust** | Public education |

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
