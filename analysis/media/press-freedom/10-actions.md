# Press Freedom: Actions

## For Individuals

### Support Press Freedom

| Action | How | Impact |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Subscribe to news** | Pay for journalism | Economic support |
| **Donate to press freedom orgs** | RCFP, CPJ, FPF | Advocacy funding |
| **Share quality journalism** | Amplify important work | Visibility |
| **Defend journalists under attack** | Speak up | Social support |

### Political Engagement

| Action | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **Contact Congress** | Federal shield law, FOIA reform |
| **Contact state legislators** | State shield laws |
| **Vote for press freedom** | Candidates who support press |
| **Attend town halls** | Raise press freedom issues |

### Personal Security (for Sources)

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Use Signal** | Encrypted communication |
| **Know your rights** | Understand protections |
| **Consult lawyer before leaking** | Protect yourself |
| **Understand shield law coverage** | Know what's protected |

## Getting Started Checklist

### Week One

- [ ] Contact members of Congress about federal shield law
- [ ] Subscribe to or donate to a news organization
- [ ] Follow a press freedom organization
- [ ] Learn about your state's shield law

### Month One

- [ ] Donate to press freedom organization
- [ ] Contact state legislators if state lacks shield law
- [ ] Share press freedom issue with friends/family
- [ ] Attend a journalism-related event

### Year One

- [ ] Actively support shield law passage
- [ ] Engage in multiple advocacy activities
- [ ] Help build awareness in community
- [ ] Consider volunteering with press freedom org
