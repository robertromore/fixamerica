## Getting Started Checklist

### Week One

- [ ] Identify independent and nonprofit outlets in your area
- [ ] Subscribe to at least one independent outlet
- [ ] Follow a media reform organization
- [ ] Learn who your FCC commissioner is

### Month One

- [ ] File comment in open FCC proceeding
- [ ] Contact one legislator about media ownership
- [ ] Share information about consolidation with friends
- [ ] Attend a local advocacy meeting

### Year One

- [ ] Join or support a media reform organization
- [ ] Participate in local ownership campaign
- [ ] Testify at a hearing or write op-ed
- [ ] Help build coalition in your community
