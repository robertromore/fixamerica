## Radio Ownership

### Post-1996 Consolidation

The Telecommunications Act of 1996 eliminated national ownership caps, enabling massive consolidation.

**Major Radio Owners**

| Owner | Stations | Revenue | Status |
|-------|----------|---------|--------|
| **iHeartMedia** | 850+ | $3.5B | Emerged from bankruptcy |
| **Cumulus Media** | 400+ | $900M | Emerged from bankruptcy |
| **Townsquare Media** | 350+ | $450M | Mid-market focus |
| **Audacy** | 230+ | $1.3B | Formerly Entercom |
| **Beasley Broadcast** | 60+ | $200M | Family owned |

### Market-Level Concentration

| Metric | Pre-1996 | Current |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Max stations/owner/market | 2 AM + 2 FM | 8 stations |
| Typical major market ownership | Many owners | 2-3 dominant |
| Independent stations | Common | Rare |

### Consequences

| Outcome | Evidence |
|---------|----------|
| **Local programming cuts** | Syndicated/national content dominates |
| **News staff elimination** | Many stations have no local news |
| **Playlist homogenization** | Same songs across markets |
| **Financial instability** | Multiple bankruptcies |

| Rule | Status |
|------|--------|

### Opacity Challenges

| Issue | Description |
|-------|-------------|

| **Sidecar arrangements** | Circumvent ownership limits |

### Disclosure Requirements

| Requirement | Status |
|-------------|--------|
| **FCC broadcast ownership** | Public filings required |
| **SEC public company** | Financial disclosure |
| **Private company** | No disclosure required |
| **Hedge fund holdings** | Limited disclosure |

## Geographic Patterns

### News Deserts and Consolidation

| Pattern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Rural areas** | Most consolidated; many news deserts |
| **Small cities** | Often single owner dominance |
| **Suburban areas** | Chain ownership common |
| **Major metros** | More competition, but declining |

### Regional Monopolies

| Region | Owner | Control |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Some state capitals** | Single chain | All major outlets |
| **Mid-sized cities** | Single TV owner | Local TV market |
| **Rural counties** | No remaining owner | News desert |
