# Media Consolidation: Solutions

## Ownership Limits

### Restore Broadcast Ownership Caps

**National Ownership Cap**

| Proposal | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| **TV household reach** | Cap at 25% of national households |
| **Station count** | Maximum stations per owner |
| **Review process** | Regular FCC review |

**Local Ownership Rules**

| Proposal | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| **TV duopoly** | Prohibit two stations in same market |
| **Radio limits** | Reduce per-market caps |
| **Cross-ownership** | Restore newspaper-broadcast prohibition |

### Newspaper Ownership Limits

| Proposal | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Market concentration caps** | Prevent local monopolies |
| **Chain size limits** | Cap papers per owner |
| **Hedge fund restrictions** | Special rules for financial buyers |

### Digital Platform Limits

| Proposal | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Structural separation** | Separate distribution from content |
| **Interoperability requirements** | Reduce network effects |
| **Market share caps** | Limit dominant positions |

## International Models

### Successful Approaches

| Country | Model | Relevance |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| **Germany** | Strong public media; ownership limits | Quality maintained |
| **UK** | Ofcom ownership review | Proactive regulation |
| **Canada** | CRTC ownership rules | Local content requirements |
| **Australia** | News Media Bargaining Code | Platform payment |

### Lessons

| Lesson | Application |
|--------|-------------|
| **Limits preserve diversity** | Rules work |
| **Public investment helps** | Not just regulation |
| **Review mechanisms matter** | Ongoing monitoring |
| **Independence structures** | Avoid political control |

## Solution Prioritization

### Highest Impact

| Solution | Feasibility | Impact | Priority |
|----------|-------------|--------|----------|
| **Transparency requirements** | High | Medium | 1 |
| **Tax incentive conditions** | Medium | Medium | 2 |
| **Nonprofit conversion support** | Medium | High | 3 |

### Implementation Sequence

| Phase | Actions |
|-------|---------|
| **Immediate** | Disclosure rules, tax conditions |

| Level | Appropriate Actions |
|-------|---------------------|

| **State** | Transparency, tax incentives, nonprofit law |
| **Local** | Community ownership support, public notices |
