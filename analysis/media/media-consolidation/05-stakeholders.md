# Media Consolidation: Stakeholders

## Who Is Harmed

### Media Workers

| Group | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| **Journalists** | Layoffs, reduced pay, worse conditions |
| **Editors** | Eliminated or overworked |
| **Photographers** | Freelance replaces staff |
| **Production staff** | Consolidated, outsourced |
| **Sales staff** | Consolidated across properties |

**Scale of Harm**:
- 70%+ of newspaper jobs eliminated since 2006
- Wages depressed industry-wide
- Career paths destroyed
- Institutional knowledge lost

### Local Communities

| Impact | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **News coverage loss** | Local government, schools uncovered |
| **Accountability gap** | Corruption opportunities increase |
| **Civic engagement decline** | Lower voter turnout, participation |
| **Economic harm** | Higher municipal borrowing costs |
| **Community identity loss** | No shared local stories |

### Democracy

| Harm | Mechanism |
|------|-----------|
| **Reduced diversity of voices** | Fewer owners = fewer perspectives |
| **Nationalization of politics** | Local issues get less attention |
| **Manipulation vulnerability** | Fewer gatekeepers |
| **Information asymmetry** | Public knows less than elites |

### Small/Independent Media

| Impact | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Competition disadvantage** | Can't compete with chains |
| **Advertising loss** | Consolidated sales squeeze independents |
| **Distribution challenges** | Controlled by consolidated players |
| **Acquisition pressure** | Sell or die |

## Who Benefits

### Hedge Funds and Private Equity

**Alden Global Capital**

| Benefit | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Asset extraction** | Sell real estate, equipment |
| **Cash flow capture** | Profits without reinvestment |
| **Tax advantages** | Depreciation, interest deductions |
| **Low acquisition cost** | Distressed assets cheap |

**Other PE/Hedge Funds**

| Benefit | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Roll-up gains** | Buy many, consolidate, sell |
| **Financial engineering** | Debt structures |
| **Short-term profits** | Extract before collapse |

### Large Media Corporations

| Company | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Nexstar, Sinclair** | Market dominance, pricing power |
| **iHeartMedia** | National advertising deals |
| **Disney, Comcast** | Content + distribution integration |

### Shareholders

| Benefit | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Cost savings** | From consolidation |
| **Pricing power** | From market dominance |
| **Dividends/buybacks** | From extracted profits |
| **Asset appreciation** | During growth phases |

### Those Who Prefer Less Scrutiny

| Actor | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| **Corrupt officials** | Less local coverage |
| **Corporate bad actors** | Less investigative journalism |
| **National politicians** | Attention on national narratives |
