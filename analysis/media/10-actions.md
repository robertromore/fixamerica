# Media and Information: Actions

## For Individuals

### Support Local News Directly

**Subscribe and Pay**

| Action | Impact | Effort |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Subscribe to local newspaper | Direct revenue | Low |
| Pay for digital subscriptions | Sustains journalism | Low |

- Each subscription provides essential revenue
- Demonstrates market for local news
- Reduces dependence on advertising
- Creates sustainable business model

### Be an Informed Consumer

**Improve Your Information Diet**

| Practice | How To |
|----------|--------|
| Check sources before sharing | Look for original reporting |
| Use fact-checking sites | Snopes, PolitiFact, FactCheck.org |
| Seek diverse perspectives | Read across political spectrum |
| Prefer primary sources | Read documents, not summaries |
| Slow down | Don't share impulsively |

**Recognize Misinformation**

| Red Flag | What It Suggests |
|----------|------------------|
| Extreme emotional appeal | Designed for engagement, not accuracy |
| No sources cited | Unverifiable claims |
| Unknown outlet | May be unreliable |
| Too good to be true | Probably isn't true |
| Asks you to share urgently | Manipulation tactic |

**Tools to Help**

| Tool | Use |
|------|-----|
| NewsGuard | Browser extension rates source reliability |
| Ground News | Shows same story from different perspectives |
| AllSides | Identifies bias in coverage |
| Reverse image search | Verify photos aren't manipulated |

### Engage with Journalists

**Constructive Engagement**

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Comment thoughtfully on articles | Builds community |
| Suggest story ideas | Helps reporters find stories |
| Provide tips on local issues | Enables accountability |
| Correct errors respectfully | Improves accuracy |
| Defend journalists from harassment | Protects press freedom |

**What NOT to Do**

- Harass or threaten journalists
- Spread misinformation about reporters
- Dismiss all media as "fake news"
- Share articles you haven't read

## The Stakes

**What Happens If We Don't Act**

- More communities become news deserts
- Local corruption goes unchecked
- Misinformation spreads further
- Democratic accountability erodes
- Shared reality disappears

**What We Can Achieve**

- Sustainable local news in every community
- Informed citizens making better decisions
- Accountable government at all levels
- Healthier information environment
- Stronger democracy

The crisis is severe, but not inevitable. Individual actions, combined with systemic reform, can rebuild American journalism. The time to act is now.
