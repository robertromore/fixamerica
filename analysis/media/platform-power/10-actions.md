# Platform Power: Actions

## For Individuals

### Reduce Platform Dependency

| Action | Impact | How |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Diversify news sources** | Reduce algorithm control | Go directly to news sites |
| **Use multiple platforms** | Avoid single gatekeeper | Try alternatives |
| **Adjust algorithm settings** | Reduce manipulation | Use chronological feeds |
| **Limit platform time** | Reduce data collection | Screen time controls |

### Protect Your Privacy

| Action | Impact | How |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Review privacy settings** | Limit data collection | Check each platform |
| **Use ad blockers** | Reduce tracking | Browser extensions |
| **Opt out of targeted ads** | Limit surveillance | Platform settings |
| **Use privacy-focused alternatives** | Better protection | DuckDuckGo, Signal, etc. |

## Getting Started Checklist

### Week One

- [ ] Review privacy settings on all platforms
- [ ] Subscribe to at least one news outlet directly
- [ ] Follow a platform accountability organization
- [ ] Learn about pending legislation

### Month One

- [ ] Contact legislators about platform regulation
- [ ] Adjust algorithm settings to reduce manipulation
- [ ] Share information about platform power with others
- [ ] Attend an advocacy event or meeting

### Year One

- [ ] Actively support platform regulation efforts
- [ ] Reduce platform dependency significantly
- [ ] Participate in regulatory comment processes
- [ ] Build awareness in your community
