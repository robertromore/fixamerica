# Trade & Exports: Citizen Actions

## Overview

Building resilient agricultural exports requires action at multiple levels—from individual producers exploring export opportunities to communities developing trade infrastructure to advocates pushing for trade policy reform. This guide provides concrete steps for each audience, recognizing that trade success depends on both market access and domestic capacity.
