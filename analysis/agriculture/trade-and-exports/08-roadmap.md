# Trade & Exports: Implementation Roadmap

## Strategic Approach

This roadmap outlines a phased approach to diversifying export markets, modernizing compliance and certification systems, expanding access for small exporters, and investing in trade infrastructure. The strategy aligns trade diplomacy with domestic investment, balancing immediate opportunities with long-term resilience building. Implementation requires coordination across USDA, USTR, DOT, and state partners.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Year 1)

### Objectives

- Establish diversification incentives and emerging market programs
- Launch SPS modernization planning and pilot programs
- Expand export finance access for small and midsize exporters
- Build technical assistance capacity for small exporters
- Strengthen trade enforcement and dispute resolution capacity

### Key Actions

| Action | Lead Agency | Timeline | Deliverable |
|--------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| Launch Market Diversification Incentive Program | FAS | Q1-Q2 | Bonus MAP allocations for new market entry |
| Establish Emerging Market Development Fund | FAS | Q2 | $100M fund operational |
| Begin SPS digital certification platform design | APHIS/FSIS | Q1-Q4 | Platform specifications and vendor selection |
| Expand GSM-102 small exporter access | FAS | Q2-Q3 | Simplified application; lower thresholds |
| Deploy initial small exporter advisors | FAS/SBA | Q2-Q4 | 25 advisors at SBDCs and extension offices |
| Establish Agricultural Trade Enforcement Unit | USTR | Q1-Q2 | Dedicated staff; priority barrier list |
| Launch trade data enhancement project | FAS/ERS | Q3-Q4 | Data platform design completed |
| Conduct port and infrastructure needs assessment | DOT/USDA | Q2-Q4 | Priority project identification |
| Begin EU deforestation compliance support | USDA | Q1 | Technical assistance for affected exporters |

## Risk Mitigation

### Implementation Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Appropriations shortfalls** | Medium | High | Build bipartisan coalition; demonstrate ROI |
| **Agency coordination failures** | Medium | Medium | Clear lead agency; interagency agreements |
| **Technology development delays** | Medium | Medium | Phased implementation; vendor diversity |
| **Partner country resistance** | Medium | Medium | Trade agreement leverage; reciprocity |
| **Infrastructure project delays** | High | Medium | Prioritize shovel-ready; multi-year commitment |
| **Program capture by large firms** | Medium | High | Eligibility rules; set-asides; monitoring |

### External Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Trade policy volatility** | High | High | Diversification; long-term agreements |
| **Geopolitical disruptions** | High | High | Market diversification; contingency planning |
| **Climate disruptions** | Medium | Medium | Climate-resilient infrastructure; adaptation |
| **Currency volatility** | High | Medium | Risk insurance; hedging support |
| **Competitor investments** | High | Medium | Accelerate infrastructure; competitiveness focus |

### Contingency Responses

| Trigger | Response |
|---------|----------|
| Major market closure (tariffs, ban) | Accelerate diversification; emergency market development |
| Infrastructure failure (port, river) | Alternative routing; emergency capacity |
| SPS barrier escalation | Enforcement acceleration; diplomatic engagement |
| Funding shortfall | Prioritize highest-impact programs; seek alternative sources |
| Technology platform failure | Fallback to manual processes; vendor replacement |

## Monitoring and Evaluation

### Reporting Schedule

| Report | Frequency | Lead Agency | Audience |
|--------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Trade Program Performance** | Quarterly | FAS | USDA leadership; OMB |
| **SPS Modernization Progress** | Quarterly | APHIS/FSIS | Interagency group |
| **Small Exporter Metrics** | Semi-annual | FAS/SBA | Congress; stakeholders |
| **Infrastructure Progress** | Semi-annual | DOT/USDA | Congress; states |
| **Trade Resilience Report** | Annual | FAS | Public; Congress |
| **Comprehensive Evaluation** | Biennial | USDA/GAO | Congress; public |

### Evaluation Questions

| Phase | Key Evaluation Questions |
|-------|-------------------------|
| **Year 1** | Are programs launching on schedule? Are early participants engaged? |
| **Year 2** | Are diversification incentives changing behavior? Is digital certification working? |
| **Year 3** | Are small exporter metrics improving? Are infrastructure projects on track? |
| **Year 4** | Are market concentration metrics improving? Are enforcement efforts effective? |
| **Year 5** | Did we achieve diversification goals? What is infrastructure ROI? |

### Adaptive Management

| Condition | Adaptation |
|-----------|------------|
| Diversification incentives underutilized | Increase incentive levels; simplify access |
| Digital certification adoption slow | Additional technical assistance; mandate pilots |
| Small exporter programs concentrated | Strengthen outreach; adjust eligibility |
| Infrastructure projects delayed | Reprioritize; address bottlenecks |
| New trade barriers emerge | Shift enforcement resources; diplomatic escalation |
