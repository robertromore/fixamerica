# Nutrition & Food Security: Citizen Actions

## Overview

Addressing food insecurity requires action at multiple levelsâ€”from individual support and community organizing to policy advocacy and systemic change. This guide provides concrete steps for each audience, recognizing that hunger is both an immediate crisis requiring direct service and a policy failure requiring political engagement.

---

## For Individual Citizens

### Direct Action

**Personal Engagement**

| Action | Impact | How |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Donate to food banks | Immediate relief | Feeding America locator |
| Volunteer at pantries | Direct service | Local food bank |
| Support nutrition incentives | Double SNAP dollars | GusNIP programs |
| Grow and donate produce | Fresh food access | Community gardens |

**Consumer Choices**

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Shop at farmers markets accepting SNAP | Support local farms, access |
| Support businesses paying living wages | Address root causes |
| Reduce household food waste | Model, free resources |
| Choose retailers supporting programs | Incentivize participation |

### Help Others Access Benefits

**SNAP Outreach**

| Action | Method |
|--------|--------|
| Share eligibility information | Know the rules |
| Help with applications | Application assistance |
| Connect to local offices | Transportation, translation |
| Reduce stigma | Normalize benefit use |

**Information to Share**

- Eligibility extends higher than many think (130-200% poverty)
- Online applications available in most states
- Working families are eligible
- Many immigrants are eligible

### Learn and Educate

**Personal Education**

| Resource Type | Examples |
|---------------|----------|
| Books | *A Place at the Table*, *Breadlines Knee-Deep in Wheat* |
| Documentaries | A Place at the Table, Fed Up |
| Reports | Feeding America Map the Meal Gap |
| News | FRAC updates, local food reporting |

**Spread Awareness**

- Share food insecurity data for your community
- Discuss hunger with friends and family
- Write letters to local newspapers
- Organize film screenings and discussions

## For Advocacy Organizations

### Federal Policy Priorities

**Farm Bill Engagement**

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 2+ years before | Build relationships with Ag Committee staff |
| 1 year before | Submit coalition priorities |
| During markup | Targeted amendments |
| Implementation | Comment on rulemaking |

**Priority Issues**

| Issue | Ask |
|-------|-----|
| SNAP benefit adequacy | Maintain or increase benefits |
| Universal school meals | Federal funding |
| GusNIP expansion | Increased mandatory funding |
| SNAP access | Reduce administrative burdens |
| Summer EBT | Full implementation support |
