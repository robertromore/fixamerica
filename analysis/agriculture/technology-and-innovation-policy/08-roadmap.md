# Technology & Innovation Policy: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines a phased approach to transforming agricultural technology and innovation policy through coordinated investments in research, extension, data governance, technology access, and emerging technology frameworks. The strategy prioritizes equitable adoption, farmer data rights, and accountable AI while building on existing programs and partnerships. Implementation spans a 5-year initial period with ongoing system development and continuous improvement.

---

## Strategic Approach

### Guiding Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Farmer-centered** | Technology serves farmer needs; farmers control their data |
| **Equitable access** | Programs designed for small, beginning, and underserved farmers |
| **Phased deployment** | Pilots before national rollout; learn and adapt |
| **Voluntary participation** | Opt-in frameworks; no mandates on farmers |
| **Multi-agency coordination** | USDA, DOL, FCC working together |
| **Public-private partnership** | Leverage private investment alongside public programs |

### Implementation Layers

| Layer | Focus | Lead Agencies |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| **Research and extension** | Funding, staffing, technology transfer | NIFA, ARS |
| **Data governance** | Standards, privacy, interoperability | USDA OCIO, AMS |
| **Technology access** | Grants, loans, equipment sharing | USDA RD, NRCS |
| **Workforce** | Training, certification, career pathways | DOL, NIFA |
| **Connectivity** | Rural broadband deployment | USDA RD, NTIA, FCC |
| **AI governance** | Transparency, accountability, auditing | USDA OCIO, ARS |

### Building Blocks

| Component | Prerequisite | Timeline |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| **Extension expansion** | Appropriations; hiring authority | Year 1 launch |
| **Data governance framework** | Stakeholder input; legal review | Year 1 development |
| **Technology Access Grants** | Program design; funding | Year 1 launch |
| **Workforce grants** | DOL coordination; curriculum development | Year 1-2 |
| **Interoperability standards** | Industry engagement; technical development | Years 1-3 |
| **AI accountability framework** | Research; stakeholder input | Years 2-4 |

| **Industry resistance** | Medium | Medium | Transition periods; voluntary start |
