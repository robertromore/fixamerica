## For Farmworkers

### Know Your Rights

**Basic Rights (All Workers)**

| Right | Coverage | Resource |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Minimum wage | Federal + state | DOL Wage and Hour |
| Safe workplace | OSHA general duty | OSHA complaint |
| Workers' compensation | Most states | State agency |
| Freedom from discrimination | Federal law | EEOC |

**H-2A Worker Rights**

| Right | Requirement | Enforcement |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Guaranteed wage | AEWR or higher | DOL complaint |
| Free housing | Must meet standards | DOL, state |
| Transportation | To/from work | Contract |
| No recruitment fees | Cannot charge workers | DOL |
| Written contract | In worker's language | Required |

**Documentation**

| What to Keep | Why |
|--------------|-----|
| Pay stubs | Prove wage theft |
| Work schedules | Verify hours |
| Housing photos | Document conditions |
| Contract copy | Verify promises |
| Witness contacts | Corroborate claims |

### Reporting Violations

**Where to Report**

| Issue | Agency | Contact |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Wage theft | DOL Wage and Hour | 1-866-4-USA-DOL |
| Safety hazards | OSHA | 1-800-321-OSHA |
| Discrimination | EEOC | eeoc.gov |
| Human trafficking | DOJ | 1-888-373-7888 |
| H-2A violations | DOL | foreignlaborcert.doleta.gov |

**Protections Against Retaliation**

- Federal law prohibits retaliation for complaints
- Document any changes after complaint
- Contact legal aid if retaliation occurs
- Anonymous complaints possible

### Finding Help

**Legal Aid**

| Need | Resource |
|------|----------|
| Wage theft | Local legal aid, worker center |
| Immigration | CLINIC directory |
| Safety | OSHA consultation |
| Housing | Legal aid, housing authority |

**Health Services**

| Service | Provider |
|---------|----------|
| Primary care | Federally Qualified Health Centers |
| Farmworker-specific | Migrant Health Centers |
| Emergency | Hospital emergency rooms |
| Pesticide exposure | Poison Control 1-800-222-1222 |

## For Agricultural Employers

### Compliance and Beyond

**Basic Compliance**

| Area | Requirements | Resources |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| Wages | Federal/state minimum, overtime where applicable | DOL wage resources |
| Safety | OSHA standards, heat protections | OSHA guidance |
| Housing | H-2A standards or better | DOL housing guide |
| Documentation | I-9, pay records | DOL compliance |

**Beyond Compliance**

| Practice | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Above-minimum wages | Recruitment, retention |
| Year-round employment | Workforce stability |
| Career advancement | Skilled workforce |
| Health benefits | Worker health, retention |
| Training programs | Productivity |

### Ethical Recruitment

**H-2A Best Practices**

| Practice | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Direct recruitment | Avoid contractor abuses |
| No worker-paid fees | Legal requirement, ethical |
| Clear contracts | Worker understanding |
| Pre-departure orientation | Prepared workers |
| Complaint mechanisms | Early problem identification |

**Certified Programs**

| Certification | Standards | Benefit |
|---------------|-----------|---------|
| Fair Food Program | Comprehensive | Market access |
| Equitable Food Initiative | Multi-stakeholder | Premium markets |
| Fair Trade USA | Various standards | Consumer recognition |
