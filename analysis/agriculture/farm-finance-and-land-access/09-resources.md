# Farm Finance & Land Access: Resources

## Overview

This resource guide provides pathways to information on farm credit, land access, and agricultural finance for producers, advocates, researchers, and policymakers.

---

## Federal Programs and Agencies

### USDA Farm Service Agency (FSA)

**Loan Programs**

| Program | Purpose | Website |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Direct Farm Ownership Loans | Purchase land, construct buildings | fsa.usda.gov/farmloans |
| Direct Operating Loans | Annual operating expenses | fsa.usda.gov/farmloans |
| Microloans | Small-scale operations (<$50K) | fsa.usda.gov/microloans |
| Emergency Loans | Disaster recovery | fsa.usda.gov/programs |
| Beginning Farmer Loans | New producers (favorable terms) | fsa.usda.gov/farmloans |

**Key Resources**

- Farm Loan Discovery Tool: fsa.usda.gov/farmloans
- Service Center Locator: farmers.gov/service-locator
- Farm Loan Programs Overview: fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs

**Structure**

- Farm Credit Banks (4 regional)
- Farm Credit Associations (~60 local)
- CoBank (cooperative lending)

## State and Regional Programs

### Land Link Programs

**What They Do**

- Match retiring farmers with new entrants
- Facilitate farm transfers
- Provide transition planning resources

**State Programs**

| State/Region | Program | Website |
|--------------|---------|---------|
| National | Land For Good | landforgood.org |
| California | California FarmLink | californiafarmlink.org |
| Iowa | Beginning Farmer Center | extension.iastate.edu |
| Maine | Maine Farmland Trust | mainefarmlandtrust.org |
| Vermont | Vermont Land Link | uvm.edu/farmtransfer |
| Minnesota | Passing on the Farm | mda.state.mn.us |

### State Financing Programs

- State agricultural finance authorities
- Beginning farmer tax credits (varies by state)
- Linked deposit programs
- State loan guarantees
