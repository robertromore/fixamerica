# Farm Finance & Land Access: Overview

Farm finance and land access addresses the capital and real estate dynamics that determine who can enter, stay, or expand in agriculture. This subtopic traces the role of credit, debt, land prices, succession planning, and speculative pressures that squeeze beginning, BIPOC, and leverage-light producers.
