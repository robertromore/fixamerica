## Legal Resources

### Federal Statutes

| Law | Citation | Relevance |
|-----|----------|-----------|
| Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) | 15 U.S.C. 6501-6506 | Primary federal children's privacy law |
| COPPA Rule | 16 C.F.R. Part 312 | FTC implementing regulations |
| Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) | 20 U.S.C. 1232g | Student educational records |
| Children's Internet Protection Act (CIPA) | 47 U.S.C. 254(h)(5) | Internet filtering in schools/libraries |
| FTC Act, Section 5 | 15 U.S.C. 45 | Unfair and deceptive practices authority |

### Key Court Cases

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|--------------|
| *Brown v. Entertainment Merchants Ass'n* | 564 U.S. 786 (2011) | First Amendment limits on restricting minors' access to content |
| *NetChoice v. Bonta* | No. 22-cv-08861 (N.D. Cal. 2023) | Preliminary injunction against California Age-Appropriate Design Code |
| *NetChoice v. Paxton* | No. 21-51178 (5th Cir. 2024) | Texas social media age restriction challenge |
| *Reno v. ACLU* | 521 U.S. 844 (1997) | First Amendment limits on internet content regulation |
| *Ashcroft v. ACLU* | 542 U.S. 656 (2004) | Content filtering vs. less restrictive alternatives |
