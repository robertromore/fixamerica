# International Privacy: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding international privacy law, cross-border data flows, and the US position in the global privacy landscape. Resources are organized by type and relevance.

---

## Primary Legal Sources

### International Instruments

- **Universal Declaration of Human Rights, Article 12** (1948) -- Privacy as a universal human right
- **European Convention on Human Rights, Article 8** (1950) -- Right to respect for private life
- **OECD Guidelines on the Protection of Privacy and Transborder Flows of Personal Data** (1980, updated 2013) -- Foundational international privacy principles
- **Council of Europe Convention 108** (1981, modernized as Convention 108+ in 2018) -- First binding international data protection treaty
- **EU Charter of Fundamental Rights, Articles 7 and 8** (2000) -- EU fundamental right to data protection

### European Union

- **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, Regulation (EU) 2016/679 -- Full text and recitals
- **European Commission adequacy decisions** -- List of countries recognized as providing adequate protection
- **EU-US Data Privacy Framework adequacy decision** (July 10, 2023)
- **European Data Protection Board (EDPB) Recommendations 01/2020** -- Supplementary measures for international transfers

### Key Court Decisions

- ***Schrems v. Data Protection Commissioner*** (CJEU, C-362/14, 2015) -- Invalidated Safe Harbor
- ***Data Protection Commissioner v. Facebook Ireland and Maximillian Schrems*** (CJEU, C-311/18, 2020) -- Invalidated Privacy Shield
- ***Carpenter v. United States*** (US Supreme Court, 585 U.S. ___, 2018) -- Warrant required for cell-site location data
- ***Sorrell v. IMS Health*** (US Supreme Court, 564 U.S. 552, 2011) -- Data restrictions and First Amendment

### US Statutes and Executive Orders

- **Executive Order 14086** (October 7, 2022) -- Enhancing Safeguards for US Signals Intelligence Activities
- **FISA Amendments Act of 2008, Section 702** (50 U.S.C. 1881a) -- Foreign intelligence surveillance
- **Executive Order 12333** (1981, as amended) -- United States Intelligence Activities
- **Fair Credit Reporting Act** (15 U.S.C. 1681) -- Consumer credit data regulation
- **California Consumer Privacy Act / California Privacy Rights Act** (Cal. Civ. Code 1798.100 et seq.)
