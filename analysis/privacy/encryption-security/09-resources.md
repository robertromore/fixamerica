# Encryption and Security: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles research, organizations, tools, and data sources relevant to understanding and advancing strong encryption policy in the United States.

## Key Research and Reports

### Government Reports

| Report | Author | Year | Focus |
|--------|--------|------|-------|

| "Report on Cybersecurity" | Commission on Enhancing National Cybersecurity | 2016 | Bipartisan commission recommending against encryption backdoors |

| Paper/Book | Author(s) | Year | Key Finding |

## Legal Resources

### Key Cases

| Case | Citation | Relevance |
|------|----------|-----------|
| *Bernstein v. United States* | 176 F.3d 1132 (9th Cir. 1999) | Encryption source code is protected speech under the First Amendment |
| *Junger v. Daley* | 209 F.3d 481 (6th Cir. 2000) | Affirmed First Amendment protection for encryption code |
| *Apple Inc. v. United States* (In re Search of an Apple iPhone) | No. ED 15-0451M (C.D. Cal. 2016) | FBI sought court order to compel Apple to bypass iPhone encryption; case dropped when FBI found alternative |
| *United States v. Apple MacPro Computer* | 851 F.3d 238 (3d Cir. 2017) | Addressed compelled decryption and Fifth Amendment privilege |
| *Carpenter v. United States* | 585 U.S. 296 (2018) | Digital privacy; Fourth Amendment applies to cell-site location data |
| *Riley v. California* | 573 U.S. 373 (2014) | Warrant required to search cell phone; recognized privacy interest in encrypted devices |

### Key Statutes and Regulations

| Statute | Citation | Relevance |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Communications Assistance for Law Enforcement Act (CALEA) | 47 U.S.C. ss 1001-1010 | Requires telecom wiretap capability; explicitly exempts information services (internet) from backdoor mandates |
| All Writs Act | 28 U.S.C. ss 1651 | Legal basis for FBI's iPhone access order in San Bernardino case |
| Electronic Communications Privacy Act (ECPA) | 18 U.S.C. ss 2510-2522 | Framework for government access to electronic communications |
| Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act (FISA) | 50 U.S.C. ss 1801 et seq. | Intelligence surveillance authority; Section 702 collection |
| Export Administration Regulations (EAR) | 15 C.F.R. Parts 730-774 | Current encryption export controls (successor to ITAR classification) |
| Australia Telecommunications and Other Legislation Amendment (TOLA) Act | 2018 | International precedent for encryption backdoor mandates |
| UK Online Safety Act | 2023 | Ofcom authority to require scanning of E2EE messages |
