### Key Court Cases

| Case | Citation | Significance |
|------|----------|--------------|
| *Carpenter v. United States* | 585 U.S. 296 (2018) | Fourth Amendment requires warrant for cell site location information; implications for commercial data |
| *TransUnion v. Ramirez* | 594 U.S. 413 (2021) | Standing requirements for privacy class actions |
| *Van Buren v. United States* | 593 U.S. 374 (2021) | Computer Fraud and Abuse Act scope narrowed |
| *Patel v. Facebook* | 932 F.3d 1264 (9th Cir. 2019) | BIPA claims do not require showing of actual harm |
| *Rosenbach v. Six Flags* | 2019 IL 123186 | BIPA statutory damages available without actual injury |
| *In re Facebook (FTC)* | 2019 consent order | $5 billion FTC fine; enhanced privacy obligations |
