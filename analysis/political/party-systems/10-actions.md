### Stay Informed

**Learn About Reform**

| Resource | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| FairVote | RCV education |

| Unite America | Electoral reform |

**Understand Your State**

- What primary system does your state have?
- What ballot access rules exist?
- Are reform initiatives underway?
- What are your representatives' positions?

## Action Planning

### Immediate (This Week)

- [ ] Learn about your state's primary and ballot access rules
- [ ] Contact your representative about electoral reform
- [ ] Follow one electoral reform organization
- [ ] Share reform information with one person

### Short-Term (This Month)

- [ ] Attend a reform organization meeting
- [ ] Write a letter to the editor about electoral reform
- [ ] Donate to a reform organization
- [ ] Research third party and independent candidates

### Medium-Term (This Year)

- [ ] Volunteer for a reform campaign
- [ ] Participate in signature gathering (if applicable)
- [ ] Organize a community education event
- [ ] Support reform candidates in elections

### Long-Term (Ongoing)

- [ ] Build and maintain coalition relationships
- [ ] Develop expertise in electoral systems
- [ ] Run for office or recruit candidates
- [ ] Sustain engagement through wins and losses
