# War Powers Reform: Actions

## For Individual Citizens

### Immediate Actions

**Contact Your Representatives**

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| Call congressional offices | Capitol switchboard: (202) 224-3121 |
| Write letters | Personalized letters more effective than form emails |
| Request meetings | Ask for appointment with staff |
| Attend town halls | Ask questions publicly |

**Key Messages**

| Issue | Talking Point |
|-------|---------------|
| Constitutional authority | "Congress should vote before we go to war" |
| Accountability | "Representatives should be on record about war" |
| Endless wars | "The 2001 AUMF should be replaced with limited authorization" |
| Costs | "We spend trillions on wars without congressional debate" |

**When to Contact**

| Trigger | Message |
|---------|---------|
| Military action reported | "Did you vote on this?" |
| AUMF debate | "Support replacing outdated authorizations" |
| Defense budget | "Include war powers reforms" |
| Election season | "What's your position on war powers?" |
