## Research and Reports

### Essential Academic Research

**On Duverger's Law**

- Duverger, Maurice. "Political Parties" (1954) - Original formulation
- Riker, William. "The Two-Party System and Duverger's Law" (1982)
- Cox, Gary. "Making Votes Count" (1997) - Strategic voting analysis

**On Ranked-Choice Voting**

- FairVote. "Data on Ranked Choice Voting" - Ongoing research
- Santucci, Jack. "More Parties or No Parties" (2022)
- Donovan, Todd, et al. "Campaign Civility Under Preferential and Plurality Voting" (2016)

**On Proportional Representation**

- Lijphart, Arend. "Patterns of Democracy" (2012)
- Amy, Douglas. "Behind the Ballot Box" (2000)
- Carey, John & Hix, Simon. "The Electoral Sweet Spot" (2011)
