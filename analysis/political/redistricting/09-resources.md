## Legal Resources

### Key Court Cases

**Federal Cases**

| Case | Year | Holding |
|------|------|---------|
| *Baker v. Carr* | 1962 | Courts can hear redistricting cases |
| *Reynolds v. Sims* | 1964 | One person, one vote |
| *Davis v. Bandemer* | 1986 | Partisan gerrymandering justiciable |
| *Vieth v. Jubelirer* | 2004 | No manageable standard (plurality) |
| *Rucho v. Common Cause* | 2019 | Partisan claims nonjusticiable |

**State Cases**

| Case | State | Outcome |
|------|-------|---------|
| League of Women Voters v. Commonwealth | PA | Maps thrown out |
| Harper v. Hall | NC | Maps thrown out (then reversed) |
| League of Women Voters v. Ohio | OH | Multiple map rejections |
| Clarke v. Wisconsin Elections Comm'n | WI | Ongoing challenges |
