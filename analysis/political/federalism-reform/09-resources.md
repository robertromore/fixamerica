**Contemporary Analysis**:

## Legal Resources

### Key Supreme Court Cases

**Commerce Clause**:

- *McCulloch v. Maryland*, 17 U.S. 316 (1819): Federal supremacy, implied powers
- *Gibbons v. Ogden*, 22 U.S. 1 (1824): Federal commerce power
- *United States v. Lopez*, 514 U.S. 549 (1995): Commerce Clause limits
- *United States v. Morrison*, 529 U.S. 598 (2000): VAWA provision struck down
- *Gonzales v. Raich*, 545 U.S. 1 (2005): Marijuana regulation upheld

**Anti-Commandeering**:

- *New York v. United States*, 505 U.S. 144 (1992): Cannot commandeer legislatures
- *Printz v. United States*, 521 U.S. 898 (1997): Cannot commandeer executives
- *Murphy v. NCAA*, 584 U.S. ___ (2018): Extended anti-commandeering

**Spending Power**:

- *South Dakota v. Dole*, 483 U.S. 203 (1987): Conditional spending upheld
- *NFIB v. Sebelius*, 567 U.S. 519 (2012): Coercion limit on spending power

**Preemption**:

- *Wyeth v. Levine*, 555 U.S. 555 (2009): Presumption against preemption
- *Arizona v. United States*, 567 U.S. 387 (2012): Immigration preemption

**State Sovereign Immunity**:

- *Seminole Tribe v. Florida*, 517 U.S. 44 (1996): State immunity
- *Alden v. Maine*, 527 U.S. 706 (1999): Immunity in state courts

### Federal Statutes

- **Unfunded Mandates Reform Act** (2 U.S.C. §§ 1501-1571)
