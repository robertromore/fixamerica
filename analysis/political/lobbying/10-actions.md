# Lobbying Reform: Actions

## For Individuals

### Stay Informed

**Track Lobbying**

| Resource | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| OpenSecrets | Follow lobbying spending |
| Senate LDA Database | Official filings |
| FARA Database | Foreign lobbying |
| News coverage | Scandals, developments |

**Understand the System**

- Learn how lobbying works
- Follow specific industries/issues
- Track your representatives' connections
- Understand disclosure requirements

### Contact Your Representatives

**Effective Communication**

| Method | Tips |
|--------|------|
| Phone calls | Most impactful, be specific |
| Written letters | Personal stories effective |
| Town halls | Public questioning |
| Social media | Public pressure |

**Key Messages**

| Issue | Message |
|-------|---------|
| Disclosure | "Close shadow lobbying loopholes" |
| Revolving door | "Extend cooling-off periods to 5 years" |
| Enforcement | "Support independent ethics oversight" |
| Capacity | "Fund congressional staff and expertise" |
