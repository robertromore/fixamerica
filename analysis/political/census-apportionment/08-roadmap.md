# Census and Apportionment: Roadmap

## Strategic Overview

Census and apportionment reform must align with the decennial census cycle. The 2030 census will be the next major opportunity to implement changes, but preparation must begin years in advance. This roadmap outlines a phased approach to reform.

## Timeline Constraints

### Census Cycle Milestones

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| April 1, 2030 | Census Day |
| December 31, 2030 | Apportionment counts due to President |
| March 31, 2031 | Redistricting data due to states |
| 2032 | First elections under new apportionment |

### Key Decision Points

| Decision | Deadline | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Bureau independence | 2025-2026 | Before 2030 planning finalized |
| Funding commitment | 2026 | Lifecycle budget starts |
| House expansion | 2028 | Before 2030 count |
| Prison counting | 2029 | Before enumeration |
| Methodology changes | 2028 | Testing required |

## Phase 3: 2030 Census Execution (2028-2031)

### Objectives

- Execute accurate 2030 census
- Protect enumeration from interference
- Deliver timely, quality data
- Monitor for problems

### Operational Timeline

| Period | Activity |
|--------|----------|
| 2028 | Final systems testing, dress rehearsal |
| 2029 | Address system development, hiring begins |
| Q1 2030 | Self-response period opens (March) |
| Q2 2030 | Census Day (April 1), field operations |
| Q3 2030 | Non-response follow-up |
| Q4 2030 | Data processing, apportionment delivery |
| Q1 2031 | Redistricting data delivery |

### Monitoring and Response

| Risk | Monitoring | Response |
|------|------------|----------|
| Political interference | Watchdog oversight | Public exposure, litigation |
| Funding shortfalls | Appropriations tracking | Supplemental requests |
| Technology failures | Testing, redundancy | Manual backup procedures |
| Pandemic/emergency | Contingency planning | Operational flexibility |

### Milestones

| Milestone | Target Date | Indicator |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Self-response rate | April-July 2030 | >70% |
| Field completion | October 2030 | >99.9% |
| Apportionment delivery | December 31, 2030 | On time |
| Quality metrics | Q1 2031 | Reduced undercount differential |

## Scenario Planning

### Optimistic Scenario

| Development | Timeline | Outcome |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| Bureau independence passed | 2025 | Protected 2030 census |
| Adequate funding secured | 2026 | Full operational capability |
| House expansion passed | 2027 | Implemented 2032 |
| 2030 census successful | 2030-2031 | Reduced undercount |

### Realistic Scenario

| Development | Timeline | Outcome |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| Partial independence protections | 2026 | Some improvement |
| Funding adequate but not ideal | 2027-2030 | Operational constraints |
| House expansion stalled | Post-2030 | Advocacy continues |
| 2030 census improved | 2030-2031 | Modest gains |

### Pessimistic Scenario

| Development | Timeline | Outcome |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| No independence legislation | - | Continued vulnerability |
| Funding cuts | 2028-2030 | Operational problems |
| Political interference | 2030 | Accuracy compromised |
| House unchanged | - | Representation disparities continue |

### Contingency Responses

| Scenario | Contingency |
|----------|-------------|
| Legislative failure | Executive actions, state-level reform |
| Funding cuts | Emergency supplemental, private foundation support |
| Political interference | Litigation, whistleblower protection, oversight |
| Technology problems | Manual backup, timeline flexibility |

## Critical Path

### Must-Have by Timeline

| Deadline | Requirement | Consequence if Missed |
|----------|-------------|-----------------------|
| 2026 | Adequate FY 2027 funding | Delayed preparation |
| 2027 | Technology ready | Operational risk |
| 2028 | Dress rehearsal complete | Undetected problems |
| 2029 | Hiring complete | Inadequate field staff |
| April 2030 | Systems operational | Census failure |

### Dependencies

```text
Bureau independence
         ↓
Protected 2030 planning → Adequate funding
         ↓                      ↓
Technology development → Successful dress rehearsal
         ↓                      ↓
Accurate 2030 census ← Adequate field operations
         ↓
Quality redistricting data
         ↓
Fair apportionment (with or without House expansion)
```
