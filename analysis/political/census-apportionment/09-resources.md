### Key Academic Works

**On Census Accuracy**

- Citro, Constance F., and Michael L. Cohen, eds. *The 2000 Census: Counting Under Adversity*. National Academies Press, 2004.
- National Academies. *Modernizing the U.S. Census*. National Academies Press, 2010.
- West, Kirsten K. "The Census Undercount." Demography (various years).

**On Apportionment**

- Balinski, Michel L., and H. Peyton Young. *Fair Representation: Meeting the Ideal of One Man, One Vote*. Yale University Press, 1982.
- Eagles, Charles W. *Democracy Delayed: Congressional Reapportionment and Urban-Rural Conflict in the 1920s*. University of Georgia Press, 1990.

**On House Size**

- Frederick, Brian. "The People's House: Increasing the Size of the U.S. House of Representatives." PS: Political Science and Politics, 2021.
- Ladewig, Jeffrey W., and Mathew P. Jasinski. "On the Causes and Consequences of and Remedies for Interstate Malapportionment of the U.S. House of Representatives." Perspectives on Politics, 2008.

## Legal Resources

### Key Supreme Court Cases

| Case | Year | Issue | Holding |
|------|------|-------|---------|
| *Wesberry v. Sanders* | 1964 | Congressional district equality | One person, one vote |
| *Department of Commerce v. Montana* | 1992 | Apportionment method | Huntington-Hill upheld |
| *Utah v. Evans* | 2002 | Imputation for overseas military | Allowed |
| *Department of Commerce v. New York* | 2019 | Citizenship question | Blocked (pretextual) |
| *Trump v. New York* | 2020 | Undocumented exclusion | Dismissed as not ripe |
