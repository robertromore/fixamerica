## Legal Resources

### Key Constitutional Provisions

- **Article I, Section 8, Clause 17**: Federal district clause
- **Article IV, Section 3**: Territories and new states
- **Article V**: Amendment process (Senate protection)
- **23rd Amendment**: DC electoral votes

### Key Court Cases

**Territorial Status**:

- *Downes v. Bidwell*, 182 U.S. 244 (1901) (Insular Cases)
- *Balzac v. Porto Rico*, 258 U.S. 298 (1922)
- *Boumediene v. Bush*, 553 U.S. 723 (2008) (territories and Constitution)

**DC Representation**:

- *Adams v. Clinton*, 90 F. Supp. 2d 35 (D.D.C. 2000) (DC lacks constitutional right to representation)
- *Banner v. United States*, 428 F.3d 303 (D.C. Cir. 2005)

**Apportionment**:

- *U.S. Department of Commerce v. Montana*, 503 U.S. 442 (1992)
- *Evenwel v. Abbott*, 578 U.S. 54 (2016)
