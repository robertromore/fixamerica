# Statehood and Representation: Actions

## For All Citizens

### Get Educated

**Understand the Issues**:

- How many Americans lack voting representation?
- What is DC's current status and what would statehood change?
- What are Puerto Rico's status options?
- Why is the House frozen at 435 members?

**Key Resources**:

| Topic | Resource |
|-------|----------|
| DC Statehood | dcvote.org, statehood.dc.gov |
| Puerto Rico | centropr.hunter.cuny.edu |
| House Size | fairvote.org |
| General | CRS reports (congress.gov) |

### Contact Congress

**For DC Statehood**:

- Call your senators: Support DC statehood
- Call your representative: Thank if supported, urge if not
- Ask about filibuster reform to enable vote

**For Puerto Rico**:

- Urge binding status resolution process
- Support congressional commitment to act on referendum
- Ask for clear options and timelines

**For House Expansion**:

- Ask representatives about Wyoming Rule or cube root expansion
- Frame as fairness and representation issue
- Less partisan than statehood issues

**Key Messages**:

- "700,000 DC residents pay taxes but have no voting representation"
- "3.2 million Puerto Ricans are citizens without presidential vote"
- "Representation is a right, not a partisan favor"

### Amplify the Issue

**Social Media**:

- Share information about representation gaps
- Amplify DC and Puerto Rico voices
- Challenge opposition arguments

**Conversations**:

- Talk to friends and family about these issues
- Counter misconceptions
- Connect to democratic values

### Vote and Organize

**Elections**:

- Support candidates who back statehood
- Make this a voting issue
- Primary challenges for opponents

**Organizations**:

- Join or donate to advocacy groups
- Participate in campaigns
- Build coalitions

## Key Questions for Candidates

### For Congress

1. "Do you support DC statehood? Will you vote for it?"
2. "What is your position on Puerto Rico's political status?"
3. "Would you support expanding the House of Representatives?"
4. "Will you support filibuster reform to enable statehood votes?"
5. "How do you justify denying representation to American citizens?"

### For President

1. "Will you sign DC statehood legislation?"
2. "What is your plan for Puerto Rico status resolution?"
3. "Will you advocate for representation reforms?"
