# Budget Process Reform: Resources

## Primary Sources

### Federal Law and Regulations

**Congressional Budget Act of 1974**

- Pub. L. 93-344

- 2 U.S.C. §§ 601-688

- Established modern budget process

- [GovInfo](https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/STATUTE-88/pdf/STATUTE-88-Pg297.pdf)

**Impoundment Control Act of 1974**

- Title X of Congressional Budget Act

- 2 U.S.C. §§ 681-688

- Restricts executive impoundment

**Budget Enforcement Act of 1990**

- Pub. L. 101-508

- Created PAYGO and discretionary caps

- Expired 2002

**Budget Control Act of 2011**

- Pub. L. 112-25

- Responded to debt ceiling crisis

- Created sequestration

**Fiscal Responsibility Act of 2023**

- Pub. L. 118-5

- Suspended debt ceiling to January 2025

- Imposed discretionary caps

- Process analysis

Rubin, Irene S. *The Politics of Public Budgeting: Getting and Spending, Borrowing and Balancing*. 9th ed. CQ Press, 2019.

- Political dynamics

- Comparative analysis

- Theoretical frameworks

Wildavsky, Aaron, and Naomi Caiden. *The New Politics of the Budgetary Process*. 5th ed. Longman, 2004.

- Incrementalism theory

- Historical perspective

- Policy options
