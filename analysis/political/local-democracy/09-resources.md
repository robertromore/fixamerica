## Essential Reading

### Books

**Local Democracy and Governance**:

- Frug, Gerald and David Barron. *City Bound: How States Stifle Urban Innovation*. Cornell University Press, 2008.
- Schragger, Richard. *City Power: Urban Governance in a Global Age*. Oxford University Press, 2016.
- Berry, Jeffrey et al. *The Rebirth of Urban Democracy*. Brookings, 1993.
- Fung, Archon. *Empowered Participation: Reinventing Urban Democracy*. Princeton University Press, 2004.

**Participation and Engagement**:

- Nabatchi, Tina and Matt Leighninger. *Public Participation for 21st Century Democracy*. Jossey-Bass, 2015.
- Gastil, John and Erik Olin Wright, eds. *Legislature by Lot*. Verso, 2019.
- Wampler, Brian. *Participatory Budgeting in Brazil*. Penn State University Press, 2007.

**Electoral Reform**:

- Richie, Rob et al. *Whose Votes Count*. Beacon Press (various)
- Engstrom, Richard. *The Future of the Voting Rights Act*. Russell Sage, 2006.
