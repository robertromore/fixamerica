# Local Democracy: Actions

## For All Citizens

### Get Informed About Local Government

**Learn the Basics**:

- Who are your city council members?
- What form of government does your city have?
- When are local elections held?
- What issues is local government currently addressing?

**Sources**:

| Source | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| City website | Officials, meetings, agendas |
| Local news | Current issues, decisions |
| Neighbors | Community perspective |
| Civic organizations | Analysis, advocacy |

### Participate in Local Processes

**Attend Meetings**:

- City/town council meetings
- Planning/zoning hearings
- School board meetings
- Budget hearings

**Engage Between Elections**:

- Submit public comments
- Contact council members
- Attend community meetings
- Participate in surveys

### Vote in Local Elections

**Every Election**:

- Register and stay registered
- Research local candidates
- Vote in all elections (including primaries, off-year)
- Encourage others to vote

**Understand What's on the Ballot**:

- Research candidates before election day
- Understand ballot measures
- Check endorsements from trusted groups
- Use voter guides

### Advocate for Reform

**Key Messages**:

- "Move local elections to November even years"
- "Implement ranked choice voting"
- "Increase participation opportunities"
- "Protect local authority from state preemption"

**Contact**:

| Level | Who | On What |
|-------|-----|---------|
| City | Council, mayor | Local reforms |
| State | Legislators | Home rule, enabling laws |
| Media | Letters to editor | Public awareness |

## For Local Officials

### Elected Officials

**Improve Accessibility**:

- Hold meetings at varied times
- Offer virtual participation
- Provide language access
- Go to community, not just city hall

**Enhance Engagement**:

- Community office hours
- Regular communication
- Social media presence
- Responsive constituent service

**Support Reform**:

- Champion electoral reforms
- Implement participatory budgeting
- Increase transparency
- Advocate for local authority

### City Administrators

**Administrative Improvements**:

- Modernize public engagement
- Improve website and communications
- Implement open data
- Streamline public comment

**Professional Development**:

- Attend ICMA training
- Learn best practices
- Connect with peer cities
- Evaluate programs

## For Businesses

### Local Business Support

**Constructive Engagement**:

- Participate in planning processes
- Support community benefits
- Engage on local issues
- Model good corporate citizenship

**Avoid**:

- Dominating processes
- Excessive campaign spending
- Undermining local democracy
- State preemption lobbying

## For Media

### Local Journalism

**Coverage**:

- Regular local government reporting
- Investigative journalism
- Election coverage
- Civic information

**Support for Participation**:

- Publish meeting schedules
- Explain how to participate
- Cover participation opportunities
- Highlight citizen engagement

## Key Questions for Local Candidates

1. "How will you make local government more accessible to working people?"
2. "Do you support moving elections to November of even years?"
3. "What's your position on ranked choice voting?"
4. "How will you increase participation between elections?"
5. "Do you support participatory budgeting?"
6. "How will you protect local authority from state preemption?"
7. "What will you do to improve transparency?"
8. "How will you ensure diverse voices are heard?"

## Building Long-Term Engagement

### Personal Commitment

- Attend meetings regularly
- Vote in every election
- Stay informed
- Build relationships with officials

### Community Organizing

- Form or join local civic group
- Build neighborhood networks
- Train new participants
- Sustain engagement over time

### Coalition Building

- Connect across issues
- Build diverse coalitions
- Engage business and labor
- Include underrepresented groups

## Issue-Specific Actions

### Electoral Reform

| Reform | Action |
|--------|--------|
| On-cycle elections | Support ballot initiative or council action |
| Ranked choice voting | Educate, advocate, vote yes |
| District elections | Research, propose, campaign |
| Campaign finance | Support local limits/disclosure |

### Participation Improvement

| Reform | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Participatory budgeting | Advocate for pilot, participate |
| Accessible meetings | Request accommodations, advocate |
| Citizen assemblies | Propose for specific issues |
| Online engagement | Use and advocate improvement |

### Local Authority Protection

| Issue | Action |
|-------|--------|
| State preemption | Contact state legislators |
| Home rule | Support constitutional protection |
| Fiscal authority | Advocate for local revenue options |
| Legal defense | Support litigation where appropriate |
