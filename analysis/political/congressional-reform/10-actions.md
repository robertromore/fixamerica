# Congressional Reform: Actions

## For Individuals

### Stay Informed

**Track Congress**

| Resource | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Congress.gov | Official legislation |
| GovTrack | Bill tracking with analysis |
| C-SPAN | Floor proceedings |
| Countable | Simplified bill summaries |

**Follow Reform Progress**

- Reform organizations' newsletters
- Political news on Congress
- Academic analysis
- Member positions on reform

### Contact Your Representatives

**Effective Communication**

| Method | Tips |
|--------|------|
| Phone calls | Most impactful for constituents |
| Letters | Personal stories effective |
| Town halls | Direct engagement |
| Social media | Public pressure |

**Key Messages**

| Issue | Message |
|-------|---------|
| Filibuster reform | "Majority should be able to govern" |
| Ethics reform | "Stock trading ban needed" |
| Transparency | "We deserve to know how you vote" |
| Capacity | "Give Congress resources to function" |

### Vote Strategically

**Primary Elections**

| Strategy | Purpose |

**General Elections**

| Consideration | Weight |
|---------------|--------|
| Reform positions | Primary focus |
| Governance record | Track record |
| Bipartisan work | Willingness to cooperate |
