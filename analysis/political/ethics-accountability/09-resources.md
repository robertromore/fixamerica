## Essential Reading

### Books

**Government Ethics**:

- Anechiarico, Frank and James Jacobs. *The Pursuit of Absolute Integrity: How Corruption Control Makes Government Ineffective*. University of Chicago Press, 1996.
- Maskell, Jack. *Conflicts of Interest and Ethics*. Congressional Research Service (various editions).
- Johnson, Roberta Ann. *Whistleblowing: When It Works—And Why*. Lynne Rienner, 2002.

**Corruption and Reform**:

- Thompson, Dennis. *Ethics in Congress: From Individual to Institutional Corruption*. Brookings, 1995.
- Teachout, Zephyr. *Corruption in America*. Harvard University Press, 2014.
- Lessig, Lawrence. *Republic, Lost*. Twelve, 2011.

## Legal Resources

### Primary Authorities

**Statutes**:

- Ethics in Government Act (5 U.S.C. App.)
- Criminal Conflicts of Interest (18 U.S.C. 201-209)
- Inspector General Act (5 U.S.C. App.)
- Whistleblower Protection Act (5 U.S.C. 2302)
- False Claims Act (31 U.S.C. 3729-3733)

**Regulations**:

- Standards of Ethical Conduct (5 C.F.R. Part 2635)
- Financial Disclosure (5 C.F.R. Part 2634)
- Post-Employment Restrictions (5 C.F.R. Part 2641)

**Constitutional**:

- Foreign Emoluments Clause (Art. I, § 9, cl. 8)
- Domestic Emoluments Clause (Art. II, § 1, cl. 7)

### Key Court Cases

**Conflicts of Interest**:

- *United States v. Mississippi Valley Generating Co.*, 364 U.S. 520 (1961)

**Official Acts**:

- *McDonnell v. United States*, 579 U.S. 550 (2016)

**Whistleblowers**:

- *Garcetti v. Ceballos*, 547 U.S. 410 (2006)
- *Lane v. Franks*, 573 U.S. 228 (2014)

**Emoluments**:

- *CREW v. Trump* (various)
- *Blumenthal v. Trump* (various)
