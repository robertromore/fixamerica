# Ethics and Accountability: Actions

## For All Citizens

### Stay Informed

**Monitor Ethics Issues**:

- Follow ethics watchdog organizations
- Read financial disclosures of public officials
- Track ethics legislation
- Understand ethics rules

**Key Resources**:

| Source | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| CREW | Ethics violations, litigation |
| POGO | IG issues, whistleblowers |
| OGE | Official guidance, disclosures |
| News media | Current ethics stories |

### Contact Officials

**Congress**:

- Support ethics reform legislation
- Demand IG independence
- Advocate for whistleblower protection
- Request ethics oversight

**Key Messages**:

- "Protect inspectors general from political retaliation"
- "Strengthen whistleblower protections"
- "Require presidential tax disclosure"
- "Create independent ethics enforcement"

### Report Violations

**Channels**:

| Concern | Where to Report |
|---------|-----------------|
| Federal employee misconduct | Agency IG |
| Contractor fraud | Agency IG, DOJ |
| Ethics violations | OGE, agency ethics |
| Congressional misconduct | House/Senate Ethics |
| Financial disclosure issues | OGE |

### Vote and Advocate

**Elections**:

- Consider candidates' ethics records
- Ask about ethics commitments
- Support ethics reform champions

**Advocacy**:

- Join good government organizations
- Participate in campaigns for reform
- Amplify ethics issues

## For Federal Employees

### Know Your Rights and Responsibilities

**Ethics Training**:

- Complete required training
- Seek guidance when uncertain
- Document compliance

**Whistleblower Rights**:

- Know protected disclosures
- Understand reporting channels
- Document concerns properly

### Report Wrongdoing

**Protected Channels**:

| Channel | Use For |
|---------|---------|
| Agency IG | Waste, fraud, abuse |
| OGE | Ethics rule violations |
| OSC | Prohibited personnel practices |
| Congress | Policy matters, IG retaliation |

**Self-Protection**:

- Keep records of disclosures
- Use official channels first
- Consider legal advice
- Document retaliation immediately

### Model Ethical Behavior

- Follow ethics rules carefully
- Avoid appearances of conflicts
- Report violations you observe
- Support ethical culture

## For Whistleblowers

### Before Disclosing

**Preparation**:

- Document the violation thoroughly
- Understand what's protected
- Know available channels
- Consider legal consultation

**Resources**:

| Organization | Support Offered |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Government Accountability Project | Guidance, legal referrals |
| National Whistleblower Center | Legal resources |
| OSC | Federal employee rights |
| Agency IG | Investigation |

### During the Process

**Best Practices**:

- Use official channels when possible
- Keep contemporaneous records
- Maintain professionalism
- Build support network

**If Retaliation Occurs**:

- Document immediately
- Report to appropriate authority
- Seek legal counsel
- Contact advocacy organizations

### Protecting Yourself

- Understand timeline requirements
- Know burden of proof
- Preserve evidence
- Seek support

## For Inspectors General

### Maintain Independence

**Priorities**:

- Resist pressure to soften findings
- Report interference
- Maintain professional standards
- Coordinate through CIGIE

**Advocacy**:

- Support IG independence legislation
- Document obstruction
- Communicate challenges to Congress

### Strengthen Community

- Participate in CIGIE activities
- Share best practices
- Develop peer support
- Train future IGs

## Key Questions for Officials and Candidates

### For Executive Branch

1. "Will you release your tax returns?"
2. "What conflicts of interest do you have and how will you address them?"
3. "Will you protect inspector general independence?"
4. "Do you support strengthening whistleblower protections?"
5. "Will you require divestiture of conflicting financial interests?"

### For Congress

1. "Do you support IG Independence Act?"
2. "Will you vote for ethics enforcement reform?"
3. "Do you support enhanced financial disclosure?"
4. "Will you require presidential divestiture and tax disclosure?"
5. "How will you strengthen congressional ethics enforcement?"

## Building Long-Term Change

### Citizen Engagement

- Sustained attention beyond scandals
- Build ethics into voting decisions
- Support reform organizations
- Demand accountability

### Institutional Reform

- Career ethics official development
- Professional standards
- Independent enforcement
- Resource adequacy

### Cultural Change

- Ethics as priority, not obstacle
- Leadership modeling
- Zero tolerance messaging
- Public service values
