## Who Is Harmed

### The General Public

**Information Harm**

| Impact | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Misinformed | Make poor decisions |
| Confused | Can't distinguish truth |
| Manipulated | Beliefs engineered |
| Cynical | Trust nothing |

**Specific Groups**

| Group | Harm |
|-------|------|
| News desert residents | No local information |
| Marginalized communities | Coverage gaps |
| Rural Americans | Local news loss |
| Elderly | Misinformation vulnerability |

### Local Communities

**Community Harm**

| Impact | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| No watchdog | Corruption flourishes |
| Less civic info | Lower participation |
| Community disconnection | Social fabric frays |
| Democracy weakens | Less accountability |

### Journalists

**Professional Harm**

| Impact | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Job losses | 50%+ reduction |
| Precarity | Freelance, no benefits |
| Harassment | Threats, attacks |
| Burnout | Unsustainable workload |

### Democracy

**Systemic Harm**

| Impact | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Informed citizenry erodes | Self-governance fails |
| Accountability declines | Corruption increases |
| Shared reality fragments | Can't deliberate |
| Manipulation increases | Foreign and domestic |
