## Essential Reading

### Books

**Administrative State**:

- Mashaw, Jerry. *Creating the Administrative Constitution*. Yale University Press, 2012.
- Rosenbloom, David. *Administrative Law for Public Managers*. Routledge, 2015.
- Lewis, David. *The Politics of Presidential Appointments*. Princeton University Press, 2008.

**Civil Service**:

- Ingraham, Patricia. *The Foundation of Merit*. Johns Hopkins University Press, 1995.
- Moynihan, Donald. *The Dynamics of Performance Management*. Georgetown University Press, 2008.
- Light, Paul. *A Government Ill Executed*. Harvard University Press, 2008.

**Regulatory Agencies**:

- Carpenter, Daniel. *Reputation and Power*. Princeton University Press, 2010.
- Croley, Steven. *Regulation and Public Interests*. Princeton University Press, 2008.
- Stigler, George. "The Theory of Economic Regulation." *Bell Journal*, 1971.
