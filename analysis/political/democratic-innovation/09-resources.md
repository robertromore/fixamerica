# Democratic Innovation: Resources

## Books

### Foundational Texts

**Fishkin, James S.** *Democracy When the People Are Thinking: Revitalizing Our Politics Through Public Deliberation*. Oxford University Press, 2018.

- The definitive guide to deliberative polling by its inventor
- Evidence from decades of experiments worldwide
- Theoretical framework for deliberation

**Fishkin, James S.** *When the People Speak: Deliberative Democracy and Public Consultation*. Oxford University Press, 2009.

- Earlier comprehensive treatment
- Case studies from multiple countries
- Addresses common objections

**Landemore, Hélène.** *Open Democracy: Reinventing Popular Rule for the Twenty-First Century*. Princeton University Press, 2020.

- Theoretical case for democratic innovation
- Argues for sortition and citizen participation
- Engages with democratic theory

**Gastil, John, and Erik Olin Wright, eds.** *Legislature by Lot: Transformative Designs for Deliberative Governance*. Verso, 2019.

- Collection of essays on sortition legislatures
- Practical design proposals
- Addresses implementation challenges

**Barber, Benjamin R.** *Strong Democracy: Participatory Politics for a New Age*. University of California Press, 1984.

- Classic critique of "thin" democracy
- Call for participatory institutions
- Influential in academic and practitioner circles

### Citizens' Assemblies

**Fournier, Patrick, et al.** *When Citizens Decide: Lessons from Citizen Assemblies on Electoral Reform*. Oxford University Press, 2011.

- Detailed study of British Columbia and Ontario assemblies
- Evidence on citizen learning and deliberation
- Lessons for assembly design

**Farrell, David M., and Jane Suiter.** *Reimagining Democracy: Lessons in Deliberative Democracy from the Irish Front Line*. Cornell University Press, 2019.

- Inside account of Irish citizens' assemblies
- How they addressed contentious issues
- Implications for other democracies

### Participatory Budgeting

**Baiocchi, Gianpaolo.** *Militants and Citizens: The Politics of Participatory Democracy in Porto Alegre*. Stanford University Press, 2005.

- Definitive study of Porto Alegre's PB
- Political context and dynamics
- Outcomes and lessons

**Wampler, Brian.** *Participatory Budgeting in Brazil: Contestation, Cooperation, and Accountability*. Penn State University Press, 2007.

- Comparative analysis across Brazilian cities
- When and why PB succeeds
- Design variations

**Gilman, Hollie Russon.** *Democracy Reinvented: Participatory Budgeting and Civic Innovation in America*. Brookings Institution Press, 2016.

- PB in the U.S. context
- Case studies from multiple cities
- Implementation lessons

### Historical and Theoretical

**Manin, Bernard.** *The Principles of Representative Government*. Cambridge University Press, 1997.

- How modern democracy differs from ancient
- History of sortition vs. election
- Theoretical framework

**Ober, Josiah.** *Democracy and Knowledge: Innovation and Learning in Classical Athens*. Princeton University Press, 2008.

- How Athenian democracy worked
- Knowledge aggregation through participation
- Relevance to modern democracy

**Elster, Jon, ed.** *Deliberative Democracy*. Cambridge University Press, 1998.

- Classic collection of essays
- Theoretical foundations
- Multiple perspectives

### Digital Democracy

**Noveck, Beth Simone.** *Wiki Government: How Technology Can Make Government Better, Democracy Stronger, and Citizens More Powerful*. Brookings Institution Press, 2009.

- Collaboration and open government
- Technology for participation
- Practical examples

**Tang, Audrey.** *Plurality: The Future of Collaborative Technology and Democracy*. 2024.

- Taiwan's digital democracy pioneer
- vTaiwan and other innovations
- Future directions
