# Political Violence: Overview

Political violence encompasses acts or threats of violence intended to influence political outcomes, intimidate political opponents, or express political grievances. This includes domestic terrorism, threats and harassment of public officials, election-related violence, and organized extremism. Addressing political violence is essential to preserving democratic discourse and peaceful transfer of power.
