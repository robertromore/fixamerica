## Essential Reading

### Books

**On Political Violence**:

- Perliger, Arie. *American Zealots: Inside Right-Wing Domestic Terrorism*. Columbia University Press, 2020.
- Neiwert, David. *Alt-America: The Rise of the Radical Right in the Age of Trump*. Verso, 2017.
- Simi, Pete and Robert Futrell. *American Swastika: Inside the White Power Movement's Hidden Spaces of Hate*. Rowman & Littlefield, 2015.

**On Radicalization**:

- McCauley, Clark and Sophia Moskalenko. *Friction: How Conflict Radicalizes Them and Us*. Oxford University Press, 2017.
- Bjørgo, Tore and John Horgan, eds. *Leaving Terrorism Behind*. Routledge, 2009.
- Moghaddam, Fathali. *The Psychology of Democracy*. American Psychological Association, 2016.

**On Political Violence Prevention**:

- Striegher, Jason-Leigh. *Early Intervention*. Lynne Rienner, 2020.
- Ellis, B. Heidi and Saida Abdi. *Building Community Resilience to Violent Extremism*. Guilford Press, 2021.
