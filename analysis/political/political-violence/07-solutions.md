# Political Violence: Solutions

## Framework for Response

Comprehensive response to political violence requires:

1. **Prevention**: Addressing root causes and intervening early
2. **Protection**: Securing potential targets
3. **Investigation**: Detecting and disrupting threats
4. **Prosecution**: Holding perpetrators accountable
5. **Resilience**: Strengthening democratic institutions and norms

## Legal Framework Strengthening

### Problem

Domestic terrorism legal framework is weaker than international terrorism framework.

### Solution: Domestic Terrorism Prevention Act

**Key Provisions**:

- Create dedicated domestic terrorism offices in FBI, DHS, DOJ
- Require annual reporting on domestic terrorism threats
- Authorize resources specifically for domestic terrorism
- Enhance penalties for terrorism-related offenses
- Create domestic terrorism charge (carefully drafted)

**Important Safeguards**:

| Safeguard | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| No ideology-based designation | Avoid First Amendment issues |
| Conduct-based definitions | Focus on violence, not belief |
| Civil liberties protections | Prevent abuse |
| Oversight requirements | Congressional and IG oversight |
| Transparency | Public reporting |

**What NOT to Do**:

- Domestic terrorist organization designation (too broad)
- Ideology-based categories
- Expanded surveillance without warrants
- Guilt by association

### Solution: Enhanced Threat Prosecution

**True Threats Enhancement**:

- Clearer statutory definition of true threats
- Penalties appropriate to severity
- Social media threats included
- Protection for all public officials

**Conspiracy and Obstruction**:

- Clarify application to political violence
- Enhance penalties for election-related violence
- Address militia coordination

## Protection Improvements

### Problem

Many public officials, election workers, and potential targets lack adequate protection.

### Solution: Comprehensive Protection Program

**Federal Officials**:

- Enhanced Capitol Police jurisdiction
- USMS protection extended to more judges
- Security funding for congressional district offices
- Threat intelligence sharing

**State and Local Officials**:

- Federal grants for security
- Threat assessment training
- Security consultation services
- Emergency response protocols

**Election Worker Protection Act**:

| Provision | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| Federal penalties for threats | Clear deterrent |
| Personal information protection | Reduce targeting |
| Security grants to states | Fund local protection |
| Training programs | Better threat response |

### Solution: Targeted Community Protection

**Houses of Worship**:

- Security grant expansion
- Threat assessment services
- Community coordination
- Emergency planning

**Schools and Public Gatherings**:

- Threat assessment programs
- Security improvements
- Coordination with law enforcement
- De-escalation training

## Prevention and Intervention

### Problem

Prevention and intervention programs are underdeveloped.

### Solution: Comprehensive Prevention Framework

**Early Intervention**:

| Stage | Intervention |
|-------|--------------|
| At-risk | Mental health, social services |
| Radicalizing | Targeted intervention |
| Mobilizing | Law enforcement engagement |
| Post-violence | Prosecution, de-radicalization |

**Prevention Programs**:

- School-based programs
- Community-based prevention
- Mental health integration
- Social media monitoring (with safeguards)

### Solution: Off-Ramps and Exit Programs

**Life After Hate Model**:

- Former extremist counselors
- Family intervention support
- Reintegration services
- Long-term support

**Funding**:

- Dedicated federal funding
- Non-profit partnerships
- Research and evaluation
- Scaling successful programs

## Technology and Platforms

### Problem

Online platforms enable radicalization and operational planning.

### Solution: Platform Accountability

**Transparency Requirements**:

- Report on extremist content prevalence
- Algorithm audit access for researchers
- Disclosure of moderation decisions
- Advertising transparency

**Amplification Responsibility**:

- Don't require removal (First Amendment)
- Responsibility for algorithmic amplification
- Recommendation system accountability
- User control over algorithms

**Section 230 Modification**:

- Maintain core liability protection
- Carve-out for algorithm-driven amplification
- Research access requirements
- Transparency mandates

### Solution: Information Sharing

**Public-Private Partnership**:

- Threat information sharing
- Content moderation coordination
- Research collaboration
- Clear legal framework for sharing

**Cross-Platform Coordination**:

- Shared extremist databases
- Coordinated response to threats
- Best practice sharing
- Industry standards

## Addressing Root Causes

### Problem

Political violence emerges from broader social and political dysfunction.

### Solution: Address Underlying Factors

**Polarization Reduction**:

- Support for cross-partisan dialogue
- Media literacy education
- Local community building
- Institutional trust rebuilding

**Economic Factors**:

- Address economic anxiety
- Opportunity in affected communities
- Social safety net strengthening
- Rural development

**Mental Health**:

- Increased mental health services
- Crisis intervention
- Integration with prevention
- Community-based care

### Solution: Political Norm Restoration

**Leadership Responsibility**:

- Clear condemnation of all political violence
- Reject violent rhetoric
- Accept election outcomes
- Model civil discourse

**Institutional Responses**:

- Congressional censure for violent rhetoric
- Party accountability
- Media responsibility
- Public education

## Law Enforcement Improvements

### Problem

Law enforcement faces resource, intelligence, and coordination challenges.

### Solution: Enhanced Capacity

**Resources**:

| Area | Investment |
|------|------------|
| FBI domestic terrorism agents | Significant increase |
| Task force support | Enhanced funding |
| State/local assistance | Grant programs |
| Training | Specialized programs |

**Intelligence**:

- Enhanced domestic intelligence collection (with safeguards)
- Improved information sharing
- Open source intelligence capabilities
- Social media monitoring (appropriate limits)

### Solution: Improved Coordination

**Federal-State-Local**:

- Joint Terrorism Task Force enhancement
- Information sharing systems
- Threat assessment coordination
- Unified response protocols

**Interagency**:

- FBI-DHS coordination
- Intelligence community integration
- DOJ prosecution coordination
- White House coordination

## Election Security

### Problem

Elections face unprecedented threats of violence and disruption.

### Solution: Election Security Package

**Physical Security**:

- Polling place security grants
- Drop box protection
- Election facility hardening
- Worker protection protocols

**Legal Protections**:

- Enhanced penalties for election interference
- Federal jurisdiction for election worker threats
- Protection for ballot counting
- Certification protection

**Planning and Coordination**:

- Election security task forces
- Multi-agency coordination
- Emergency protocols
- Public communication plans
