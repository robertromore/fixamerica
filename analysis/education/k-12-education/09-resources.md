# K-12 Public Education: Resources

## Overview

This resource guide provides citations, data sources, and further reading for understanding and reforming American K-12 education. Sources are organized by topic and type.

## Key Legal References

### Federal Law

**Elementary and Secondary Education Act (ESEA)**

- Original: Pub. L. 89-10 (1965)
- Current: Every Student Succeeds Act, Pub. L. 114-95 (2015)
- 20 U.S.C. Chapter 70

**Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA)**

- 20 U.S.C. Chapter 33
- 34 C.F.R. Part 300 (regulations)

**Civil Rights Act of 1964**

- Title VI: 42 U.S.C. ยง 2000d (prohibits discrimination in federally funded programs)

**Equal Educational Opportunities Act of 1974**

- 20 U.S.C. ยง 1703

### Landmark Court Cases

**Desegregation**

- *Brown v. Board of Education*, 347 U.S. 483 (1954)
- *Green v. County School Board*, 391 U.S. 430 (1968)
- *Swann v. Charlotte-Mecklenburg*, 402 U.S. 1 (1971)
- *Milliken v. Bradley*, 418 U.S. 717 (1974)
- *Parents Involved in Community Schools v. Seattle*, 551 U.S. 701 (2007)

**School Finance**

- *San Antonio Independent School District v. Rodriguez*, 411 U.S. 1 (1973)
- *Serrano v. Priest*, 5 Cal.3d 584 (1971) (California)
- *Rose v. Council for Better Education*, 790 S.W.2d 186 (Ky. 1989)
- *Abbott v. Burke* (series), (N.J. 1985-2011)
- *DeRolph v. State*, 78 Ohio St.3d 193 (1997) (Ohio)

**Student Rights**

- *Tinker v. Des Moines*, 393 U.S. 503 (1969) (free speech)
- *Goss v. Lopez*, 419 U.S. 565 (1975) (due process in discipline)
- *Plyler v. Doe*, 457 U.S. 202 (1982) (undocumented students' rights)

## Academic Research

### Foundational Studies

**Coleman Report**

- Coleman, James S., et al. *Equality of Educational Opportunity*. U.S. Government Printing Office, 1966.
- Foundational study on school effects and family background

**Does Money Matter?**

- Hanushek, Eric A. "The Economics of Schooling: Production and Efficiency in Public Schools." *Journal of Economic Literature* 24.3 (1986): 1141-1177.
- Jackson, C. Kirabo, Rucker C. Johnson, and Claudia Persico. "The Effects of School Spending on Educational and Economic Outcomes: Evidence from School Finance Reforms." *Quarterly Journal of Economics* 131.1 (2016): 157-218.

**School Desegregation Effects**

- Johnson, Rucker C. "Long-Run Impacts of School Desegregation & School Quality on Adult Attainments." NBER Working Paper 16664, 2011.
- Reardon, Sean F., and Ann Owens. "60 Years After Brown: Trends and Consequences of School Segregation." *Annual Review of Sociology* 40 (2014): 199-218.

### Achievement Gaps

- Reardon, Sean F. "The Widening Academic Achievement Gap Between the Rich and the Poor: New Evidence and Possible Explanations." In *Whither Opportunity?*, edited by Greg J. Duncan and Richard J. Murnane. Russell Sage, 2011.
- Fryer, Roland G., and Steven D. Levitt. "The Black-White Test Score Gap Through Third Grade." *American Law and Economics Review* 8.2 (2006): 249-281.

### Teacher Effects

- Chetty, Raj, John N. Friedman, and Jonah E. Rockoff. "Measuring the Impacts of Teachers I: Evaluating Bias in Teacher Value-Added Estimates." *American Economic Review* 104.9 (2014): 2593-2632.
- Rivkin, Steven G., Eric A. Hanushek, and John F. Kain. "Teachers, Schools, and Academic Achievement." *Econometrica* 73.2 (2005): 417-458.

### School Funding

- Baker, Bruce D. *Educational Inequality and School Finance: Why Money Matters for America's Students*. Harvard Education Press, 2018.
- Lafortune, Julien, Jesse Rothstein, and Diane Whitmore Schanzenbach. "School Finance Reform and the Distribution of Student Achievement." *American Economic Journal: Applied Economics* 10.2 (2018): 1-26.

### High-Dosage Tutoring

- Nickow, Andre, Philip Oreopoulos, and Vincent Quan. "The Impressive Effects of Tutoring on PreK-12 Learning: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis of the Experimental Evidence." NBER Working Paper 27476, 2020.
- Robinson, Carly D., et al. "Accelerating Student Learning with High-Dosage Tutoring." EdResearch for Recovery Project, 2021.

### Class Size

- Schanzenbach, Diane Whitmore. "Does Class Size Matter?" National Education Policy Center, 2014.
- Krueger, Alan B. "Experimental Estimates of Education Production Functions." *Quarterly Journal of Economics* 114.2 (1999): 497-532.
