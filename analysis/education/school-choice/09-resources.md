# School Choice: Resources

## Overview

This document provides a curated guide to research, data sources, and organizations for those seeking to understand school choice policy in greater depth.

## Academic Research

### Foundational Studies

| Study | Authors | Year | Key Finding |
|-------|---------|------|-------------|
| "Effects of School Choice on Curriculum and Atmosphere" | Hoxby | 2000 | Competition modestly improves TPS performance |
| "The Effect of Charter Schools on Charter Students and Public Schools" | CREDO | 2009 | Urban charters outperform, non-urban neutral |
| "A Comprehensive Assessment of Charter School Achievement" | CREDO | 2023 | Updated findings on charter performance |
| "The Louisiana Scholarship Program" | Abdulkadiroglu et al. | 2018 | Vouchers: -0.4 SD math, -0.3 SD ELA |
| "Segregation, Race, and Charter School Possibilities" | Frankenberg et al. | 2011 | 70% of Black charter students in 90%+ minority schools |

## Legal Resources

### Key Court Decisions

| Case | Year | Holding |
|------|------|---------|
| *Zelman v. Simmons-Harris* | 2002 | Vouchers to religious schools constitutional |
| *Espinoza v. Montana* | 2020 | Cannot exclude religious schools from programs |
| *Carson v. Makin* | 2022 | Must include religious schools in aid programs |
| *Davey v. Locke* | 2004 | May exclude devotional training from scholarships |

### State Constitutional Provisions

|----------------|--------|
