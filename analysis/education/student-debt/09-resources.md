# Student Debt: Resources

## Primary Sources

### Federal Government Data

**Federal Student Aid (FSA)**

- Portfolio Summary: <https://studentaid.gov/data-center/student/portfolio>
- Loan Status Reports: Quarterly updates on repayment, default, and forbearance
- PSLF Reports: Monthly data on applications and approvals
- Direct Loan origination data by institution and program

**Federal Reserve**

- Consumer Credit G.19 Release: <https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g19/current/>
- Survey of Consumer Finances: Detailed household debt data
- Report on the Economic Well-Being of U.S. Households (SHED): Annual survey with student debt questions
- Quarterly Report on Household Debt and Credit: New York Fed

**Department of Education**

- College Scorecard: <https://collegescorecard.ed.gov/>
- IPEDS (Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System): Institutional data
- National Postsecondary Student Aid Study (NPSAS): Detailed borrower surveys
- Beginning Postsecondary Students Study: Longitudinal outcomes

**Government Accountability Office (GAO)**

- Reports on student loan programs: <https://www.gao.gov/topics/education>
- Servicer oversight reports
- PSLF implementation reviews
- For-profit college investigations

**Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)**

- Student Loan Servicing Reports: Annual analysis
- Consumer Complaint Database: <https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/>
- Enforcement actions against servicers
- Research publications on student lending

### Legal Sources

**Statutes**

- Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended (20 U.S.C. Section 1001 et seq.)
- Title IV Student Aid Programs (20 U.S.C. Section 1070 et seq.)
- Bankruptcy Code Section 523(a)(8) (student loan exception)
- HEROES Act (20 U.S.C. Section 1098bb)
- CARES Act student loan provisions (P.L. 116-136)

**Regulations**

- 34 C.F.R. Parts 600-699 (Federal Student Aid regulations)
- Income-Driven Repayment regulations
- PSLF regulations
- Borrower Defense regulations

**Key Court Cases**

- *Biden v. Nebraska*, 600 U.S. ___ (2023): Blocked broad cancellation under HEROES Act
- *Brunner v. New York State Higher Education Services Corp.*, 831 F.2d 395 (2d Cir. 1987): Established undue hardship test
- *Sweet v. Cardona* (N.D. Cal.): Borrower Defense class action
- *Department of Education v. Brown*, 600 U.S. ___ (2023): Standing issues

### Congressional Sources

**Congressional Research Service (CRS)**

- Federal Student Loans Made Through the William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program
- Student Loan Forgiveness and Repayment Programs
- The PSLF Program: An Overview
- For-Profit Colleges: Background and Issues

**Congressional Budget Office (CBO)**

- Cost estimates for student loan legislation
- Fair-value accounting analyses
- Higher education spending projections

**Committee Hearings**

- Senate HELP Committee: <https://www.help.senate.gov/>
- House Education and Labor Committee: <https://edlabor.house.gov/>
