# Higher Education: Resources

## Key Federal Data Sources

### Department of Education

**Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System (IPEDS)**

- Universe of all Title IV institutions
- Enrollment, completion, finance, staffing
- Annual data collection
- <https://nces.ed.gov/ipeds/>

**College Scorecard**

- Institution and program-level outcomes
- Earnings, debt, completion rates
- Student demographics
- <https://collegescorecard.ed.gov/>

**National Student Loan Data System (NSLDS)**

- Borrower-level loan data
- Repayment and default information
- Federal aid history
- <https://nslds.ed.gov/>

**Federal Student Aid Data Center**

- Aid volume and recipient data
- Loan portfolio statistics
- School participation
- <https://studentaid.gov/data-center/>

### Other Federal Sources

**Bureau of Labor Statistics**

- Employment projections by education
- Occupational Outlook Handbook
- Earnings data
- <https://www.bls.gov/>

**Census Bureau**

- Educational attainment data
- American Community Survey
- Current Population Survey
- <https://www.census.gov/topics/education.html>

**Government Accountability Office (GAO)**

- Program evaluations
- Policy analyses
- Fraud investigations
- <https://www.gao.gov/>

## Legal and Legislative Resources

### Federal Law

**Higher Education Act (20 U.S.C. Chapter 28)**

- Main statutory framework
- Authorization, appropriations
- Program requirements

**Key Provisions**

- Title IV: Student Assistance
- Title II: Teacher Quality
- Title III: Strengthening Institutions
- Title VI: International Education

### Regulatory Framework

**34 C.F.R. Parts 600-699**

- Federal student aid regulations
- Institutional eligibility
- Program integrity
