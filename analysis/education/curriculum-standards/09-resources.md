# Curriculum Standards: Resources

## Research and Data Sources

### Curriculum Quality Research

**EdReports**

- Website: edreports.org
- Independent curriculum reviews
- Alignment to standards ratings
- Free, publicly accessible reviews
- Most comprehensive curriculum quality resource

**What Works Clearinghouse**

- Website: ies.ed.gov/ncee/wwc
- IES evidence reviews
- Intervention effectiveness ratings
- Practice guides for implementation
- Research standards for education

**RAND Corporation Education Research**

- Website: rand.org/education
- American Teacher Panel surveys
- Curriculum implementation research
- Policy analysis and evaluation

**Johns Hopkins Evidence for ESSA**

- Website: evidenceforessa.org
- Program ratings by ESSA evidence tiers
- Searchable database by subject and grade
- Focus on programs with rigorous evidence

### Standards Quality Research

**Fordham Institute Standards Reviews**

- Website: fordhaminstitute.org
- State standards quality ratings
- Subject-specific evaluations
- ELA, math, science, history ratings
- Periodic updates

**Achieve Standards Benchmarking**

- Website: achieve.org
- International comparisons
- College and career readiness alignment
- Standards coherence analysis

### Assessment and Achievement Data

**National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP)**

- Website: nces.ed.gov/nationsreportcard
- National and state achievement data
- Trend data over time
- Disaggregated by demographics
- "Nation's Report Card"

**State Assessment Results**

- Each state department of education
- Annual results by district, school
- Disaggregated data available
- Varies in accessibility and detail

**PISA (International Comparison)**

- Website: oecd.org/pisa
- International student assessment
- Reading, math, science comparisons
- 15-year-old students assessed
- Published every three years

**TIMSS (International Comparison)**

- Website: timssandpirls.bc.edu
- Math and science international comparison
- 4th and 8th grade assessments
- Published every four years

**American Library Association Office for Intellectual Freedom**

**PEN America**

**National Coalition Against Censorship**

- Website: ncac.org
- Anti-censorship advocacy
- Educational resources
- Case documentation
