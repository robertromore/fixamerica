# Education: Resources

## Overview

This section compiles key resources for understanding education policy, tracking data, finding organizations, and supporting advocacy. Resources span academic research, policy reports, data sources, and tools for action across K-12, higher education, and early childhood.

| **Brown v. Board of Education** | 347 U.S. 483 (1954) | Segregation unconstitutional |
| **San Antonio v. Rodriguez** | 411 U.S. 1 (1973) | No federal right to equal funding |

| **Students for Fair Admissions v. Harvard** | 600 U.S. 181 (2023) | Race-conscious admissions unconstitutional |
