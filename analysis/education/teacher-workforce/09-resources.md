# Teacher Workforce: Resources

## Key Research Organizations

### Learning Policy Institute

**Website**: [learningpolicyinstitute.org](https://learningpolicyinstitute.org/)

The Learning Policy Institute, founded by Linda Darling-Hammond, produces the most comprehensive research on teacher workforce issues. Key publications:

- *A Coming Crisis in Teaching? Teacher Supply, Demand, and Shortages in the U.S.* (2016, updated annually)
- *Developing Effective Principals* (2022)
- *Teacher Residencies: Building a High-Quality, Sustainable Workforce* (2019)
- *Investing in Effective School Leadership* (2020)

### Economic Policy Institute

**Website**: [epi.org](https://epi.org/)

EPI publishes the annual teacher pay penalty analysis, the most authoritative accounting of teacher compensation gaps:

- *Teacher Pay Penalty Dips but Persists in 2019* (Allegretto & Mishel)
- *The Teacher Weekly Wage Penalty Hit 21.4 Percent in 2018* (2019)
- Annual updates to teacher wage data

### National Center for Education Statistics (NCES)

**Website**: [nces.ed.gov](https://nces.ed.gov/)

Federal statistical agency providing authoritative data on:

- Schools and Staffing Survey (SASS) / National Teacher and Principal Survey (NTPS)
- Teacher attrition and mobility
- Teacher salaries by state and district
- Teacher preparation program completers

### RAND Corporation

**Website**: [rand.org/education](https://rand.org/education)

RAND provides rigorous policy research on teacher workforce issues:

- *Teachers' Views on What Should Be Done* (2023)
- *The State of the American Teacher and American Principal* (annual survey)
- Research on teacher evaluation, compensation, and professional development

## Foundational Research

### Teacher Effectiveness

Chetty, R., Friedman, J., & Rockoff, J. (2014). Measuring the impacts of teachers I: Evaluating bias in teacher value-added estimates. *American Economic Review*, 104(9), 2593-2632.

*Landmark study demonstrating teacher effects on long-term student outcomes including earnings.*

Rivkin, S., Hanushek, E., & Kain, J. (2005). Teachers, schools, and academic achievement. *Econometrica*, 73(2), 417-458.

*Foundational study establishing teacher effectiveness as largest in-school factor.*

Kane, T., & Staiger, D. (2008). Estimating teacher impacts on student achievement: An experimental evaluation. *NBER Working Paper 14607*.

*Random assignment study validating teacher effectiveness measures.*

### Teacher Compensation

Allegretto, S., & Mishel, L. (2020). Teacher pay penalty dips but persists in 2019. *Economic Policy Institute*.

*Annual analysis of teacher pay relative to comparable professions.*

Hendricks, M. (2014). Does it pay to pay teachers more? Evidence from Texas. *Journal of Public Economics*, 109, 50-63.

*Research showing salary effects on teacher quality and student outcomes.*

Figlio, D. (1997). Teacher salaries and teacher quality. *Economics Letters*, 55(2), 267-271.

*Early demonstration that higher salaries attract better-qualified teachers.*

### Teacher Preparation

Boyd, D., Grossman, P., Lankford, H., Loeb, S., & Wyckoff, J. (2009). Teacher preparation and student achievement. *Educational Evaluation and Policy Analysis*, 31(4), 416-440.

*Comprehensive analysis of how preparation features affect teacher effectiveness.*

Darling-Hammond, L. (2017). Teacher education around the world: What can we learn from international practice? *European Journal of Teacher Education*, 40(3), 291-309.

*International comparison of teacher preparation systems.*

Papay, J., West, M., Fullerton, J., & Kane, T. (2012). Does an urban teacher residency increase student achievement? Early evidence from Boston. *Educational Evaluation and Policy Analysis*, 34(4), 413-434.

*Evidence on residency program effectiveness.*

### Teacher Retention

Ingersoll, R. (2001). Teacher turnover and teacher shortages: An organizational analysis. *American Educational Research Journal*, 38(3), 499-534.

*Foundational analysis of why teachers leave and what predicts retention.*

Simon, N., & Johnson, S. M. (2015). Teacher turnover in high-poverty schools: What we know and can do. *Teachers College Record*, 117(3), 1-36.

*Analysis of turnover patterns in high-need schools.*

Kraft, M., Marinell, W., & Shen-Wei Yee, D. (2016). School organizational contexts, teacher turnover, and student achievement. *American Educational Research Journal*, 53(5), 1411-1449.

*Research linking school conditions to retention and outcomes.*

### Teacher Diversity

Gershenson, S., Hart, C., Hyman, J., Lindsay, C., & Papageorge, N. W. (2018). The long-run impacts of same-race teachers. *NBER Working Paper 25254*.

*Landmark study showing students benefit from same-race teachers.*

Carver-Thomas, D. (2018). Diversifying the teaching profession: How to recruit and retain teachers of color. *Learning Policy Institute*.

*Comprehensive analysis of barriers and strategies for teacher diversity.*

Ingersoll, R., May, H., & Collins, G. (2019). Recruitment, employment, retention and the minority teacher shortage. *Education Policy Analysis Archives*, 27(37).

*Data analysis of teacher diversity trends and causes.*

## Data Sources

### National Data

**Schools and Staffing Survey (SASS) / National Teacher and Principal Survey (NTPS)**

- Most comprehensive federal data on teacher workforce
- Includes compensation, working conditions, demographics, preparation
- [nces.ed.gov/surveys/ntps/](https://nces.ed.gov/surveys/ntps/)

**Teacher Attrition and Mobility Data**

- Annual data on teacher turnover
- State and school-level breakdowns
- [nces.ed.gov/pubs2014/2014077.pdf](https://nces.ed.gov/pubs2014/2014077.pdf)

**Title II Reports**

- Teacher preparation program data
- State-level reporting on teacher quality
- [title2.ed.gov](https://title2.ed.gov/)

### State Data

**State education agency websites**: Most states publish teacher workforce data including:

- Salary schedules and average compensation
- Vacancy and shortage reports
- Certification data
- Demographic information

**National Council on Teacher Quality (NCTQ)**

- State Teacher Policy Database
- Annual State of the States report
- Teacher prep program reviews
- [nctq.org](https://nctq.org/)

### Survey Data

**EdWeek Research Center**

- Annual teacher surveys on working conditions, satisfaction
- Salary analyses and state comparisons
- [edweek.org/research-center](https://edweek.org/research-center)

**PDK/Gallup Poll**

- Annual poll of attitudes toward public schools
- Includes teacher-focused questions
- [pdkpoll.org](https://pdkpoll.org/)
