# Education: Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming American education requires a phased approach that builds political coalitions, demonstrates success through pilot programs, and progressively addresses structural barriers. This roadmap sequences reforms from immediately achievable to systemic transformation, recognizing that education policy operates at federal, state, and local levels with different levers and timelines at each.

## Risks and Contingencies

### Political Risks

| Risk | Mitigation | Contingency |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Election losses** | Build bipartisan support | State/local action |
| **Culture war escalation** | Focus on achievement | Defend public schools |
| **Union opposition** | Engage on working conditions | Work around resistance |
| **Voucher expansion** | Demonstrate public school quality | Litigation, regulation |
| **Federal retrenchment** | State self-reliance | State-level funding |

### Implementation Risks

| Risk | Mitigation | Contingency |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Teacher shortage worsens** | Pipeline investment | Alternative pathways |
| **Learning loss persists** | Intensive intervention | Extended timeline |
| **Integration resistance** | Voluntary programs | Magnet strategies |
| **Reform fatigue** | Focus on basics | Incremental approach |
| **Capacity gaps** | Technical assistance | Phased rollout |

### Economic Risks

| Risk | Mitigation | Contingency |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Recession** | Countercyclical funding | Maintenance of effort |
| **Pension obligations** | Reform, refinancing | State assistance |
| **Construction costs** | Prefab, renovation | Prioritization |
| **Higher ed default crisis** | IDR expansion | Emergency relief |
| **State budget pressure** | Federal support | Revenue reform |

### Contingency Plans

| If This Fails | Then |
|---------------|------|
| Federal funding blocked | State litigation, revenue reform |
| Integration rejected | Voluntary magnet programs |
| Teacher pay stalls | Non-salary benefits, conditions |
| Vouchers expand | Accountability requirements |
| Standards resistance | Local flexibility within equity |
