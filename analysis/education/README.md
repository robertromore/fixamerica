# Education: Overview

American education faces a paradox: the nation spends more per student than almost any other developed country yet produces mediocre outcomes, with vast disparities by race, income, and geography. From early childhood through higher education, the system fails to deliver on its promise of equal opportunity. This domain analyzes the structural causes of educational dysfunction and proposes evidence-based reforms.
