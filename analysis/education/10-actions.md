|--------|-------------|

## Quick Reference

### If You Have 5 Minutes

- Call your legislator about education funding
- Email your school board member
- Share education news on social media
- Sign up for an education newsletter

### If You Have 1 Hour

- Attend a school board meeting
- Volunteer at your child's school
- Write a letter to the editor
- Research local education issues

### If You Have Ongoing Commitment

- Join an education advocacy organization
- Run for school board
- Organize a parent group
- Mentor a student
- Support a teacher candidate
- Build a community coalition
