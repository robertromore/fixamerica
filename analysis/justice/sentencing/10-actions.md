### Contact Elected Officials

**Federal Level**:

| Official | Message |
|----------|---------|
| Senators | Support mandatory minimum reform |
| Representatives | Co-sponsor EQUAL Act, sentencing reform |
| President | Expand clemency, direct DOJ policy |

**Sample Message Points**:

- Support eliminating federal mandatory minimums
- Make First Step Act 924(c) fix retroactive
- Support second look legislation
- Expand compassionate release
- Prioritize racial disparity reduction

**State Level**:

| Official | Message |
|----------|---------|
| State legislators | Support state sentencing reform |
| Governor | Expand clemency, parole reform |
| Attorney General | Policy changes |

## Professional Actions

### Legal Professionals

**Defense Attorneys**:

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Challenge mandatory minimums | Build record for reform |
| Aggressive resentencing | Use available mechanisms |
| Document disparity | Create evidentiary record |
| Pro bono resentencing | Represent those seeking relief |

**Prosecutors** (Reform-Minded):

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Decline mandatory minimum charges | Direct impact |
| Support resentencing | Agree to reductions |
| Change office policy | Systemic effect |
| Speak publicly | Shift narrative |

**Judges**:

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Use variance authority | Individualized sentences |
| Write opinions | Document injustice |
| Speak publicly | (Carefully) advocate for reform |
| Participate in reform | Judicial conferences, commissions |
