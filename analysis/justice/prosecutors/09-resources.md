## Legal Resources

### Key Cases

**Prosecutorial Discretion**:

| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| *Bordenkircher v. Hayes* | 434 U.S. 357 (1978) | Charging threats for plea leverage valid |
| *Wayte v. United States* | 470 U.S. 598 (1985) | High bar for selective prosecution claims |
| *United States v. Armstrong* | 517 U.S. 456 (1996) | Nearly impossible to get discovery in discrimination claims |

**Brady Doctrine**:

| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| *Brady v. Maryland* | 373 U.S. 83 (1963) | Must disclose exculpatory evidence |
| *Giglio v. United States* | 405 U.S. 150 (1972) | Must disclose impeachment evidence |
| *Kyles v. Whitley* | 514 U.S. 419 (1995) | Brady applies to police files |
| *Strickler v. Greene* | 527 U.S. 263 (1999) | Materiality standard clarified |

**Immunity**:

| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| *Imbler v. Pachtman* | 424 U.S. 409 (1976) | Absolute immunity for prosecutorial functions |
| *Burns v. Reed* | 500 U.S. 478 (1991) | Qualified immunity for some acts |
| *Connick v. Thompson* | 563 U.S. 51 (2011) | High bar for office liability |
