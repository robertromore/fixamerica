# Prosecutorial Reform: Actions

## Individual Actions

### Voter Actions

**District Attorney Elections**:

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| Learn about your DA | Research current policies, charging data, CIU status |
| Find election dates | DA elections often in primary or off-year |
| Research candidates | Policy positions, endorsements, background |
| Vote in DA elections | Turnout is typically low; your vote matters more |
| Volunteer for candidates | Reform candidates need grassroots support |
| Donate to campaigns | DA races often underfunded |

**Where to Find Information**:

| Resource | What It Provides |
|----------|-----------------|
| Local news | Coverage of DA races |
| Bolts magazine | Prosecution election coverage |
| Color of Change voter guides | Progressive candidate information |
| ACLU voter guides | Civil liberties positions |
| League of Women Voters | Nonpartisan information |
