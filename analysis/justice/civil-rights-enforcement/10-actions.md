# Actions for Civil Rights Enforcement Reform

## Overview

Meaningful civil rights enforcement reform requires sustained action at every level—from individual citizens to national organizations. This document provides specific, actionable steps organized by actor type and impact level.

## Call to Action

Civil rights enforcement reform will not happen without sustained pressure from all sectors of society. Each person reading this document can take meaningful action:

1. **Today**: Contact your representatives about qualified immunity reform
2. **This week**: Join a local police accountability organization
3. **This month**: Attend a city council or oversight board meeting
4. **This year**: Vote for candidates committed to police accountability
5. **Always**: Document, educate, organize, and demand change

The accountability gap exists because those in power benefit from it. Closing that gap requires collective action from those who demand justice.
