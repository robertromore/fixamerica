# Courts Reform: Actions

## For Individuals

### Educate Yourself

**Learn About Your Courts**:

- How is your public defender funded?
- What is your jurisdiction's bail system?
- How are judges selected in your state?
- What are court fees in your area?

**Understand the System**:

- Read books on public defense and courts
- Follow court reform news
- Attend a court session (public)
- Learn about plea bargaining

### Support Bail Funds

**Community Bail Funds**:

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Donate | Local and national bail funds |
| Volunteer | Administrative support |
| Fundraise | Community events |
| Spread awareness | Share information |

**National Organizations**:

- The Bail Project
- National Bail Fund Network
- Local community bail funds

### Jury Service

**Serve When Called**:

| Action | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Don't avoid service | System needs fair jurors |
| Take it seriously | Lives at stake |
| Presume innocence | Constitutional right |
| Deliberate carefully | Beyond reasonable doubt |

**Know Your Power**:

- Jurors can acquit for any reason
- Hold prosecution to its burden
- Don't defer to authority
- Question evidence carefully

### Vote and Participate

**Key Elections**:

| Office | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Judges | Bail, sentencing, fairness |
| District attorney | Adversary in system |
| State legislators | Funding, reform laws |
| Local officials | Court funding |

**Beyond Voting**:

- Research judicial candidates
- Attend candidate forums
- Support reform candidates
- Ask about court reform
