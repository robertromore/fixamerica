# Surveillance Technology in Criminal Justice: Resources

## Introduction

This document compiles essential resources for understanding, researching, and advocating on surveillance technology in criminal justice. It includes organizations, research reports, books, datasets, and tools for advocates, researchers, journalists, and community members.
