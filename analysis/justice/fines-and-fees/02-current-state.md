# Current State of Fines, Fees, and Monetary Sanctions

## The Modern Fines and Fees Landscape

Today's justice system imposes a bewildering array of monetary obligations on defendants, creating a complex web of debt that can persist for decades. What begins as a simple traffic ticket or misdemeanor can snowball into thousands of dollars in compounding obligations.

## Types of Monetary Sanctions

### Criminal Fines

Fines are punitive penalties intended as punishment for offenses.

| Offense Level | Typical Fine Range | Federal Maximum |
|---------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Infractions | $25-$500 | $5,000 |
| Misdemeanors | $100-$5,000 | $100,000 |
| Felonies | $1,000-$250,000 | $250,000+ |
| Organizational crimes | — | $500 million+ |
