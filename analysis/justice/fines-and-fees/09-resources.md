## Legal Resources

### Key Court Decisions

| Case | Year | Holding |
|------|------|---------|
| Bearden v. Georgia | 1983 | Cannot jail for non-payment without ability inquiry |
| Tate v. Short | 1971 | Cannot auto-convert fines to jail for indigents |
| Williams v. Illinois | 1970 | Cannot extend incarceration beyond max for non-payment |
| Timbs v. Indiana | 2019 | Excessive Fines Clause applies to states |
| Nelson v. Colorado | 2017 | Exonerated must receive refund |
