# Victims' Rights and Services: Implementation Roadmap

## Timeline for Reform

### Phase 1: Foundation Building (Year 1)

#### Federal Actions

| Action | Timeline | Lead Entity | Resources Needed |
|--------|----------|-------------|------------------|
| Pass VOCA Fix 2.0 legislation | Months 1-6 | Congress | Legislative drafting, advocacy |
| Establish $3 billion floor guarantee | Month 6 | Congress | Budget allocation |
| OVC guidance on multi-year grants | Months 3-6 | DOJ/OVC | Staff time, stakeholder input |
| Expand VINE to all federal cases | Months 6-12 | DOJ | Technology investment |
| U-visa cap increase (25,000 to 50,000) | Months 1-12 | Congress | Legislative action |
| Launch national trauma-informed training | Month 6 | OVC | Training development, funding |

**Milestone**: Stable VOCA funding floor in place by end of Year 1.

#### State Actions

| Action | Timeline | Lead Entity | Resources Needed |
|--------|----------|-------------|------------------|
| Assess current victim services gaps | Months 1-3 | State VOCA administrators | Assessment tools, data |
| Identify compensation program barriers | Months 1-4 | Compensation boards | Stakeholder input |
| Begin legislative drafting for reforms | Months 4-8 | Victim advocates, legislators | Model legislation |
| Launch provider capacity assessment | Months 3-6 | State agencies | Survey, site visits |
| Establish victim rights ombudsman pilot | Months 6-12 | Select states | Staff, authority |

**Milestone**: Comprehensive state-by-state gap assessment completed.

#### Local Actions

| Action | Timeline | Lead Entity | Resources Needed |
|--------|----------|-------------|------------------|
| Audit current notification practices | Months 1-3 | Prosecutor offices | Staff time |
| Establish victim advisory councils | Months 3-6 | DA offices, courts | Meeting support |
| Begin trauma-informed training | Months 1-6 | Law enforcement, prosecution | Training resources |
| Map underserved population needs | Months 3-6 | Service providers | Community engagement |
| Pilot restorative justice programs | Months 6-12 | Courts, prosecutors | Program design, facilitators |

**Milestone**: Local notification audits completed; gaps identified.

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|------------|---------------------|
| VOCA funding volatility | Medium | Build reserves, diversify funding |
| Political opposition | Medium | Bipartisan framing, coalition building |
| Implementation capacity | High | Phased rollout, technical assistance |

| Workforce shortages | High | Compensation investment, pipeline programs |
