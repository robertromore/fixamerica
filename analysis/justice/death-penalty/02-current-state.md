# Death Penalty Reform: Current State of Capital Punishment

## Overview

As of 2024, the United States maintains a fragmented system of capital punishment, with 27 states retaining the death penalty on the books, though only a handful actively pursue executions. This document examines the current landscape of capital punishment across the nation.

### Lethal Injection Protocols

States have struggled to obtain lethal injection drugs due to pharmaceutical company restrictions:

**One-Drug Protocol (Pentobarbital or Secobarbital):**
- Texas
- Missouri
- Georgia
- Most other active states

**Three-Drug Protocol (where used):**
1. Anesthetic (sodium thiopental/pentobarbital)
2. Paralytic agent (pancuronium bromide)
3. Potassium chloride (stops heart)

**Compounding Pharmacy Issues:**
- Lack of FDA oversight
- Quality control concerns
- Botched executions
