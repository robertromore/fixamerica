# Death Penalty Reform: Action Guide

## Overview

This guide provides concrete actions that individuals, communities, and organizations can take to advance death penalty reform and abolition. Actions are organized by actor type and time commitment, with specific steps and resources for each.

## Effectiveness Tips

### Making Calls Effective

| Tip | Explanation |
|-----|-------------|
| Be concise | 30 seconds to 1 minute |
| Be personal | Share why you care |
| Be specific | Name the bill/issue |
| Be polite | Staffers are people |
| Follow up | Thank or hold accountable |

### Making Letters Effective

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Personal connection | Why this matters to you |
| Local tie | Your community context |
| Specific ask | What action you want |
| Credibility | Your expertise/experience |
| Call to action | Clear next step |

### Making Testimony Effective

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Personal story | Emotional connection |
| Brief data | Support your point |
| Clear ask | What you want them to do |
| Leave-behind | Written summary |
| Follow-up | After hearing contact |
