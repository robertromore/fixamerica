# Solitary Confinement Reform: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles essential resources for understanding solitary confinement and advancing reform, including organizations, research, legal resources, media, and data sources.

## Legal Resources

### Key Cases

| Case | Jurisdiction | Year | Significance |
|------|--------------|------|--------------|
| *In re Medley* | U.S. Supreme Court | 1890 | Early recognition of isolation harms |
| *Ruiz v. Estelle* | Texas (5th Cir.) | 1980 | Comprehensive prison conditions |
| *Madrid v. Gomez* | California (N.D. Cal.) | 1995 | Pelican Bay conditions, mentally ill |
| *Wilkinson v. Austin* | U.S. Supreme Court | 2005 | Due process for supermax placement |
| *Ashker v. Governor* | California | 2015 | SHU settlement |
| *Peoples v. Annucci* | New York (S.D.N.Y.) | Ongoing | SHU conditions |
| *Braggs v. Dunn* | Alabama (M.D. Ala.) | 2017 | Mental health and solitary |
