# Civil Asset Forfeiture Reform

Civil asset forfeiture allows law enforcement to seize property suspected of being connected to criminal activityâ€”often without charging the owner with any crime. Originally designed to disrupt organized crime and drug trafficking, this practice has expanded dramatically, creating a system where innocent Americans can lose their cash, cars, and homes based on mere suspicion, with the burden falling on them to prove their innocence.
