# Economic Mobility: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding and advocating for economic mobility, including research institutions, data sources, advocacy organizations, and foundational texts.

## Key Research

### Foundational Studies

| Study | Authors | Key Finding |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| "Where is the Land of Opportunity?" (2014) | Chetty, Hendren, Kline, Saez | Mapped mobility across U.S. commuting zones |
| "The Fading American Dream" (2017) | Chetty, Grusky, Hell, Hendren, Manduca, Narang | Absolute mobility declined from 92% to 50% |
| "Race and Economic Opportunity" (2020) | Chetty, Hendren, Jones, Porter | Black-white mobility gap persists at all income levels |
| "The Effects of Neighborhoods on Intergenerational Mobility" (2018) | Chetty, Hendren | Moving to opportunity works; each year in better neighborhood helps |
| "Mobility Report Cards" (2017) | Chetty, Friedman, Saez, Turner, Yagan | College access and outcomes by institution |

### Program Evaluations

| Program | Study | Finding |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Moving to Opportunity | Chetty, Hendren, Katz (2016) | Young children who moved earned 31% more |
| Perry Preschool | Heckman et al. (2010) | 7-10% return on investment |
| Head Start | Deming (2009) | Long-term gains in education, health |
| EITC | Chetty, Friedman, Rockoff (2011) | Increased test scores, college attendance |
| Housing vouchers | Collinson, Ellen, Ludwig (2016) | Reduced homelessness, improved stability |
