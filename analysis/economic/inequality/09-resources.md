## Research and Reports

### Essential Academic Research

**On Income and Wealth Inequality**

- Piketty, Thomas. *Capital in the Twenty-First Century* (2013)
- Piketty, Thomas & Saez, Emmanuel. "Income Inequality in the United States" (various)
- Saez, Emmanuel & Zucman, Gabriel. *The Triumph of Injustice* (2019)
- Chetty, Raj et al. "The Fading American Dream" (2017)

**On Mobility**

- Chetty, Raj. "Equality of Opportunity Project" research
- Corak, Miles. "Income Inequality, Equality of Opportunity, and Intergenerational Mobility" (2013)
- Sawhill, Isabel & Morton, John. "Economic Mobility: Is the American Dream Alive and Well?" (2007)

**On Labor and Wages**

- Card, David & Krueger, Alan. *Myth and Measurement: The New Economics of the Minimum Wage* (1995)
- Western, Bruce & Rosenfeld, Jake. "Unions, Norms, and the Rise in U.S. Wage Inequality" (2011)
- Autor, David. Various papers on wage polarization

**On Race and Wealth**

- Oliver, Melvin & Shapiro, Thomas. *Black Wealth/White Wealth* (1995, 2006)
- Darity, William & Mullen, A. Kirsten. *From Here to Equality* (2020)
- Baradaran, Mehrsa. *The Color of Money* (2017)
