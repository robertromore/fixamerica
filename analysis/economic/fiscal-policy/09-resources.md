# Fiscal Policy: Resources

## Overview

This document provides resources for deeper exploration of fiscal policy issues, including government sources, academic research, think tanks, advocacy organizations, and educational materials.

## Think Tanks and Research Organizations

### Center-Left

**Brookings Institution**

- Website: [brookings.edu](https://www.brookings.edu)
- Focus: Evidence-based policy analysis
- Key programs: Economic Studies, Hutchins Center on Fiscal and Monetary Policy

**Center on Budget and Policy Priorities**

- Website: [cbpp.org](https://www.cbpp.org)
- Focus: Budget and tax policy affecting low-income
- Strengths: Detailed budget analysis, state policy

**Economic Policy Institute**

- Website: [epi.org](https://www.epi.org)
- Focus: Economic policy affecting working people
- Strengths: Labor market, fiscal multipliers

**Roosevelt Institute**

- Website: [rooseveltinstitute.org](https://www.rooseveltinstitute.org)
- Focus: Progressive economic policy
- Strengths: Fiscal policy, inequality

### Center-Right

**American Enterprise Institute**

- Website: [aei.org](https://www.aei.org)
- Focus: Free enterprise policy
- Key programs: Economic Policy Studies

**Hoover Institution**

- Website: [hoover.org](https://www.hoover.org)
- Focus: Free markets, limited government
- Strengths: Economic modeling, historical analysis

**Manhattan Institute**

- Website: [manhattan-institute.org](https://www.manhattan-institute.org)
- Focus: Economic freedom
- Strengths: Urban policy, fiscal analysis

### Nonpartisan/Bipartisan

**Committee for a Responsible Federal Budget**

- Website: [crfb.org](https://www.crfb.org)
- Focus: Fiscal sustainability
- Strengths: Budget scoring, deficit tracking

**Concord Coalition**

- Website: [concordcoalition.org](https://www.concordcoalition.org)
- Focus: Generational equity
- Strengths: Public education, long-term analysis

**Peter G. Peterson Foundation**

- Website: [pgpf.org](https://www.pgpf.org)
- Focus: Fiscal sustainability
- Strengths: Public engagement, data visualization

**Urban Institute**

- Website: [urban.org](https://www.urban.org)
- Focus: Social and economic policy
- Strengths: Tax Policy Center, microsimulation

**Tax Policy Center** (Joint Brookings/Urban)

- Website: [taxpolicycenter.org](https://www.taxpolicycenter.org)
- Focus: Tax policy analysis
- Strengths: Revenue estimates, distributional analysis

**Bipartisan Policy Center**

- Website: [bipartisanpolicy.org](https://www.bipartisanpolicy.org)
- Focus: Bipartisan solutions
- Strengths: Debt ceiling analysis, entitlement reform

**National Academy of Social Insurance**

- Website: [nasi.org](https://www.nasi.org)
- Focus: Social insurance programs
- Strengths: Social Security, Medicare analysis
