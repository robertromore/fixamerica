|--------|-----------|----------|

| Increase enforcement staff | Budget request | Year 1 |
| Trade advisory committee reform | Administrative | Month 1-6 |
| Export controls expansion | Commerce authority | Ongoing |

| Bill | Purpose | Prospects |
|------|---------|-----------|

## Implementation Challenges

### Political Obstacles

| Challenge | Mitigation |
|-----------|------------|
| Corporate lobbying | Coalition building, public pressure |
| Free trade ideology | Evidence-based argument |
| WTO constraints | Reform or alternative |
| Inflation concerns | Targeted approach, phase-in |
| Budget constraints | Revenue-neutral design |

### Technical Challenges

| Challenge | Approach |
|-----------|----------|
| Supply chain complexity | Systematic mapping |
| Enforcement capacity | Staff expansion, technology |
| Coordination across agencies | Interagency structure |
| Private sector cooperation | Incentives, mandates |
| International cooperation | Coalition building |

### Economic Challenges

| Challenge | Mitigation |
|-----------|------------|
| Higher consumer prices | Targeted support for low-income |
| Transition costs | Worker adjustment |
| Retaliation risk | Allied coordination |
| Competitiveness | Productivity investment |

## Success Metrics

### Worker Outcomes

| Metric | Current | Year 5 Target | Year 10 Target |
|--------|---------|---------------|----------------|
| Manufacturing employment | 12.9M | 13.5M | 14M+ |
| TAA re-employment rate | 74% | 85% | 90% |
| Wage replacement (re-employed) | 85% | 95% | 100% |
| Worker adjustment participation | ~100K/year | 500K/year | Universal |

### Trade Balance

| Metric | Current | Year 5 Target | Year 10 Target |
|--------|---------|---------------|----------------|
| Goods deficit | $1.1T | $900B | $750B |
| China goods deficit | $280B | $200B | $150B |
| Manufacturing trade balance | Large deficit | Reduce 25% | Reduce 40% |

### Supply Chain Security

| Metric | Current | Year 5 Target | Year 10 Target |
|--------|---------|---------------|----------------|
| Semiconductor domestic production | <15% | 25% | 35% |
| Pharmaceutical API domestic | <20% | 35% | 50% |
| Critical mineral processing | <10% | 25% | 40% |
| Single-source dependencies | Many | Reduce 50% | Eliminate critical |

## Contingency Planning

### If Major Legislation Stalls

| Alternative | Mechanism |
|-------------|-----------|
| Executive action | Existing authorities |
| Administrative reform | Agency action |
| State-level action | Model legislation |
| Incremental approach | Smaller bills |

### If Economic Disruption

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Inflation spike | Phase in more slowly |
| Retaliation | Allied coordination |
| Supply shortage | Emergency measures |
| Recession | Accelerate investment |

### If Political Environment Changes

| Scenario | Adaptation |
|----------|------------|
| Unified Democratic government | Accelerate comprehensive reform |
| Divided government | Focus on bipartisan elements |
| Republican unified government | Worker-focused elements may survive |
