# Corporate Governance Reform: Resources

## Overview

This document provides resources for understanding corporate governance problems and solutions, including academic research, advocacy organizations, legal materials, and media sources.

## Academic Research

### Foundational Works

| Work | Author(s) | Key Contribution |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| *The Modern Corporation and Private Property* (1932) | Berle & Means | Identified separation of ownership and control |

| *The Political Theory of the Company* (2017) | David Ciepley | Corporate as political entity |

### Key Court Decisions

| Case | Year | Holding |
|------|------|---------|
| *Dodge v. Ford Motor Co.* | 1919 | Corporations exist for shareholder benefit |
| *Revlon v. MacAndrews & Forbes* | 1986 | Must maximize shareholder value in sale |

| *eBay Domestic Holdings v. Newmark* | 2010 | Cannot subordinate shareholder interests |
