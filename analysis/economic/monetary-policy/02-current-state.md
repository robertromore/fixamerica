| Issue | Reality |

### Current Policy Stance

**Interest Rates (2024)**

| Rate | Level | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| Federal funds target | 5.25-5.50% | Primary policy rate |
| Discount rate | 5.50% | Emergency lending to banks |
| Interest on reserves | 5.40% | Paid to banks on reserves |

**Balance Sheet**

| Category | Amount | Change from 2008 |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| Total assets | ~$7.5 trillion | +730% |
| Treasury securities | ~$4.5 trillion | Massive increase |
| Mortgage-backed securities | ~$2.5 trillion | New since crisis |
| Runoff rate | $95 billion/month | Quantitative tightening |

### Recent Actions

**2020-2024 Timeline**

| Period | Action | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| March 2020 | Emergency rate cuts to 0% | COVID crisis |
| 2020-2021 | Massive QE ($120B/month purchases) | Support economy |
| 2021-2022 | Inflation surged to 9%+ | Supply chains, stimulus |
| 2022-2023 | Fastest rate hikes in 40 years | Fight inflation |
| 2024 | Rates held high | Inflation still elevated |

## Impact Assessment

### On Households

**Winners and Losers from Rate Hikes**

| Group | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| Savers | Higher returns on savings |
| Bond holders | Losses on existing bonds |
| Mortgage seekers | Much higher costs |
| Existing mortgage holders | Protected by fixed rates |
| Stock investors | Negative initially |
| Workers | Risk of job losses |

**Wealth Effects from QE (2020-2022)**

| Asset class | Impact |
|-------------|--------|
| Stocks | +40% |
| Home prices | +40% |
| Bonds | Initially up, then crashed |
| Savings accounts | Near-zero returns |

### On Businesses

**Credit Conditions**

| Business type | Access |
|---------------|--------|
| Large corporations | Good (capital markets) |
| Mid-size companies | Tightening |
| Small businesses | Significantly harder |
| Startups | Very difficult |

**Investment Effects**

| Sector | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Housing construction | Collapsed |
| Commercial real estate | Distressed |
| Tech investment | Sharply reduced |
| Manufacturing | Mixed |

### On Financial System

**Bank Stress**

| Issue | Status |
|-------|--------|
| Unrealized losses | ~$500+ billion |
| Regional bank failures | 3 major (2023) |
| Commercial real estate exposure | Significant concern |
| Deposit flight risk | Elevated |

## Current Debates

### Inflation vs. Employment

**Hawks vs. Doves**

| Position | Argument |
|----------|----------|
| Hawks | Inflation must reach 2% before cuts |
| Doves | Labor market cooling warrants caution |
| Moderates | Data-dependent, gradual approach |

**Key Uncertainties**

| Factor | Question |
|--------|----------|
| Neutral rate | Has it risen permanently? |
| Inflation expectations | Are they anchored? |
| Labor market | Softening or resilient? |
| Productivity | AI-driven surge coming? |

### Fed Independence Under Pressure

**Political Pressures**

| Source | Pressure |
|--------|----------|
| Trump administration | Called for lower rates |
| Progressive Democrats | Criticized tight policy |
| Wall Street | Lobbies for looser policy |
| Banks | Want higher rates on reserves |

### Balance Sheet Policy

**QT Debates**

| Issue | Positions |
|-------|-----------|
| Pace of runoff | Too fast vs. too slow |
| Terminal size | How small should it get? |
| MBS sales | Whether to actively sell |
| Reserve adequacy | When to stop |

### Current Practices

**FOMC Communication**

| Tool | Frequency |
|------|-----------|

|------|-------|
