## Academic Research

### Key Papers

**Fed Governance and Independence**

| Paper | Author(s) | Year | Key finding |
|-------|-----------|------|-------------|
| "Central Bank Independence and Inflation" | Alesina and Summers | 1993 | Independence correlated with low inflation |
| "The Politics of Central Bank Independence" | Goodhart | 1994 | Independence is political choice |
| "Central Bank Accountability and Independence" | Eijffinger and Hoeberichts | 1998 | Accountability trade-offs |
| "The Fed and Inequality" | Montecino and Epstein | 2015 | QE distributional effects |

**Monetary Policy Effects**

| Paper | Author(s) | Year | Key finding |
|-------|-----------|------|-------------|
| "The Distributional Effects of Monetary Policy" | Coibion et al. | 2017 | Unequal effects |
| "Monetary Policy and Racial Inequality" | Bartscher et al. | 2021 | Black workers more affected |
| "QE and Inequality" | Montecino and Epstein | 2017 | Wealth concentration |
| "Central Banking and Financial Stability" | Bernanke | 2020 | Financial stability role |
