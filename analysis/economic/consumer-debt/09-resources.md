# Consumer Debt: Resources

## Overview

This document provides resources for understanding consumer debt issues, including data sources, research organizations, advocacy groups, legal resources, and practical tools for those dealing with debt.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Data available**: Substantial government and private data on debt
2. **Research robust**: Strong academic and think tank literature
3. **Advocacy organized**: Multiple organizations working on reform
4. **Legal help exists**: Free and low-cost legal resources available
5. **Tools accessible**: Many free debt management tools
6. **Information asymmetry**: Key problem reform addresses
