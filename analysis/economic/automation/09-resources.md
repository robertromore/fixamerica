## Research and Reports

### Essential Academic Research

**On Job Displacement**

- Frey, Carl Benedikt & Osborne, Michael. "The Future of Employment" (2013)
- Acemoglu, Daron & Restrepo, Pascual. "Robots and Jobs" (2020)
- Autor, David. "Why Are There Still So Many Jobs?" (2015)
- Bessen, James. "AI and Jobs: The Role of Demand" (2019)

**On Technological Transitions**

- Autor, David & Salomons, Anna. "Is Automation Labor-Displacing?" (2018)
- Mokyr, Joel, Vickers, Chris & Ziebarth, Nicolas. "The History of Technological Anxiety" (2015)
- Gordon, Robert. *The Rise and Fall of American Growth* (2016)

**On Solutions**

- Van Parijs, Philippe & Vanderborght, Yannick. *Basic Income: A Radical Proposal* (2017)
- Oren Cass. *The Once and Future Worker* (2018)
- Daron Acemoglu. "Remaking the Post-COVID World" (2021)

**On Geographic Impacts**

- Autor, David, Dorn, David & Hanson, Gordon. "The China Shock" (2016)
- Moretti, Enrico. *The New Geography of Jobs* (2013)
- Andes, Scott & Muro, Mark. "Don't Blame the Robots" (2015)
