### Key Statutes

| Statute | Citation | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Sherman Act | 15 U.S.C. §§ 1-7 | Prohibits restraints of trade, monopolization |
| Clayton Act | 15 U.S.C. §§ 12-27 | Prohibits anticompetitive mergers, practices |
| FTC Act | 15 U.S.C. §§ 41-58 | Creates FTC, prohibits unfair competition |
| Hart-Scott-Rodino Act | 15 U.S.C. § 18a | Pre-merger notification |
| Robinson-Patman Act | 15 U.S.C. §§ 13-13b | Price discrimination |
