# Taxation: Actions

## Overview

This document provides specific, actionable steps for citizens, advocates, and officials to advance tax fairness. Actions are organized by audience and level of commitment, from simple steps anyone can take to intensive engagement for dedicated advocates.

## For All Citizens

### Learn and Understand

| Action | Time | Impact |
|--------|------|--------|
| Calculate your effective tax rate | 30 min | Personal understanding |
| Read ProPublica's "Secret IRS Files" | 1 hour | See how wealthy pay less |
| Understand the 2025 TCJA expirations | 1 hour | Know what's at stake |
| Compare your rate to billionaires' | 15 min | Motivate advocacy |

**How to Calculate Your Effective Rate**:

1. Find your total income (Form 1040, line 9)
2. Find your total tax (Form 1040, line 24)
3. Add payroll taxes (6.2% + 1.45% = 7.65% of wages)
4. Divide (tax + payroll) by total income
5. Compare to billionaires' average: 3-8%

### Speak Up

| Action | Frequency | How |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Contact representatives | Monthly on key votes | Phone calls, emails |
| Write letters to editor | 2-3 per year | Local newspapers |
| Post on social media | Weekly | Share articles, facts |
| Discuss with friends/family | Ongoing | Normalize conversation |

**Key Messages**:

- "Billionaires should pay at least the same rate as nurses"
- "Close loopholes, don't cut services"
- "Fund the IRS to catch wealthy tax cheats"
- "The 2025 tax debate affects everyone"

### Vote Informed

| Action | When | Resources |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Research candidate positions | Before elections | Campaign websites, voting guides |
| Ask candidates about tax policy | Town halls, forums | Specific questions |
| Support reform candidates | Primaries and general | Donations, volunteering |
| Track representative votes | Ongoing | GovTrack, congressional websites |

**Questions for Candidates**:

1. "Will you vote to increase taxes on income over $1 million?"
2. "Do you support closing the carried interest loophole?"
3. "Will you protect IRS enforcement funding?"
4. "Do you support taxing capital gains at death?"
5. "What's your position on corporate tax reform?"

## For Advocates and Professionals

### Policy Advocacy

| Action | Target | Approach |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Develop state legislation | State legislators | Draft bills, find sponsors |
| Provide expert testimony | Committees | Prepare written and oral testimony |
| Write policy briefs | Officials, media | Clear, evidence-based analysis |
| Participate in regulatory process | Treasury, IRS | Comment on proposed rules |

**Testimony Tips**:

1. Lead with personal story or compelling data
2. Connect to shared values
3. Be specific about what you want
4. Anticipate and address objections
5. Provide written backup for the record
