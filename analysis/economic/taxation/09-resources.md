## Legal Research

### Case Law

| Case | Year | Significance |
|------|------|--------------|
| *Pollock v. Farmers' Loan* | 1895 | Income tax unconstitutional |
| *Brushaber v. Union Pacific* | 1916 | 16th Amendment upheld |
| *Commissioner v. Glenshaw Glass* | 1955 | Definition of income |
| *Eisner v. Macomber* | 1920 | Realization requirement |

### Potential Challenges

| Issue | Key Precedents |
|-------|----------------|
| Wealth tax constitutionality | *Pollock*, 16th Amendment |
| Mark-to-market | *Eisner v. Macomber* (but see *Cottage Savings*) |
| State tax limitations | Commerce Clause cases |
