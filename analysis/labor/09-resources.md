# Labor: Resources

## Overview

This section compiles key resources for understanding labor policy, tracking data, finding organizations, and supporting advocacy. Resources span academic research, policy reports, data sources, legal materials, and tools for action across the full spectrum of labor issues.

## Legal Resources

### Key Laws

| Law | Citation | Content |
|-----|----------|---------|
| **National Labor Relations Act** | 29 U.S.C. § 151-169 | Collective bargaining rights |
| **Fair Labor Standards Act** | 29 U.S.C. § 201-219 | Minimum wage, overtime |
| **Occupational Safety and Health Act** | 29 U.S.C. § 651-678 | Workplace safety |
| **Family and Medical Leave Act** | 29 U.S.C. § 2601-2654 | Unpaid leave |
| **Employee Retirement Income Security Act** | 29 U.S.C. § 1001-1461 | Pension protection |
| **Title VII Civil Rights Act** | 42 U.S.C. § 2000e | Employment discrimination |

### Key Cases

| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|
| **NLRB v. Jones & Laughlin Steel** | 301 U.S. 1 (1937) | NLRA constitutional |
| **NLRB v. Mackay Radio** | 304 U.S. 333 (1938) | Permanent replacements allowed |
| **Lechmere v. NLRB** | 502 U.S. 527 (1992) | Limited union access |
| **Janus v. AFSCME** | 585 U.S. 878 (2018) | Public sector agency fees unconstitutional |
| **Epic Systems v. Lewis** | 584 U.S. 497 (2018) | Arbitration agreements upheld |
