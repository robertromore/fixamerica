## Reporting and Investigation Tools

### How to Report a Hazard

**Options**:

1. **Online**: osha.gov/workers/file-complaint
2. **Phone**: 1-800-321-OSHA
3. **In person**: Local OSHA area office
4. **Mail/fax**: Written complaint to area office

**What to Include**:

- Employer name and address
- Hazard description
- Location of hazard
- Number of workers exposed
- How long hazard has existed
- Whether you've complained to employer

### Fatality/Severe Injury Reporting

**Employer Requirements**:

- Report fatality within 8 hours
- Report amputation, loss of eye, hospitalization within 24 hours
- Online: osha.gov/severeinjury
- Phone: 1-800-321-OSHA
