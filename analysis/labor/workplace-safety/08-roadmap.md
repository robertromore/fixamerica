# Workplace Safety: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Transforming American workplace safety requires a phased approach that builds political momentum, addresses immediate crises, and creates lasting institutional change. This roadmap sequences reforms based on political feasibility, implementation capacity, and impact on worker safety.

## Success Indicators

### Short-Term (Years 1-2)

- Heat standard finalized
- 500+ new inspectors hired
- Whistleblower improvements enacted
- State plan benchmarks established
- First criminal prosecution for worker death

### Medium-Term (Years 3-5)

- 10% reduction in fatalities
- Ergonomics standard in place
- Safety committees operational
- Public employee coverage expanded
- Routine criminal enforcement

### Long-Term (Years 7-10)

- 50% reduction in fatalities
- U.S. approaches European rates
- Self-sustaining enforcement system
- Worker voice institutionalized
- Political consensus for safety
