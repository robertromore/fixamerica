### Academic Research

**Key Scholars**

| Scholar | Affiliation | Key Contributions |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| David Card | UC Berkeley | Card-Krueger studies |
| Alan Krueger (deceased) | Princeton | Card-Krueger studies |
| Arindrajit Dube | UMass Amherst | Border discontinuity studies |
| Michael Reich | UC Berkeley | Local minimum wage research |
| Sylvia Allegretto | UC Berkeley | Living wage research |
| David Neumark | UC Irvine | Employment effects (skeptical) |
| William Wascher | Federal Reserve | Employment effects (skeptical) |

**Key Academic Papers**

| Paper | Authors | Year | Finding |
|-------|---------|------|---------|
| "Minimum Wages and Employment" | Card & Krueger | 1994 | No job loss in NJ fast food |
| "Myth and Measurement" | Card & Krueger | 1995 | Book-length treatment |
| "Minimum Wage Effects Across State Borders" | Dube, Lester, Reich | 2010 | Minimal employment effects |
| "The Effect of Minimum Wages on Low-Wage Jobs" | Cengiz et al. | 2019 | No job loss detectable |
| "The Employment Effect of the Minimum Wage" | Neumark & Wascher | 2007 | Some negative effects claimed |
