- Bureau of Health Workforce
- National Health Service Corps
- Teaching Health Center Graduate Medical Education
- Area Health Education Centers

**Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)**
- Graduate Medical Education payment policy
- Telehealth policy
- Medicare physician payment
- Website: https://www.cms.gov/

**Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)**
- Healthcare workforce research
- Quality improvement resources
- Primary care research
- Website: https://www.ahrq.gov/

## Key Reports and Studies

### Workforce Projections

**AAMC Physician Workforce Projections**
- Association of American Medical Colleges
- Biennial reports on physician supply and demand
- Specialty and geographic breakdowns
- Most recent: "The Complexities of Physician Supply and Demand: Projections From 2019 to 2034"

**National Center for Health Workforce Analysis Reports**
- HRSA projections for various professions
- State-level workforce data
- Shortage area analysis
- Website: https://bhw.hrsa.gov/data-research

**AACN Nursing Workforce Reports**
- Annual faculty vacancy surveys
- Enrollment and graduation data
- Pipeline analysis

### Burnout and Well-Being

**Medscape National Physician Burnout Reports**
- Annual survey of physician burnout
- Specialty-specific data
- Trend analysis
- Website: https://www.medscape.com/

**National Academy of Medicine Action Collaborative on Clinician Well-Being**
- Multi-stakeholder initiative
- Resources and tools
- Research compendium
- Website: https://nam.edu/clinicianwellbeing/

**"Taking Action Against Clinician Burnout: A Systems Approach to Professional Well-Being" (2019)**
- National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine
- Comprehensive burnout analysis
- Systems-level recommendations

### Scope of Practice

**Naylor and Kurtzman, "The Role of Nurse Practitioners in Reinventing Primary Care"**
- *Health Affairs*, 2010
- Evidence on NP primary care quality

**Cochrane Review: Substitution of Doctors by Nurses in Primary Care**
- Systematic review of evidence
- Finds comparable outcomes

**American Medical Association Scope of Practice Data Series**
- Position papers on various professions
- Physician perspective on scope issues

### Graduate Medical Education

**Medicare Payment Advisory Commission (MedPAC) Reports on GME**
- Annual analysis of GME financing
- Reform recommendations
- Chapter in annual Report to Congress

**Council on Graduate Medical Education (COGME) Reports**
- Advisory body to HHS and Congress
- Workforce recommendations
- Training program analysis
- Website: https://www.hrsa.gov/advisory-committees/graduate-medical-edu

**IOM Report: "Graduate Medical Education That Meets the Nation's Health Needs" (2014)**
- Landmark GME reform recommendations
- Financing and accountability proposals

## Data Sources

### Provider Supply Data

**HRSA Data Warehouse**
- HPSA designations
- NHSC placement data
- Training program data
- Website: https://data.hrsa.gov/

**AMA Physician Masterfile**
- Comprehensive physician data
- Specialty and location information
- Accessed through AAMC and researchers

**National Provider Identifier (NPI) Registry**
- CMS provider database
- All healthcare providers
- Website: https://npiregistry.cms.hhs.gov/

### Healthcare Facility Data

**American Hospital Association Annual Survey**
- Hospital workforce data
- GME program information
- Hospital characteristics

**HRSA Uniform Data System**
- FQHC workforce and patient data
- Annual community health center statistics

### Labor Market Data

**Bureau of Labor Statistics Occupational Outlook**
- Employment projections
- Wage data by profession
- Website: https://www.bls.gov/ooh/

**AAMC Physician Compensation Survey**
- Specialty-specific compensation
- Regional variation
- Practice setting differences
