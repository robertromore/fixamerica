# Insurance Markets: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Government Agencies

- **Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)**: [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Exchange regulation, essential health benefits
- **Department of Labor (DOL)**: [dol.gov](https://www.dol.gov) - ERISA plan regulation
- **Treasury/IRS**: [irs.gov](https://www.irs.gov) - Tax treatment of insurance
- **Federal Trade Commission (FTC)**: [ftc.gov](https://www.ftc.gov) - Healthcare antitrust
- **Department of Justice Antitrust Division**: [justice.gov/atr](https://www.justice.gov/atr)
- **State Insurance Departments**: State-level regulation

### Research Organizations

- **Kaiser Family Foundation**: [kff.org](https://www.kff.org) - Comprehensive insurance market data
- **Commonwealth Fund**: [commonwealthfund.org](https://www.commonwealthfund.org) - Coverage and quality research
- **Urban Institute**: [urban.org](https://www.urban.org) - Policy simulation and analysis
- **RAND Corporation**: [rand.org](https://www.rand.org) - Insurance market research
- **National Academy for State Health Policy**: [nashp.org](https://www.nashp.org) - State policy analysis
- **Georgetown University Health Policy Institute**: [chir.georgetown.edu](https://chir.georgetown.edu)

### Advocacy Organizations

- **Families USA**: [familiesusa.org](https://www.familiesusa.org) - Consumer advocacy
- **Community Catalyst**: [communitycatalyst.org](https://www.communitycatalyst.org) - State-level organizing
- **United States of Care**: [unitedstatesofcare.org](https://www.unitedstatesofcare.org) - Bipartisan coverage expansion
- **Center for American Progress**: [americanprogress.org](https://www.americanprogress.org) - Progressive policy
- **National Conference of State Legislatures**: [ncsl.org](https://www.ncsl.org) - State policy resources

### Industry Organizations

- **AHIP (America's Health Insurance Plans)**: [ahip.org](https://www.ahip.org) - Insurance industry
- **Blue Cross Blue Shield Association**: [bcbs.com](https://www.bcbs.com) - BCBS plans
- **National Association of Insurance Commissioners**: [naic.org](https://www.naic.org) - State regulators

## Essential Reading

### Reports and Analysis

- Kaiser Family Foundation. "Employer Health Benefits Survey." Annual. [kff.org](https://www.kff.org) - Authoritative employer coverage data
- CMS. "Marketplace Open Enrollment Reports." [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Exchange enrollment data
- Commonwealth Fund. "Biennial Health Insurance Survey." - Underinsurance and affordability
- Urban Institute. "Health Insurance Policy Simulation Model (HIPSM)." - Coverage projections
- ASPE. "Trends in Health Insurance Coverage." [aspe.hhs.gov](https://aspe.hhs.gov)

### Academic Literature

- Oberlander, Jonathan. "The Politics of Health Reform." *NEJM* 362 (2010): 2461-2464. - ACA political analysis
- Gruber, Jonathan. "The Tax Exclusion for Employer-Sponsored Health Insurance." *NBER* Working Paper 15766 (2010).
- Dafny, Leemore. "Are Health Insurance Markets Competitive?" *American Economic Review* 100, no. 4 (2010): 1399-1431.
- Cutler, David M., and Richard J. Zeckhauser. "The Anatomy of Health Insurance." *Handbook of Health Economics* 1 (2000): 563-643.
- Hall, Mark A. "The Geography of Health Insurance Regulation." *Health Affairs* 19, no. 2 (2000): 173-184.

### Books

- Oberlander, Jonathan. *The Political Life of Medicare*. University of Chicago Press, 2003.
- Starr, Paul. *Remedy and Reaction: The Peculiar American Struggle over Health Care Reform*. Yale University Press, 2011.
- Hacker, Jacob S. *The Road to Nowhere: The Genesis of President Clinton's Plan for Health Security*. Princeton University Press, 1997.
- McDonough, John E. *Inside National Health Reform*. University of California Press, 2011.

## Data Sources

### Market Data

- **CMS Marketplace Data**: [cms.gov/marketplace](https://www.cms.gov/marketplace) - Enrollment, premiums, issuers
- **Medical Expenditure Panel Survey (MEPS)**: [meps.ahrq.gov](https://meps.ahrq.gov) - Coverage and costs
- **Current Population Survey (CPS)**: [census.gov](https://www.census.gov) - Uninsured estimates
- **American Community Survey (ACS)**: [census.gov](https://www.census.gov) - Coverage by geography
- **KFF State Health Facts**: [kff.org/statedata](https://www.kff.org/statedata/)

### Premium Data

- **Healthcare.gov Window Shopping**: [healthcare.gov](https://www.healthcare.gov) - Marketplace premiums
- **State exchange websites**: State-specific premium data
- **SERFF Filing Access**: [serff.com](https://www.serff.com) - Rate filings (state-specific)

### Quality Data

- **NCQA Health Plan Ratings**: [ncqa.org](https://www.ncqa.org) - Plan quality ratings
- **QHP Quality Ratings**: [healthcare.gov](https://www.healthcare.gov) - Marketplace plan ratings
- **Consumer complaints**: State insurance department data
