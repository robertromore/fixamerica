# Mental Health Integration: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Government Agencies

- **SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration)**: [samhsa.gov](https://www.samhsa.gov) - Federal mental health and substance abuse agency
- **NIMH (National Institute of Mental Health)**: [nimh.nih.gov](https://www.nimh.nih.gov) - Mental health research
- **CMS (Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services)**: [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Payment policy
- **DOL (Department of Labor)**: [dol.gov](https://www.dol.gov) - ERISA parity enforcement
- **HHS Office of Civil Rights**: Parity enforcement coordination
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**: [988lifeline.org](https://988lifeline.org)

### Advocacy Organizations

- **NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness)**: [nami.org](https://www.nami.org) - Largest grassroots mental health organization
- **Mental Health America**: [mhanational.org](https://www.mhanational.org) - Advocacy and screening
- **The Kennedy Forum**: [thekennedyforum.org](https://www.thekennedyforum.org) - Parity enforcement advocacy
- **National Council for Mental Wellbeing**: [thenationalcouncil.org](https://www.thenationalcouncil.org) - Provider organization
- **NASMHPD (National Association of State Mental Health Program Directors)**: [nasmhpd.org](https://www.nasmhpd.org)
- **SAMHSA's National Helpline**: 1-800-662-4357

### Professional Organizations

- **American Psychiatric Association (APA)**: [psychiatry.org](https://www.psychiatry.org)

### Research Organizations

- **RAND Corporation**: [rand.org](https://www.rand.org) - Mental health research
- **Mathematica**: [mathematica.org](https://www.mathematica.org) - Policy evaluation
- **RTI International**: [rti.org](https://www.rti.org) - Health services research
- **AIMS Center (University of Washington)**: [aims.uw.edu](https://aims.uw.edu) - Collaborative Care research and training

## Essential Reading

### Reports and Analysis

- SAMHSA. "National Survey on Drug Use and Health (NSDUH)." Annual. - Treatment gap data
- Mental Health America. "State of Mental Health in America." Annual.
- NAMI. "State Mental Health Legislation Report." Annual.
- The Kennedy Forum. "Don't Deny Me" reports on parity compliance.
- Milliman. "Addiction and Mental Health vs. Physical Health" comparative analyses.

### Academic Literature

- Unutzer, Jurgen, et al. "Collaborative Care Management of Late-Life Depression in the Primary Care Setting: A Randomized Controlled Trial." *JAMA* 288, no. 22 (2002): 2836-2845. - Foundational CoCM evidence
- Wang, Philip S., et al. "Failure and Delay in Initial Treatment Contact After First Onset of Mental Disorders in the National Comorbidity Survey Replication." *Archives of General Psychiatry* 62, no. 6 (2005): 603-613. - Treatment delay data
- Archer, Jenna, et al. "Collaborative Care for Depression and Anxiety Problems." *Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews* 10 (2012). - Meta-analysis of integration
- Barry, Colleen L., et al. "Explaining the Mental Health and Medical Care Expenditure Gap." *Medical Care Research and Review* 69, no. 5 (2012): 560-575. - Parity research

### Books

- Frank, Richard G., and Sherry A. Glied. *Better But Not Well: Mental Health Policy in the United States Since 1950*. Johns Hopkins University Press, 2006.
- Grob, Gerald N. *The Mad Among Us: A History of the Care of America's Mentally Ill*. Free Press, 1994.
- Whitaker, Robert. *Anatomy of an Epidemic*. Crown, 2010. (Controversial perspective on medication)
