## For Individuals and Families

### Before Care: Price Shopping

**Get Price Estimates**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| Request written estimates | Ask provider for procedure costs |
| Check insurance cost estimator | Most insurers offer online tools |
| Use price transparency sites | Healthcare Bluebook, FAIR Health, Turquoise Health |
| Compare facilities | Outpatient vs. hospital, in-network options |
| Ask about alternatives | Lower-cost procedures, generic drugs |

**Choose Lower-Cost Settings**

| Condition | Higher Cost | Lower Cost Alternative |
|-----------|-------------|------------------------|
| Minor illness | Emergency room | Urgent care |
| Imaging | Hospital | Freestanding center |
| Lab tests | Hospital | Independent lab |
| Infusions | Hospital outpatient | Physician office |
| Minor surgery | Hospital | Ambulatory surgery center |

### During Care: Avoid Unnecessary Costs

**Question Recommendations**

| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Is this test necessary? | Avoid overuse |
| What are alternatives? | Find lower-cost options |
| Is there a generic? | Reduce drug costs |
| Can I wait? | Elective procedures can be planned |
| Is this in-network? | Avoid surprise bills |

**Use Choosing Wisely Resources**

The ABIM Foundation's Choosing Wisely initiative identifies tests and procedures that may be unnecessary:

- Review specialty-specific recommendations
- Ask "Do I really need this test?"
- Discuss overuse with your physician

### After Care: Bill Review

**Review Bills Carefully**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| Request itemized bill | Get line-by-line breakdown |
| Compare to estimate | Flag discrepancies |
| Check for errors | Duplicate charges, wrong codes |
| Review EOB | Ensure insurance processed correctly |
| Appeal denials | Insurers often reverse on appeal |

**Negotiate and Appeal**

| Strategy | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| Ask for cash discount | Uninsured: 30-50% reduction common |
| Request payment plan | Interest-free options |
| Apply for financial assistance | Nonprofit hospitals must offer |
| Dispute incorrect charges | Billing errors are common |
| Use patient advocates | Many hospitals have ombudsmen |

### Drug Cost Strategies

**Find Lower Prices**

| Strategy | Savings Potential |
|----------|-------------------|
| Request generics | 80-90% savings |
| Use GoodRx/RxSaver | Compare pharmacy prices |
| Mail-order pharmacy | Lower prices, convenience |
| Manufacturer coupons | Check drug company sites |
| Patient assistance programs | Free drugs for qualifying patients |
| 340B pharmacies | Discounts at qualifying facilities |
| Pill splitting | Some medications can be split (ask pharmacist) |
