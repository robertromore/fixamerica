# Rural Healthcare: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Government Agencies

- **HRSA Office of Rural Health Policy**: [hrsa.gov/rural-health](https://www.hrsa.gov/rural-health) - Federal rural health programs
- **USDA Rural Development**: [rd.usda.gov](https://www.rd.usda.gov) - Rural infrastructure funding
- **CMS Rural Health**: [cms.gov/center/special-topic/rural-health](https://www.cms.gov/center/special-topic/rural-health) - Medicare/Medicaid policy
- **FCC Rural Broadband**: [fcc.gov/rural-health](https://www.fcc.gov) - Telehealth and broadband
- **State Offices of Rural Health (SORH)**: In every state - Local coordination

### Research Organizations

- **Rural Health Information Hub**: [ruralhealthinfo.org](https://www.ruralhealthinfo.org) - Comprehensive rural health resource
- **RUPRI (Rural Policy Research Institute)**: [rupri.org](https://www.rupri.org) - Rural policy analysis
- **North Carolina Rural Health Research Program**: [ruralhealthresearch.org](https://www.shepscenter.unc.edu/programs-projects/rural-health/) - Closure tracking, research
- **WWAMI Rural Health Research Center**: [depts.washington.edu/uwrhrc](https://depts.washington.edu/uwrhrc/) - Western rural research
- **Southwest Rural Health Research Center**: [srhrc.tamhsc.edu](https://srhrc.tamhsc.edu) - Southern rural research

### Advocacy Organizations

- **National Rural Health Association**: [ruralhealth.us](https://www.ruralhealth.us) - Primary rural advocacy
- **National Organization of State Offices of Rural Health**: [nosorh.org](https://www.nosorh.org) - State coordination
- **National Association of Rural Health Clinics**: [narhc.org](https://www.narhc.org) - RHC advocacy
- **National Rural Hospital Association**: [nrharural.org](https://www.nrharural.org) - Hospital advocacy
- **American Farm Bureau**: [fb.org](https://www.fb.org) - Agricultural rural advocacy
- **Save Rural Hospitals**: [saveruralhospitals.org](https://www.saveruralhospitals.org) - Closure prevention advocacy

### Professional Organizations

- **American Academy of Family Physicians (Rural Section)**: [aafp.org](https://www.aafp.org) - Rural family medicine
- **RTT Collaborative**: [rfractionorg](https://rfractionorg.wpengine.com) - Rural training tracks
- **National AHEC Organization**: [nationalahec.org](https://www.nationalahec.org) - Rural workforce training

- HRSA. "Health Professional Shortage Areas." [data.hrsa.gov](https://data.hrsa.gov) - Shortage designation data
- MedPAC. "Rural Healthcare Provider Payment Chapters." Annual Reports. [medpac.gov](https://www.medpac.gov)
- GAO. Rural Hospital Reports. Various. [gao.gov](https://www.gao.gov)

- MacKinney, A. Clinton, et al. "Access to Rural Health Care." *Journal of Rural Health* 30, no. 1 (2014): 1-7.
- Ricketts, Thomas C. "The Changing Nature of Rural Health Care." *Annual Review of Public Health* 21 (2000): 639-657.

### Books

## Data Sources

### Hospital Data

- **Medicare Cost Reports**: [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Hospital financial data
- **AHA Annual Survey**: [aha.org](https://www.aha.org) - Hospital characteristics
- **NC Closure Tracking**: [shepscenter.unc.edu](https://www.shepscenter.unc.edu/programs-projects/rural-health/) - Real-time closure data
- **CMS Provider of Services**: [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Facility characteristics

### Workforce Data

- **Area Health Resource File (AHRF)**: [data.hrsa.gov](https://data.hrsa.gov) - County-level health data
- **HRSA HPSA Data**: [data.hrsa.gov](https://data.hrsa.gov) - Shortage area designations
- **AAMC Physician Workforce Data**: [aamc.org](https://www.aamc.org) - Physician supply
- **ANA Nursing Workforce**: [nursingworld.org](https://www.nursingworld.org) - Nursing data

### Community Data

- **USDA Rural-Urban Continuum Codes**: [ers.usda.gov](https://www.ers.usda.gov) - Rural definitions
- **Census Geographic Data**: [census.gov](https://www.census.gov) - Population, demographics
- **FCC Broadband Maps**: [broadbandmap.fcc.gov](https://broadbandmap.fcc.gov) - Connectivity data
- **County Health Rankings**: [countyhealthrankings.org](https://www.countyhealthrankings.org) - Health outcomes by county

## Legal Resources

### Statutes

- Social Security Act, Title XVIII (Medicare)
- Medicare Improvements for Patients and Providers Act (rural provisions)
- Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021 (REH program)
- Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (broadband)

### Regulations

- 42 C.F.R. Part 485 (Critical Access Hospitals)
- 42 C.F.R. Part 491 (Rural Health Clinics)
- CMS telehealth regulations

### CMS Guidance

- Critical Access Hospital Manual
- Rural Health Clinic Manual
- Medicare Benefit Policy Manual (telehealth)
