# Resources for Maternal Health

## Essential Data Sources

### Government Data

**CDC National Center for Health Statistics:**
- Maternal Mortality Rates (annual reports)
- National Vital Statistics System
- Pregnancy Mortality Surveillance System
- Website: cdc.gov/nchs

**CDC Division of Reproductive Health:**
- Pregnancy Mortality Surveillance System
- State-based maternal mortality data
- HEAR HER campaign resources
- Website: cdc.gov/reproductivehealth

**Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA):**
- Maternal and Child Health Bureau data
- Title V program data
- Health workforce data
- Website: mchb.hrsa.gov

**CMS Medicaid Data:**
- Medicaid birth coverage data
- State Medicaid program information
- Postpartum coverage status
- Website: medicaid.gov

**Census Bureau:**
- Demographic data for analysis
- Insurance coverage surveys
- American Community Survey
- Website: census.gov

### Research Databases

**PubMed/MEDLINE:**
- Maternal health research
- Clinical studies
- Systematic reviews
- Website: pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov

**Cochrane Library:**
- Evidence reviews on maternal health interventions
- Pregnancy and childbirth reviews
- Website: cochranelibrary.com

**AHRQ Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project (HCUP):**
- Hospital utilization data

- State-level analysis
- Website: hcup-us.ahrq.gov

### Landmark Reports

- Recommendations for improvement

- Preventability and contributing factors

- March of Dimes
- County-level access analysis
- State rankings

**Birth Settings in America (2020)**
- National Academies of Sciences
- Analysis of birth settings and outcomes
- Policy recommendations

**Optimizing Postpartum Care (2018)**
- ACOG Committee Opinion 736
- Comprehensive postpartum care model
- Implementation guidance
