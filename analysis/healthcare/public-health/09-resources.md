# Public Health: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Government Agencies

- **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)**: [cdc.gov](https://www.cdc.gov) - Federal disease control
- **National Institutes of Health (NIH)**: [nih.gov](https://www.nih.gov) - Biomedical research
- **Food and Drug Administration (FDA)**: [fda.gov](https://www.fda.gov) - Food, drug, device safety
- **Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA)**: [hrsa.gov](https://www.hrsa.gov) - Health resources
- **Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)**: [samhsa.gov](https://www.samhsa.gov)
- **Administration for Strategic Preparedness and Response (ASPR)**: [phe.gov](https://www.phe.gov) - Emergency preparedness
- **Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)**: [epa.gov](https://www.epa.gov) - Environmental health

### Professional Organizations

- **American Public Health Association (APHA)**: [apha.org](https://www.apha.org) - Primary professional organization
- **National Association of County and City Health Officials (NACCHO)**: [naccho.org](https://www.naccho.org) - Local health officials
- **Association of State and Territorial Health Officials (ASTHO)**: [astho.org](https://www.astho.org) - State health officials
- **Council of State and Territorial Epidemiologists (CSTE)**: [cste.org](https://www.cste.org) - Epidemiologists
- **Society for Public Health Education (SOPHE)**: [sophe.org](https://www.sophe.org) - Health education
- **National Association of Local Boards of Health (NALBOH)**: [nalboh.org](https://www.nalboh.org) - Health boards

### Research and Advocacy Organizations

- **Trust for America's Health (TFAH)**: [tfah.org](https://www.tfah.org) - Public health advocacy
- **de Beaumont Foundation**: [debeaumont.org](https://www.debeaumont.org) - Public health workforce
- **Robert Wood Johnson Foundation**: [rwjf.org](https://www.rwjf.org) - Health philanthropy
- **Commonwealth Fund**: [commonwealthfund.org](https://www.commonwealthfund.org) - Health system research
- **Kaiser Family Foundation**: [kff.org](https://www.kff.org) - Health policy analysis

### Academic Institutions

- **Association of Schools and Programs of Public Health (ASPPH)**: [aspph.org](https://www.aspph.org)
- **Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health**: [jhsph.edu](https://www.jhsph.edu)
- **Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health**: [hsph.harvard.edu](https://www.hsph.harvard.edu)
- **Columbia Mailman School of Public Health**: [mailman.columbia.edu](https://www.mailman.columbia.edu)
- **CDC Foundation**: [cdcfoundation.org](https://www.cdcfoundation.org)

## Essential Reading

### Reports and Analysis

- Trust for America's Health. "The Impact of Chronic Underfunding on America's Public Health System." Annual.
- de Beaumont Foundation. "Public Health Workforce Interests and Needs Survey." Periodic.
- National Academies. "The Future of Public Health." (1988) and "For the Public's Health" series (2011-2012).
- TFAH. "Ready or Not: Protecting the Public's Health from Diseases, Disasters, and Bioterrorism." Annual.
- CDC. "Public Health Surveillance: Preparing for the Future." Various reports.

### Academic Literature

- Institute of Medicine. *The Future of Public Health*. National Academies Press, 1988. - Foundational critique
- Gostin, Lawrence O. *Public Health Law: Power, Duty, Restraint*. 3rd ed. University of California Press, 2016. - Comprehensive legal analysis
- Frieden, Thomas R. "A Framework for Public Health Action: The Health Impact Pyramid." *American Journal of Public Health* 100, no. 4 (2010): 590-595.
- DeSalvo, Karen B., et al. "Public Health 3.0: A Call to Action for Public Health to Meet the Challenges of the 21st Century." *Preventing Chronic Disease* 14 (2017): E78.
- Mays, Glen P., and Sharla A. Smith. "Evidence Links Increases in Public Health Spending to Declines in Preventable Deaths." *Health Affairs* 30, no. 8 (2011): 1585-1593.

### Books

- Fee, Elizabeth. *Disease and Discovery: A History of the Johns Hopkins School of Hygiene and Public Health*. Johns Hopkins University Press, 1987.
- Mullan, Fitzhugh. *Plagues and Politics: The Story of the United States Public Health Service*. Basic Books, 1989.
- Garrett, Laurie. *Betrayal of Trust: The Collapse of Global Public Health*. Hyperion, 2000.
- Barry, John M. *The Great Influenza*. Viking, 2004. - 1918 pandemic history
