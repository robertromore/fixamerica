# Medicare: Resources

## Key Organizations

### Government Agencies

- **Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)**: [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Program administration
- **Medicare Payment Advisory Commission (MedPAC)**: [medpac.gov](https://www.medpac.gov) - Congressional advisory body
- **Congressional Budget Office (CBO)**: [cbo.gov](https://www.cbo.gov) - Fiscal analysis
- **Government Accountability Office (GAO)**: [gao.gov](https://www.gao.gov) - Program oversight
- **HHS Office of Inspector General (OIG)**: [oig.hhs.gov](https://oig.hhs.gov) - Fraud and abuse investigation

### Research Organizations

- **Kaiser Family Foundation**: [kff.org/medicare](https://www.kff.org/medicare/) - Comprehensive Medicare analysis
- **Commonwealth Fund**: [commonwealthfund.org](https://www.commonwealthfund.org) - Health system research
- **Urban Institute**: [urban.org/policy-centers/health-policy-center](https://www.urban.org/policy-centers/health-policy-center) - Policy analysis
- **Brookings Institution**: [brookings.edu/topic/health-care-policy](https://www.brookings.edu/topic/health-care-policy/) - Policy research
- **Center on Budget and Policy Priorities**: [cbpp.org/topics/health](https://www.cbpp.org/topics/health) - Progressive policy analysis

### Advocacy Organizations

- **AARP**: [aarp.org](https://www.aarp.org) - Largest beneficiary organization
- **Medicare Rights Center**: [medicarerights.org](https://www.medicarerights.org) - Beneficiary advocacy
- **National Committee to Preserve Social Security and Medicare**: [ncpssm.org](https://www.ncpssm.org) - Program protection
- **Physicians for a National Health Program**: [pnhp.org](https://pnhp.org) - Single-payer advocacy
- **Center for Medicare Advocacy**: [medicareadvocacy.org](https://www.medicareadvocacy.org) - Legal advocacy

### Industry Organizations

- **America's Health Insurance Plans (AHIP)**: [ahip.org](https://www.ahip.org) - Insurance industry
- **Better Medicare Alliance**: [bettermedicarealliance.org](https://www.bettermedicarealliance.org) - MA advocacy
- **Pharmaceutical Research and Manufacturers of America (PhRMA)**: [phrma.org](https://www.phrma.org) - Pharmaceutical industry
- **American Hospital Association (AHA)**: [aha.org](https://www.aha.org) - Hospital industry
- **American Medical Association (AMA)**: [ama-assn.org](https://www.ama-assn.org) - Physician organization

## Essential Reading

### Reports and Analysis

- MedPAC. "Report to the Congress: Medicare Payment Policy." Annual. [medpac.gov](https://www.medpac.gov) - Authoritative payment analysis
- Kaiser Family Foundation. "An Overview of Medicare." Annual update. [kff.org](https://www.kff.org) - Comprehensive primer
- CBO. "The 2023 Long-Term Budget Outlook." [cbo.gov](https://www.cbo.gov) - Fiscal projections
- Medicare Trustees. "Annual Report of the Boards of Trustees." [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Trust Fund status
- GAO. "Medicare: Actions Needed to Better Manage Fraud Risks." Various reports. [gao.gov](https://www.gao.gov)

### Academic Literature

- Oberlander, Jonathan. *The Political Life of Medicare*. University of Chicago Press, 2003. - Definitive political history
- Marmor, Theodore. *The Politics of Medicare*. 2nd ed. Aldine Transaction, 2000. - Classic political analysis
- Aaron, Henry J., and Jeanne M. Lambrew. *Reforming Medicare: Options, Tradeoffs, and Opportunities*. Brookings Institution Press, 2008.
- Hacker, Jacob S. "The Historical Logic of National Health Insurance." *Studies in American Political Development* 12 (1998): 57-130.
- Brown, Lawrence D., and Michael S. Sparer. "Poor Program's Progress: The Unanticipated Politics of Medicaid Policy." *Health Affairs* 22, no. 1 (2003): 31-44.

### Policy Proposals

- Physicians for a National Health Program. "Medicare for All Act Summary." [pnhp.org](https://pnhp.org)
- CAP. "Medicare Extra for All." [americanprogress.org](https://www.americanprogress.org)
- Urban Institute. "Medicare Eligibility at Age 60." [urban.org](https://www.urban.org)
- Commonwealth Fund. "Options for Reducing Medicare Spending." [commonwealthfund.org](https://www.commonwealthfund.org)

## Data Sources

### Program Data

- **CMS Medicare Data**: [data.cms.gov](https://data.cms.gov) - Claims, enrollment, spending
- **Medicare Compare**: [medicare.gov/care-compare](https://www.medicare.gov/care-compare/) - Provider quality
- **CMS Geographic Variation**: [cms.gov/Research-Statistics-Data-and-Systems](https://www.cms.gov/Research-Statistics-Data-and-Systems) - Regional data

### Survey Data

- **Medicare Current Beneficiary Survey (MCBS)**: [cms.gov/Research-Statistics-Data-and-Systems/Research/MCBS](https://www.cms.gov/Research-Statistics-Data-and-Systems/Research/MCBS) - Beneficiary survey
- **Health and Retirement Study (HRS)**: [hrs.isr.umich.edu](https://hrs.isr.umich.edu) - Longitudinal health/economic data
- **KFF Medicare Tracking Poll**: [kff.org](https://www.kff.org) - Public opinion

### Financial Data

- **Medicare Trustees Reports**: [cms.gov](https://www.cms.gov) - Trust Fund projections
- **CBO Medicare Baseline**: [cbo.gov](https://www.cbo.gov) - Budget projections
- **MedPAC Payment Data**: [medpac.gov](https://www.medpac.gov) - Provider payment analysis

## Legal Resources

### Statutes

- 42 U.S.C. Chapter 7, Subchapter XVIII (Medicare Act)
- 42 U.S.C. Chapter 7, Subchapter XI (General Provisions)
- Inflation Reduction Act of 2022, Title XI (Drug Pricing)
- Medicare Modernization Act of 2003 (Part D)
- MACRA (Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015)

### Regulations

- 42 C.F.R. Parts 400-699 (Medicare regulations)
- CMS Manual System [cms.gov/Regulations-and-Guidance/Guidance/Manuals](https://www.cms.gov/Regulations-and-Guidance/Guidance/Manuals)

### Key Cases

- *Heckler v. Ringer*, 466 U.S. 602 (1984) - Medicare coverage determinations
- *Shalala v. Illinois Council on Long Term Care*, 529 U.S. 1 (2000) - Administrative exhaustion
- *Azar v. Allina Health Services*, 139 S. Ct. 1804 (2019) - Notice requirements
