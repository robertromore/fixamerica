# Digital Divide: Actions for Citizens

## Overview

Citizens can advance digital equity through personal actions, community organizing, civic engagement, and support for reform organizations.

## Personal Actions

### Get Connected

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Find affordable programs | ACP (if renewed), Lifeline, provider programs |
| Compare options | Check all available providers |
| Check eligibility | Income-based programs |
| Apply for assistance | EveryoneOn, BenefitsCheckUp |

### Help Others Connect

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|

| Issue | Report To |
|-------|-----------|

## Quick-Start Actions

### This Week

1. Check your internet speed (M-Lab, Speedtest)
2. Look up ACP eligibility or alternatives
3. Find your state broadband office
4. Sign up for digital equity organization newsletters

### This Month

1. Contact your representatives about broadband funding
2. Attend a local government meeting
3. Volunteer for digital literacy training
4. Submit a broadband map challenge if needed

### This Year

1. Engage in BEAD state planning process
2. Support local broadband initiatives
3. Help organize community digital equity efforts
4. Advocate for permanent affordability programs
