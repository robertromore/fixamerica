# Digital Divide: History

## Overview

The digital divide has evolved from dial-up access disparities to complex gaps in broadband infrastructure, affordability, and digital skills, with federal policy oscillating between market-based and interventionist approaches.

## Timeline

### Early Internet Era (1990s)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 1991 | High Performance Computing Act |
| 1993 | National Information Infrastructure initiative |
| 1995 | NTIA "Falling Through the Net" reports begin |
| 1996 | Telecommunications Act |
| 1996 | E-Rate program established |
| 1997 | Universal Service Fund reform |

### Dial-Up to Broadband (2000-2008)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2000 | First broadband deployments |
| 2002 | Cable classified as information service |
| 2004 | FCC four principles of internet openness |
| 2005 | DSL reclassified as information service |
| 2007 | Rural broadband loans begin |
| 2008 | Broadband Data Improvement Act |

### Stimulus Era (2009-2016)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2009 | ARRA $7.2 billion for broadband |
| 2009 | BTOP and BIP programs |
| 2010 | National Broadband Plan released |
| 2011 | Connect America Fund replaces USF |
| 2015 | FCC raises broadband standard to 25/3 |
| 2015 | Lifeline expanded to broadband |

### Deregulation and Rollback (2017-2020)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2017 | Net neutrality repealed |
| 2017 | Restoring Internet Freedom Order |
| 2018 | CAF II auctions |
| 2019 | 5G deployment begins |
| 2020 | Rural Digital Opportunity Fund auction |

### Pandemic and Infrastructure (2020-Present)

| Year | Development |
|------|-------------|
| 2020 | COVID reveals digital divide severity |
| 2020 | Emergency Connectivity Fund |
| 2021 | Emergency Broadband Benefit |
| 2021 | Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act ($65B) |
| 2022 | Affordable Connectivity Program |
| 2022 | BEAD Program established |
| 2024 | ACP expires (funding lapse) |

## Program Successes and Failures

### Successes

| Program | Achievement |
|---------|-------------|
| E-Rate | 99% schools connected |
| CAF I | Rural telephone upgraded |
| BTOP | Infrastructure deployed |
| ACP | 23 million enrolled |

### Failures

| Program | Problem |
|---------|---------|
| RDOF | Deployment delays |
| Lifeline | Low enrollment |
| CAF II | Slow buildout |
| Connect America Fund | Insufficient for universal |

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked

| Lesson | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| Direct infrastructure investment | BTOP, E-Rate success |
| Affordability subsidies | ACP enrollment |
| Schools/libraries focus | E-Rate connectivity |
| State flexibility | Successful state programs |

### What Failed

| Lesson | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| Market-only approach | Persistent gaps |
| Insufficient funding | CAF underfunded |
| Provider accountability | RDOF failures |
| Short-term programs | ACP expiration |
