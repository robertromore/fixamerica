# Algorithmic Accountability: History

## Overview

Automated decision-making has evolved from simple rule-based systems to complex machine learning models, with civil rights and accountability frameworks failing to keep pace with technological change.

## Timeline

|------|-------------|

## Lessons from History

### Successes

| Success | Lesson |
|---------|--------|

### Failures

| Failure | Lesson |

| Vendor accountability | Chain of responsibility unclear |
