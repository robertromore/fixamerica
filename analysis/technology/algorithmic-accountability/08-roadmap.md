# Algorithmic Accountability: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing algorithmic accountability requires phased action across legislative, regulatory, enforcement, and capacity-building dimensions.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)

### Legislative Action

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Federal framework legislation | Year 1-2 | Congress |
| State legislation advancement | Year 1 | State legislatures |
| Agency authority clarification | Year 1 | Congress |

### Agency Action

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| EEOC guidance on algorithms | Year 1 | EEOC |
| CFPB fair lending guidance | Year 1 | CFPB |
| FTC unfairness rulemaking | Year 1-2 | FTC |
| HUD algorithmic guidance | Year 1 | HUD |

### Capacity Building

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| NIST standards development | Year 1-2 | NIST |
| Agency technologist hiring | Year 1 | All agencies |
| Interagency coordination | Year 1 | OSTP |
| Research funding | Year 1 | NSF |

### State and Local

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Model legislation development | Year 1 | Advocates |
| Municipal pilots | Year 1-2 | Cities |
| State AG coordination | Year 1 | State AGs |
