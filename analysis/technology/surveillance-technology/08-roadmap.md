# Surveillance Technology: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing surveillance technology reform requires coordinated action at federal, state, and local levels, prioritizing the most dangerous technologies while building toward comprehensive oversight.

## Phase 1: Immediate Actions (Years 1-2)

### Federal Executive Action

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Facial recognition moratorium (federal agencies) | Year 1 | White House |
| Grant conditions for transparency | Year 1 | DOJ |
| Data broker purchase review | Year 1 | OMB |
| Civil rights review of surveillance | Year 1 | DOJ CRD |

### Congressional Action

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Facial recognition moratorium bill | Year 1 | Congress |
| ECPA modernization | Year 1-2 | Congress |
| Location privacy legislation | Year 2 | Congress |
| Oversight hearings | Year 1 | Judiciary |

### State and Local

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Local facial recognition bans | Year 1 | Cities |
| State moratoriums | Year 1-2 | State legislatures |
| Community oversight ordinances | Year 1 | Cities |
| Transparency requirements | Year 1 | Cities/states |

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
