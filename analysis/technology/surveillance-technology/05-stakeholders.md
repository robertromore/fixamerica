# Surveillance Technology: Stakeholders

## Overview

Surveillance technology policy involves diverse stakeholders with varying degrees of power, vulnerability, and interest in reform outcomes.

## Affected Communities

### Communities of Color

| Impact | Manifestation |
|--------|---------------|
| Disproportionate surveillance | Over-policed neighborhoods |
| Facial recognition errors | Higher misidentification rates |
| Predictive policing | Targeted for enforcement |
| Immigration enforcement | Family separation risk |
| Historical pattern | Continuity with COINTELPRO |

**Advocacy capacity**: Moderate (civil rights organizations)

### Immigrants

| Impact | Manifestation |
|--------|---------------|
| ICE surveillance | Deportation risk |
| Border biometrics | Universal collection |
| Facial recognition | Database enrollment |
| Location tracking | Movement monitoring |
| Social media screening | Visa applications |

**Advocacy capacity**: Low to moderate (immigration advocates)

### Activists and Protesters

| Impact | Manifestation |
|--------|---------------|
| Protest surveillance | Chilling effect |
| Social media monitoring | Targeting |
| Facial recognition | Identification |
| Location tracking | Movement monitoring |
| Infiltration | Undercover surveillance |

**Advocacy capacity**: Variable (activist networks)

### Criminal Justice Involved

| Impact | Manifestation |
|--------|---------------|
| Facial recognition in investigations | Wrongful accusations |
| Risk assessment | Biased predictions |
| Ankle monitoring | Pervasive surveillance |
| Probation surveillance | Extensive restrictions |
| Record perpetuation | Long-term impacts |

**Advocacy capacity**: Low (criminal justice reformers)

### General Public

| Impact | Manifestation |
|--------|---------------|
| Mass surveillance | Pervasive data collection |
| Location tracking | Movement patterns |
| License plate readers | Vehicle tracking |
| Facial recognition in public | Identity capture |
| Data broker exposure | Information accessible |

**Advocacy capacity**: Low (general apathy)
