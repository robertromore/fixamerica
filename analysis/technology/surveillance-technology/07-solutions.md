# Surveillance Technology: Solutions

## Overview

Addressing surveillance technology proliferation requires a combination of outright bans on the most dangerous technologies, strict regulation of others, transparency requirements, accountability mechanisms, and community control.

## Facial Recognition

### Ban Approach

| Context | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Government use | Ban or strict moratorium |
| Law enforcement | Prohibition |
| Public spaces | Ban |
| Schools | Ban |
| Housing | Ban |

### If Regulated (Not Recommended)

| Retention limits | Delete after use |
| Transparency | Public reporting |
