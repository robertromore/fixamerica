# Emerging Technologies: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing effective emerging technology governance requires building institutional capacity, developing adaptive frameworks, engaging stakeholders, and coordinating internationally over a sustained period.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)

### Institutional Development

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Restore OTA or equivalent | Year 1 | Congress |
| Create OSTP coordination role | Year 1 | Executive |
| Establish expert advisory bodies | Year 1 | Agencies |
| Build agency technical capacity | Year 1-2 | Agencies |

### Framework Development

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Emerging tech governance principles | Year 1 | OSTP |
| Cross-agency coordination mechanism | Year 1 | OSTP |
| Risk assessment frameworks | Year 1-2 | Agencies |
| International engagement strategy | Year 1 | State |

### Priority Technologies
