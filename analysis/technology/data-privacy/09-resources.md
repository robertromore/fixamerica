## Academic Research

### Key Books

**Foundational:**
- Zuboff, Shoshana. *The Age of Surveillance Capitalism* (2019).
- Pasquale, Frank. *The Black Box Society* (2015).
- Solove, Daniel. *Understanding Privacy* (2008).
- Nissenbaum, Helen. *Privacy in Context* (2010).

**Contemporary:**
- Cohen, Julie. *Between Truth and Power* (2019).
- Citron, Danielle Keats. *The Fight for Privacy* (2022).
- Waldman, Ari. *Privacy as Trust* (2018).

### Academic Papers

**Privacy law:**
- Hartzog, Woodrow. "The Case Against Idealizing Control." *European Data Protection Law Review* (2018).
- Solove, Daniel. "A Taxonomy of Privacy." *University of Pennsylvania Law Review* (2006).
- Richards, Neil. "The Dangers of Surveillance." *Harvard Law Review* (2013).

**Data markets:**
- Acquisti, Alessandro et al. "The Economics of Privacy." *Journal of Economic Literature* (2016).
- Varian, Hal. "Economic Aspects of Personal Privacy." In *Privacy and Self-Regulation in the Information Age* (1996).

**Consent:**
- Nissenbaum, Helen. "A Contextual Approach to Privacy Online." *Daedalus* (2011).
- Obar, Jonathan and Anne Oeldorf-Hirsch. "The Biggest Lie on the Internet." *Information, Communication & Society* (2020).
