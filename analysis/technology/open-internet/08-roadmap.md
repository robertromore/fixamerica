# Open Internet: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing comprehensive open internet protections requires sustained effort across regulatory, legislative, and market dimensions. This roadmap outlines a phased approach to securing a durable open internet.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)

### Regulatory Actions

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Restore Title II classification | Year 1 | FCC |
| Implement net neutrality rules | Year 1 | FCC |
| Establish enforcement procedures | Year 1 | FCC |
| Interconnection oversight | Year 1-2 | FCC |
| Transparency requirements | Year 1 | FCC |

### Legislative Groundwork

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Draft comprehensive legislation | Year 1 | Congress |
| Build bipartisan coalition | Year 1-2 | Advocates |
| State legislation support | Year 1-2 | States |
| Municipal broadband preemption | Year 1-2 | Congress |

### Competition Policy

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| Block anticompetitive mergers | Ongoing | DOJ/FTC |
| Pole attachment reform | Year 1-2 | FCC |
| Franchise reform guidance | Year 1-2 | FCC |

### Infrastructure Investment

|--------|----------|------|
| Broadband grant programs | Year 1 | NTIA |
