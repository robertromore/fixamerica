# Technology Workforce: Actions for Citizens

Citizens can advance technology workforce goals through education, career choices, civic engagement, and support for organizations working on workforce issues.

## Quick-Start Actions

### This Week

1. Research tech career pathways
2. Sign up for advocacy organization updates
3. Find your representatives' positions on tech workforce
4. Identify local tech community groups

### This Month

1. Contact your representative about immigration/workforce
2. Join a professional or advocacy organization
3. Start a skill development program
4. Attend a community meetup or event

### This Year

1. Develop new tech skills
2. Mentor someone entering tech
3. Engage in sustained advocacy
4. Support diversity organizations
