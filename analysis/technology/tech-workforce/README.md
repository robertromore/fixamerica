# Technology Workforce: Overview

The United States faces significant technology workforce challenges including skills shortages in critical fields, lack of diversity, geographic concentration, educational pipeline gaps, and worker protection issues. Addressing these challenges requires comprehensive reform of education and training systems, immigration policy, workplace protections, and geographic distribution strategies.
