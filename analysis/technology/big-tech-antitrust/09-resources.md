- Wu, Tim. *The Curse of Bigness* (2018).
- Philippon, Thomas. *The Great Reversal* (2019).
- Lynn, Barry. *Liberty from All Masters* (2020).

**Platform Economics:**
- Parker, Geoffrey et al. *Platform Revolution* (2016).
- Evans, David and Richard Schmalensee. *Matchmakers* (2016).
