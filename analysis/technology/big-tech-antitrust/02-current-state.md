# Big Tech Antitrust: Current State

## Overview

The major technology platforms have consolidated unprecedented economic and social power. Despite years of investigation and litigation, the competitive landscape remains dominated by a handful of companies. Recent enforcement actions show promise but face uncertain remedies, while legislative efforts have stalled.

## International Comparison

### EU Digital Markets Act

**Status:** In force March 2024

**Key provisions:**
- Gatekeeper designation
- Interoperability requirements
- Self-preferencing prohibition
- Data use restrictions
- App store alternatives required

**Designated gatekeepers:** Alphabet, Amazon, Apple, ByteDance, Meta, Microsoft
