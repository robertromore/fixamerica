# Technology Policy: Individual Actions

## Overview

Citizens can influence technology policy through personal practices, civic engagement, advocacy, and professional involvement. This document outlines concrete steps individuals can take to protect themselves and push for systemic reform.

## Personal Digital Practices

### Protect Your Privacy

| Action | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Audit permissions** | Review app permissions regularly |
| **Use privacy tools** | Browser extensions, VPNs, encrypted messaging |
| **Limit data sharing** | Opt out where possible |
| **Read policies** | Check privacy settings on services |
| **Delete unused accounts** | Reduce data exposure |

### Secure Your Digital Life

| Action | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Strong passwords** | Use password manager |
| **Two-factor authentication** | Enable on all accounts |
| **Software updates** | Keep systems current |
| **Backup data** | Regular, encrypted backups |
| **Phishing awareness** | Verify before clicking |

### Mindful Technology Use

| Action | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Screen time limits** | Set boundaries |
| **Notification management** | Disable non-essential |
| **Algorithm awareness** | Understand recommendation systems |
| **Source verification** | Check before sharing |
| **Digital wellbeing** | Model healthy use |

## Advocacy Actions

### Effective Communication

| Method | Tips |
|--------|------|
| **Phone calls** | Most effective for Congress |
| **Personal stories** | How tech issues affect you |
| **Specific asks** | Name bills, specific reforms |
| **Local connection** | How it affects your community |
| **Follow-up** | Sustained engagement |

### Sample Messages

**On Privacy:**

> "I urge you to support comprehensive federal privacy legislation with a private right of action. Americans deserve control over their personal data, and current patchwork protections are inadequate."

**On Antitrust:**

> "I support strong antitrust enforcement against dominant technology platforms. These companies have too much power over our economy and our information. Please support the DOJ and FTC's ongoing cases."

**On AI:**

> "As artificial intelligence becomes more prevalent, we need strong safety guardrails. Please support legislation requiring testing, transparency, and accountability for AI systems used in high-stakes decisions."

**On Broadband:**

> "Reliable, affordable internet is essential infrastructure. Please support reauthorizing the Affordable Connectivity Program and continued broadband investment in underserved communities."
