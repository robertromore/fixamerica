# Cybersecurity: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Implementing comprehensive cybersecurity reform requires sustained effort across regulatory, workforce, investment, and international dimensions. This roadmap prioritizes high-impact, achievable actions.

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|
| CIRCIA implementation | Year 1 | CISA |
| Critical infrastructure baseline | Year 1-2 | Sector agencies |
| Software liability safe harbor | Year 2 | Congress |
| Data security requirements | Year 2 | FTC |

| Action | Timeline | Lead |
|--------|----------|------|

| State/local grants | Year 1 | CISA |
| Clearance reform | Year 1 | DNI |
| Information sharing | Year 1-2 | CISA |
