# Technology Policy: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

Technology policy reform requires sustained action across multiple fronts and jurisdictions. This roadmap prioritizes feasible near-term wins while building toward systemic change. Progress depends on leveraging multiple venues—federal, state, international—while building coalitions across partisan lines.

| Sequence | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
