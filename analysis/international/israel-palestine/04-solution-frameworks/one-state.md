# One-State Solution

## Overview

One-state frameworks envision a single political entity between the Jordan River and Mediterranean Sea, with various models for governance and citizenship.

## Variants

### Binational State

*To be developed*

- Equal citizenship for all
- Consociational/power-sharing governance
- Protection for national communities
- Examples: Belgium, Lebanon, Bosnia

### Democratic Secular State

*To be developed*

- Individual rights focus
- No official ethnic/religious character
- One person, one vote
- Historical PLO position

### Israeli Annexation with Rights

*To be developed*

- Israeli sovereignty over all territory
- Full citizenship for Palestinians
- End of Palestinian national project
- Demographic implications

### Israeli Annexation without Rights

*To be developed*

- De facto current trajectory
- Permanent occupation/apartheid
- International law violations
- Unsustainability
