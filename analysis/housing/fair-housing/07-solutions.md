# Fair Housing: Solutions

## Overview of Reform Approaches

Addressing fair housing requires a comprehensive strategy across enforcement, affirmative requirements, legal doctrine, protected class expansion, and addressing new forms of discrimination. No single reform suffices; the following solutions work together to finally realize the promise of fair housing.

## Protect Disparate Impact Doctrine

### Codify Disparate Impact

**Statutory Language**

| Current | Reform |
|---------|--------|
| Judicially implied | Explicit statutory basis |
| Subject to court limitation | Congressional definition |
| *Inclusive Communities* constraints | Clarify scope |

**Key Elements to Codify**

| Element | Definition |
|---------|------------|
| Disparate impact | Policy with discriminatory effect |
| Burden of proof | Plaintiff shows impact; defendant justifies |
| Business necessity | Must be substantial, legitimate |
| Less discriminatory alternative | Defendant must consider |
