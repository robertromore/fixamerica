# Fair Housing: Stakeholders

## Who Is Harmed by Housing Discrimination

### Protected Classes Under Fair Housing Act

**Federal Protected Classes**

| Protected Class | Added | Key Issues |
|-----------------|-------|-----------|
| Race | 1968 | All forms of discrimination; most complaints |
| Color | 1968 | Intraracial discrimination; colorism |
| National Origin | 1968 | Immigrant discrimination; language access |
| Religion | 1968 | Religious community housing; discrimination |
| Sex | 1974 | Sexual harassment; gender discrimination |
| Familial Status | 1988 | Families with children under 18 |
| Disability | 1988 | Physical and mental; reasonable accommodations |

**Additional State/Local Protected Classes**

| Class | Jurisdictions |
|-------|--------------|
| Sexual orientation | 23+ states, many localities |
| Gender identity | 20+ states, many localities |
| Source of income | 21+ states, DC, many localities |
| Marital status | Many states |
| Age | Some states |
| Veteran status | Some states |
| Criminal history | Some localities |

### Communities Most Affected

**Black Americans**

| Impact Area | Data |
|-------------|------|
| Homeownership gap | 28 points below white rate |
| Testing discrimination rate | 17-21% experience discrimination |
| Segregation level | Highest of any group |
| Wealth gap | 8:1 white-to-Black ratio |
| Lending denial rate | Nearly 2x white rate |

**Hispanic/Latino Americans**

| Impact Area | Data |
|-------------|------|
| Homeownership gap | 24 points below white rate |
| Testing discrimination rate | 19% experience discrimination |
| Language barriers | Significant in housing search |
| Documentation concerns | Chilling effect even for citizens |
| Segregation | Increasing in some metros |

**Asian Americans**

| Impact Area | Data |
|-------------|------|
| Testing discrimination rate | 15%+ experience discrimination |
| Model minority myth | Masks discrimination |
| Language access | Critical for many communities |
| Intragroup variation | Varies by ethnicity |

**People with Disabilities**

| Impact Area | Data |
|-------------|------|
| Complaint share | ~55% of HUD complaints |
| Accessibility barriers | Vast majority of housing inaccessible |
| Reasonable accommodation denials | Common |
| Institutional bias | Push toward congregate settings |

**Families with Children**

| Impact Area | Data |
|-------------|------|
| Rental discrimination | Common, especially in desirable areas |
| Steering | Toward family-designated areas |
| Occupancy standards | Used to exclude |
| School access | Tied to housing discrimination |

**Voucher Holders**

| Impact Area | Data |
|-------------|------|
| Landlord acceptance rate | 30% or less where not protected |
| Utilization rate | 60-70% find housing |
| Search time | 90+ days average |
| Neighborhood quality | Concentrated in high-poverty areas |

### The Broader Community

**All Residents of Segregated Areas**

| Impact | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| Lower school quality | Property tax base affects funding |
| Reduced job access | Geographic mismatch |
| Health disparities | Environmental factors, healthcare access |
| Higher crime | Concentrated poverty effects |
| Limited mobility | Neighborhood effects trap residents |

**Integrated Communities**

Fair housing benefits integration:

| Benefit | Evidence |
|---------|----------|
| Better outcomes for minorities | Mobility program research |
| Reduced prejudice | Contact hypothesis research |
| Economic efficiency | Labor market matching |
| Democratic benefits | Cross-racial understanding |

## Who Has Power to Change Things

### Federal Government

**Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD)**

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| FHEO (Office of Fair Housing) | Investigate complaints |
| AFFH oversight | Set requirements for grantees |
| Rulemaking | Define standards |
| Research | Testing studies, data collection |
| Grants | Fund fair housing organizations |

**Key HUD Officials**

| Position | Influence |
|----------|-----------|
| Secretary | Policy direction, priorities |
| Assistant Secretary for FHEO | Direct enforcement leadership |
| General Counsel | Legal interpretation |

**Department of Justice**

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| Pattern and practice cases | Systemic discrimination litigation |
| Criminal enforcement | Rare but available |
| Amicus briefs | Influence court interpretation |
| Coordination with HUD | Referrals, joint actions |

**Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)**

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| Fair lending enforcement | Mortgage discrimination |
| Data collection | HMDA oversight |
| Rulemaking | Lending regulations |

**Federal Courts**

| Role | Influence |
|------|-----------|
| Constitutional interpretation | Define FHA scope |
| Precedent setting | Shape doctrine |
| Remedies | Order systemic relief |

### State Government

**State Civil Rights Agencies**

| Role | Authority |

| State law enforcement | May exceed federal protections |
| Testing programs | Some states conduct |
| Education | Landlord/tenant training |

**State Attorneys General**

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| Pattern and practice suits | Systemic discrimination |
| Consumer protection | Lending, insurance |
| Amicus participation | Influence federal cases |

**State Legislatures**

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| Additional protected classes | Expand coverage |

| Preemption | Can limit or enable local action |
