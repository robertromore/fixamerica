# Fair Housing: Implementation Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap sequences fair housing reforms to build momentum, address dependencies, and maximize impact. The strategy recognizes that federal policy shifts with administrations, requiring state and local action as backup and acceleration. Litigation and grassroots organizing provide constant pressure regardless of political environment.
