# Rural Housing: Resources

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive collection of resources for understanding, researching, and advocating for rural housing improvement. Resources include federal agencies, research organizations, advocacy groups, data sources, and key publications.

## Research Organizations

### Housing Assistance Council (HAC)

**Website**: [ruralhome.org](https://ruralhome.org/)

**Role**: Primary national rural housing research and advocacy organization

**Key resources**:

- **Taking Stock**: National rural housing condition reports
- **Rural Voices**: Quarterly rural housing magazine
- **Rural Research Briefs**: Topical research documents
- **Loan Fund**: Financing for rural housing development
- **Technical Assistance**: Support for rural housing organizations

**Publications**: [HAC Publications](https://ruralhome.org/publications/)

### National American Indian Housing Council (NAIHC)

**Website**: [naihc.net](https://naihc.net/)

- Native housing data and research

## Advocacy Organizations

### National Level

**MHAction (Manufactured Housing Action)**

- Website: [mhaction.org](https://mhaction.org/)
- Focus: Manufactured housing resident advocacy
- Activities: Community organizing, policy advocacy

**ROC USA (Resident Owned Communities)**

- Website: [rocusa.org](https://rocusa.org/)
- Focus: Manufactured housing community resident ownership
- Activities: Lending, technical assistance, organizing

**Prosperity Now (formerly CFED)**

- Website: [prosperitynow.org](https://prosperitynow.org/)
- Focus: Asset building, including homeownership
- Resources: I'M HOME network for manufactured housing

**National Low Income Housing Coalition (NLIHC)**

- Website: [nlihc.org](https://nlihc.org/)
- Focus: Affordable housing advocacy broadly
- Resources: Out of Reach report, Advocates Guide
