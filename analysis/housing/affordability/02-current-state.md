## Recent Developments

### Post-2020 Price Surge

**Pandemic-Era Changes**

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Remote work shift | Increased demand in suburbs and smaller metros |
| Low interest rates (2020-2022) | Fueled price appreciation |
| Supply chain disruptions | Increased construction costs 30%+ |
| Investor purchases | Rose to 28% of all home sales |
| New construction delays | Permitting backlogs extended timelines |
