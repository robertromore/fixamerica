### Key Laws

| Law | Citation | Content |
|-----|----------|---------|
| **Fair Housing Act** | 42 U.S.C. § 3601 et seq. | Housing discrimination prohibition |
| **Housing Act of 1937** | 42 U.S.C. § 1437 | Public housing |
| **Section 8 (Housing Choice Vouchers)** | 42 U.S.C. § 1437f | Rental assistance |
| **Community Reinvestment Act** | 12 U.S.C. § 2901 | Bank community obligations |
| **LIHTC** | 26 U.S.C. § 42 | Tax credit program |

| Case | Citation | Holding |
|------|----------|---------|

| **Shelley v. Kraemer** | 334 U.S. 1 (1948) | Restrictive covenants unenforceable |
| **Jones v. Mayer** | 392 U.S. 409 (1968) | Housing discrimination prohibited |
| **Texas Dept. of Housing v. Inclusive Communities** | 576 U.S. 519 (2015) | Disparate impact cognizable |
