# Public Housing and Assisted Housing: Actions

## Overview

This section provides concrete actions citizens can take to support public and assisted housing reform. Actions range from immediate individual steps to long-term collective organizing, with guidance on effectiveness and effort required.

## Individual Actions

### Educate Yourself

**Learn the Basics**

| Action | Effort | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Read *Evicted* by Matthew Desmond | Low | Foundation for understanding |
| Review NLIHC's annual Gap report | Low | Current data and analysis |
| Understand how your local PHA operates | Low | Local context |
| Learn your state's source of income protection status | Low | Know what laws apply |

**Stay Informed**

| Action | Effort | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Subscribe to NLIHC Memo to Members | Low | Weekly federal updates |
| Follow state housing coalition newsletters | Low | State-level news |
| Monitor local housing authority meetings | Moderate | Local awareness |
| Track federal budget and appropriations | Moderate | Funding decisions |

### Personal Actions

**If You Are a Landlord**

| Action | Effort | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Accept Housing Choice Vouchers | Moderate | Direct impact on families |
| Learn voucher program requirements | Low | Reduce barriers to participation |
| Provide stable, quality housing | Ongoing | Resident wellbeing |
| Advocate for better payment standards | Low | System improvement |

**If You Are a Tenant**

| Action | Effort | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Know your rights | Low | Self-protection |
| Document housing conditions | Low | Evidence for advocacy |
| Join tenant organizations | Low | Collective voice |
| Report housing code violations | Low | Enforcement |

**Financial Support**

| Action | Effort | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Donate to housing advocacy organizations | Low | Supports advocacy capacity |
| Support legal aid organizations | Low | Resident representation |
| Contribute to housing trust funds (where possible) | Low | Direct housing support |

**Support Affordable Housing Development**
