### Key Studies and Reports

#### Foundational Research

- Culhane, Dennis P., et al. "Public Service Reductions Associated with Placement of Homeless Persons with Severe Mental Illness in Supportive Housing." *Housing Policy Debate* 13.1 (2002): 107-163.

- Tsemberis, Sam, et al. "Housing First, Consumer Choice, and Harm Reduction for Homeless Individuals with a Dual Diagnosis." *American Journal of Public Health* 94.4 (2004): 651-656.

- Gubits, Daniel, et al. "Family Options Study: 3-Year Impacts of Housing and Services Interventions for Homeless Families." HUD (2016).

#### Recent Research

- Colburn, Gregg, and Clayton Page Aldern. *Homelessness Is a Housing Problem: How Structural Factors Explain U.S. Patterns.* University of California Press, 2022.

- Aurand, Andrew, et al. "The Gap: A Shortage of Affordable Homes." National Low Income Housing Coalition (annual).

- National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine. *Permanent Supportive Housing: Evaluating the Evidence for Improving Health Outcomes Among People Experiencing Chronic Homelessness.* 2018.
