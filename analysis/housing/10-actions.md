## For Individuals

### Educate Yourself

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Learn housing policy basics** | Read *The Color of Law*, *Evicted*, *Golden Gates* |
| **Understand local zoning** | Review your city's zoning code, attend planning meetings |
| **Know tenant rights** | Research your state/local protections |
| **Follow housing news** | Subscribe to Shelterforce, NLIHC newsletters |
| **Map your neighborhood** | Use PolicyMap, Opportunity Atlas |

### Personal Actions

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Support local development** | Don't oppose housing projects near you |
| **Rent your ADU/spare room** | Add to housing supply |
| **If landlord, be a good one** | Fair treatment, reasonable rents |
| **Consider housing location** | Proximity to transit, services |
| **Share housing resources** | Help others navigate system |

### Civic Engagement

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Vote in local elections** | These decide housing policy |
| **Attend planning meetings** | Show up to support housing |
| **Comment on projects** | Counter NIMBY voices |
| **Testify at hearings** | Personal stories matter |
| **Know your representatives** | Contact them on housing issues |

### Financial Actions

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Donate to housing orgs** | Fund advocacy, legal aid |
| **Invest in CDFIs** | Community development financial institutions |
| **Support community land trusts** | Donate, volunteer |
| **Consider impact investing** | Affordable housing funds |

- [ ] Identify your local elected officials
- [ ] Look up upcoming planning/council meetings

- [ ] Attend a local planning or city council meeting
- [ ] Contact an elected official about housing
- [ ] Join or donate to a housing organization
- [ ] Read one book on housing policy
- [ ] Have a conversation with neighbors about housing

### This Year

- [ ] Become active in local housing advocacy
- [ ] Testify or comment on a housing issue
- [ ] Vote in local elections on housing grounds
- [ ] Recruit others to housing cause
- [ ] Track policy outcomes and celebrate wins
