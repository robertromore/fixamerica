# Gentrification: Resources

## Overview

This document compiles key resources for understanding gentrification and displacement, including academic research, community organization guides, data tools, and funding sources. These resources support advocates, policymakers, researchers, and community members working on anti-displacement strategies.

## Academic Research

### Foundational Texts

| Author | Title | Year | Key Contribution |
|--------|-------|------|------------------|
| Ruth Glass | *London: Aspects of Change* | 1964 | Coined term "gentrification" |
| Neil Smith | *The New Urban Frontier* | 1996 | Rent gap theory |
| David Ley | *The New Middle Class and the Remaking of the Central City* | 1996 | Consumption-side explanation |
| Loretta Lees et al. | *Gentrification* | 2008 | Comprehensive overview |
| Jason Hackworth | *The Neoliberal City* | 2007 | Third-wave gentrification |
| Kathe Newman & Elvin Wyly | "The Right to Stay Put, Revisited" | 2006 | Displacement evidence |
| Matthew Desmond | *Evicted* | 2016 | Eviction and poverty |

### Key Empirical Studies

**Displacement Research**

| Study | Authors | Finding |
|-------|---------|---------|
| "The Effects of Rent Control Expansion" | Diamond, McQuade, Qian | Rent control reduces displacement 15-20% |
| "Gentrification and Displacement" | Freeman & Braconi | Lower mobility in gentrifying areas (contested) |
| "In Defense of Gentrification" | Vigdor | Minimal displacement (contested) |
| "Does Gentrification Displace Poor Households?" | Ding et al. | Significant displacement with comprehensive measures |
| "The Urban Displacement Project" | Zuk et al. | Mapped displacement risk across metros |

**Economic Effects**

| Study | Authors | Finding |
|-------|---------|---------|
| "Rent Control Economics" | Autor, Palmer, Pathak | MIT study of Cambridge decontrol effects |
| "Effects of Just Cause Eviction" | Asquith et al. | Limited negative supply effects |
| "Housing Supply and Affordability" | Mast | New housing reduces displacement pressure |
| "Community Land Trusts" | Thaden & Lowe | CLTs maintain affordability long-term |
