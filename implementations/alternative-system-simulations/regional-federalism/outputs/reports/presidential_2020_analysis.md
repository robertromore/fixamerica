## Swing State Analysis

**Closest state:** GA (margin: 0.24%)

**Votes to flip outcome:** ~21,461

**Decisive states (closest to tipping point):** GA, AZ, WI

### Key Insight

Under the Electoral College, the election outcome depends on a small number of swing states. In 2020, approximately **21,461 votes** in the decisive states could have changed the outcome.

Under Regional Federalism, every vote counts equally regardless of state, eliminating the swing state phenomenon.
